## The Marketing Discipline Pyramid (simple)

### Level 1 — Clarity
You know exactly what to post this week.

### Level 2 — Time block

### Level 3 — Templates

## Time thieves (and how to kill them)

Time thieves are not always big.
They are small and repeated.

### 1) The “scroll trap”
You open TikTok/IG to “post”, and you start scrolling.

**Fix:**
- create a rule: no scrolling until after posting
- use airplane mode while editing
- set a 10-minute timer

### 2) The “perfection trap”
You keep editing, changing captions, overthinking.

**Fix:**
- use templates
- limit editing to 10 minutes
- post “good enough”

### 3) The “no plan” trap
You don’t know what to post, so you don’t post.

**Fix:**
- use a weekly plan (video + 2 photos)
- reuse the same formats

### 4) The “I’m too busy” trap
You are busy, so marketing disappears.

**Fix:**
- time block marketing like cooking
- 30 minutes is enough

### 5) The “I’ll do it later” trap
Later becomes never.

**Fix:**
- choose a fixed weekly time slot
- protect it like an appointment

## The “discipline rules” (simple but powerful)

Use these rules to stay consistent:

1) **Never miss two weeks in a row**  
Missing one week happens. Missing two kills momentum.

2) **Create before you consume**  
Post first. Scroll later.

3) **Reuse formats**  
Same format weekly = easy.

4) **Use templates**  
No writing from scratch.

5) **One small improvement per month**  
Keep it sustainable.

## Deep dive: the biggest marketing time thieves (and fixes)

### Time thief 1: “I need better ideas”
Fix:
Use formats, not ideas.
Formats repeat.

Your formats:
- steam/sauce/crunch video
- packing proof
- portion proof
- signature close-up

Same formats, different dish.
No new ideas needed.

### Time thief 2: “I must edit like a pro”
Fix:
Simple editing is enough.
Authentic videos often perform better than polished ads.

Set a rule:
- 10 minutes editing maximum
- post it

### Time thief 3: “I don’t know what to write”
Fix:
Use micro-copy + templates.
Save 20 lines and reuse them.

Example lines:
- “Taste of home ❤️”
- “Made fresh • Clean packing ✅”
- “DM ORDER ❤️”
- “Family packs available”

### Time thief 4: “I get distracted”
Fix:
Use “posting mode” habits:
- airplane mode while editing
- timer for 10 minutes
- no scrolling before posting

Distraction is the hidden killer.
