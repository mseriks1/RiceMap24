# Marketing Coach – Month 39  
## Focus: Signature System (one dish becomes your identity + your easiest marketing)

This month you build a **Signature System**. That means one dish becomes your identity and your easiest marketing tool — because people remember one thing better than many things.

A signature system creates:
- stronger first orders (“I want to try THAT dish”)  
- easier word of mouth (“they have that amazing [dish]”)  
- easier repeat (“I want the same again”)  
- clearer brand (“that kitchen = that dish”)  

Early important term:  
CTA = Call To Action (what you want the customer to do now). Use one CTA per post/message, always the same:  
**“Menu and ordering: [link]”**

⸻

## What a signature system is (simple)
A signature system is not just “a dish you like.” It is:

1) One dish you commit to as your “anchor”  
2) A consistent way to present it (photo style + copy + bundle)  
3) A weekly marketing habit (one signature post per week)  
4) A gateway into the rest of the menu (add-ons, for 2, for later)

You make your marketing easier because you don’t have to think: the signature dish is always a safe content choice.

⸻

## Why this works (psychology)
Customers:
- feel safer ordering the “popular thing”
- remember one strong impression
- recommend one dish easier than a whole menu
- trust kitchens that have “a thing they are known for”

This is why restaurants often have a signature dish. You can do the same.

⸻

## This month’s strategy (4 steps)
1) Choose your signature dish (or confirm it)  
2) Create a signature hero set (photos + clips)  
3) Create one signature bundle (increases order value)  
4) Build the weekly signature marketing habit + track impact

⸻

## Goals this month
1) You define your signature dish clearly  
2) You create a signature hero set (10 photos + 6 clips)

⸻

- 2 photo posts per week (FB + IG)  
- 2 videos per week (TikTok + Reels + Shorts)  
- Plus: 1 signature-focused post per week (can be one of the photo posts)

Offline:
- optional, but you can place a poster that highlights the signature dish if you want

# Part A: Choose the signature dish (commit)
Your signature should be:
- memorable  
- repeatable  
- efficient for you  
- something you can deliver consistently  

**Rule:** choose one. You can change later, but you need an anchor now.

⸻

# Part B: Signature hero set (photos + clips)
Minimum hero set:
- 10 photos (different angles)
- 6 short clips (close-up, plating, packing, “what you get”)

Photo angles to include:
- full plate
- close-up texture
- portion proof
- ingredient vibe
- packed and ready

This hero set becomes your “content engine” for months.

⸻

# Part C: Signature bundle (increase order value)
Create one bundle that makes it easier to order more:
- signature dish + add-on
- signature dish “for 2”
- signature dish + cold-ready add-on

Keep it simple and clear.

⸻

# Part D: Weekly signature marketing habit
One signature post per week. Always the same structure:
- photo/video of the signature dish
- one strong sentence
- CTA

**Template:**
> This week’s signature: [dish name] 😊  
> [one appetite line]  
> **Menu and ordering:** [link]

⸻

# Weekly plan (4 weeks)

## Week 1 — Choose signature + write the story in 2 lines
### Mini lesson (clarity creates memory)
If you can describe why it’s special, customers remember it.

### Tasks
1) Choose your signature dish  
2) Write 2 lines:
- what it is
- why it’s special (flavor/texture/story)
3) Post signature once + CTA

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now your brand has an anchor.”
- “A clear signature increases first orders.”

---

## Week 2 — Create the hero set (10 photos + 6 clips)
### Mini lesson (visuals make the signature real)
A signature must look amazing and consistent.

### Tasks
1) Take 10 photos (different angles)  
2) Record 6 clips (close-up, packing, portion proof)  
3) Post signature once (use the new hero set)

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now you have premium content you can reuse for months.”
- “Strong visuals increase conversion.”

---

## Week 3 — Build the signature bundle (easy “yes”)
### Mini lesson (bundles remove friction)
When you package it, customers order more easily.

### Tasks
1) Create one signature bundle  
2) Make 2 photos that show the bundle clearly  
3) Post:
- one signature post
- one bundle post (optional, but recommended)

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now the signature becomes an order value engine.”
- “Clarity increases average order.”

---

## Week 4 — Stabilize and measure (signature as a system)
### Mini lesson (repeat builds the identity)
One post is not a signature. Repetition is.

### Tasks
1) Post signature once again  
2) Track:
- how many orders include signature?
- do people mention it?
3) Decide what you repeat next month:
- keep signature as core anchor
- refine bundle or copy

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now the signature system is alive.”
- “You made marketing easier for the future.”
