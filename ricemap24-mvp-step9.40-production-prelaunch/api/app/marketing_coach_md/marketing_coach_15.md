# Marketing Coach – Month 15  
## Focus: Pro routine + expand your content library (warm, real, and credible)

This month we focus on two things at the same time — in a tidy way:
1) You continue the pro routine (2 photo posts + 2 videos per week)  
2) You **expand your library** with new content that makes marketing even easier going forward

The goal is for you to feel like you always have something to post, without having to “come up with something” every week. You build a library that can be reused, cut, angled, and reposted.

---

## Why this focus now
After a period of more offensive local marketing (Month 13) and new customer groups (Month 14), it’s smart to strengthen the foundation again:
- more content = easier to keep the rhythm
- more variation = more reasons to order
- more real “behind the scenes” = more credibility and trust

You are not going to become an “ad machine.” You are going to build a real and tempting expression that people recognize and like.

---

## This month’s strategy (3 steps)
1) Create new content in **batches** (one session per week)  
2) Create content in **3 types**: appetite, proof, and “you/the kitchen”  
3) Reuse smart: same clip → multiple platforms → multiple angles

---

## Goals this month
1) Add at least **40 new photos** to the library (10 per week)  
2) Add at least **16 new video clips** (4 per week)  
3) Keep the routine: **2 photo posts + 2 videos per week**  
4) Do 2 offline actions (every other week) — low threshold

---

## Fixed posting routine (baseline this month)
Every week:
- 2 photo posts (FB + IG, same content)  
- 2 videos (TikTok + Reels + Shorts where you can)  
- + offline every other week (2 total)

---

## Progress in the dashboard (two colors)
- **Library growth (60%)**: new photos + new clips added weekly  
- **Routine execution (40%)**: posts/videos published + offline completed  

---

# The 3 content types (very concrete)

## A) Appetite content (makes people hungry)
- close-ups
- steam/sizzle
- plating
- “what you get” (portion)

## B) Proof content (builds safety and trust)
- packing orders
- clean kitchen
- ready-for-pickup
- consistent portions

## C) “You/the kitchen” content (human + local)
- hands cooking
- a short hello
- “today I’m cooking…”
- a quick local vibe

**Rule:** You don’t have to show your private life. You just show the *role*: cook, owner, local kitchen.

---

# Weekly plan (4 weeks)

## Week 1 — Batch session + first library expansion
### Mini lesson (batching removes stress)
If you batch, the rest of the week is easier.

### Tasks
1) Do one batch session (60–90 min):
- take 10 new photos (mix A/B/C)
- record 4 new clips (mix A/B/C)

2) Publish the weekly routine:
- 2 photo posts
- 2 videos

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Great. Now you’re building a library that saves time later.”
- “Batching protects your rhythm.”

---

## Week 2 — Variation: new angles from the same dishes
### Mini lesson (variation is angles, not new dishes)
You don’t need new dishes every week. You need new angles:
- for 2
- for lunch
- for later
- bundles/add-ons
- fresh today

### Tasks
1) Add 10 new photos + 4 new clips  
2) Post as normal (2 + 2)  
3) Offline action #1 (low threshold):
- place or refresh a poster in 1 location
- or share your menu in one local group

✅ Done confirmations:
- “You increased variety without more cooking.”
- “Small offline actions build local familiarity.”

---

## Week 3 — Upgrade quality: “one hero set”
### Mini lesson (one strong set can be reused for months)
A hero set = 5–10 great photos + 2–3 clips of your signature/best seller.

### Tasks
1) Make one hero set (signature or best seller):
- 10 photos (different angles)
- 4 clips (short, clean, appetizing)
2) Post the weekly routine (2 + 2)

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now you have premium content you can reuse for a long time.”
- “A hero set increases first orders.”

---

## Week 4 — Stabilize the system + offline action #2
### Mini lesson (systems beat motivation)
This month is about building a system you can keep.

### Tasks
1) Add the last 10 photos + 4 clips (reach the monthly goal)  
2) Post the weekly routine (2 + 2)  
3) Offline action #2 (every other week):
- place/refresh poster in 1 location
- or local share

4) Quick review:
- which content type worked best (A/B/C)?
- which clips/photos will you reuse next month?

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now your library growth is a habit.”
- “You built a system that makes marketing easier.”
