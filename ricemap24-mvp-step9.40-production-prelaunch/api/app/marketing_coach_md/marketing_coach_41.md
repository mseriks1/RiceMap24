# Marketing Coach – Month 41  
## Focus: Promotion through others (get reach and trust without doing all the posting yourself)

This month you build **distribution through other people and other channels**. Instead of only relying on your own posts, you make it easy for:
- local groups
- connectors
- small businesses
- community leaders
- coworkers and admins

…to share you. This is powerful because it gives you:
- trust transfer (people trust their community)
- reach you can’t create alone
- local legitimacy

This is not about begging. It is about making sharing easy and low effort.

Early important term:  
CTA = Call To Action (what you want the customer to do now). Use one CTA per message/post, always the same:  
**“Menu and ordering: [link]”**

⸻

## What “promotion through others” means in practice (simple)
Promotion through others is a system with three parts:

1) You identify 10–15 potential promoters  
2) You create a **share kit** (image + link + copy/paste text)  
3) You ask in a friendly, low-pressure way and follow up lightly

The goal is not many promoters. The goal is 1–3 channels that say yes and share you repeatedly.

⸻

## Why this works (trust transfer)
People don’t trust random ads like they trust:
- a friend
- a local group admin
- a community leader
- an office manager

When someone else shares you, you gain credibility instantly.

⸻

## This month’s strategy (4 steps)
1) Build the promoter list (10–15)  
2) Build a share kit (super simple)  
3) Do 5–8 promoter asks (small and polite)  
4) Track and build repeat: which channels respond?

# Part A: Promoter list (10–15)
Examples of promoters:
- local Facebook group admins  
- coworking managers  
- gym managers  
- school/PTA leaders (where relevant)  
- church/community leaders  
- small local influencers/foodies  
- office managers  
- “connector people” (know many locals)

**Rule:** choose promoters who are local and relevant.

⸻

# Part B: Share kit (simple and effective)
A share kit includes:
1) one image (menu/hero or signature dish)  
2) one link (RiceMap24 ordering link)  
3) one copy/paste text (short)

## Share kit text (copy/paste)
> Local home kitchen in [area] 😊  
> Menu and ordering via RiceMap24: [link]  
> Pickup window: [time] (write preferred time in request).  

Optional:
> Great for lunch / for 2 / real ingredients (choose one angle).

**Rule:** one message. No long story.

⸻

# Part C: The ask (low pressure templates)

## Ask template (group admin)
> Hi! I run a small local home kitchen in [area] and sell via RiceMap24.  
> Would it be okay if I share my weekly menu once in the group?  
> I can keep it short and include a clear ordering link. Thanks 😊

## Ask template (coworking/gym/local business)
> Hi! I run a small home kitchen nearby and offer weekly menus via RiceMap24.  
> Would it be okay to share a QR/menu post in your channel/board once?  
> Here’s a simple share kit if helpful: [link/image/text]

## Ask template (connector person)
> Hi! I’ve started a small home kitchen in [area].  
> If you know someone local who would enjoy this, feel free to share the menu link 😊  
> Menu and ordering: [link]

⸻

# Part D: Tracking (simple)
Track:
- who you asked
- who said yes/no
- who shared
- whether it created orders/messages

Just a note/spreadsheet is enough.

⸻

# Weekly plan (4 weeks)

## Week 1 — Build list + create share kit
### Mini lesson (distribution starts with names)
If you don’t have a list, you won’t act.

### Tasks
1) Write 10–15 promoters  
2) Create one share kit (image + link + text)  
3) Save it in a folder so you can send it fast

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now promotion through others is actionable.”
- “A share kit removes friction.”

---

## Week 2 — Outreach wave #1 (2 asks)
### Mini lesson (ask like a human)
Short and friendly is best.

### Tasks
1) Send 2 promoter asks (choose templates)  
2) Keep routine: 2 photos + 2 videos  
3) Track responses

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now you are building distribution.”
- “Tracking helps you repeat what works.”

---

## Week 3 — Outreach wave #2 (2 asks) + follow-up
### Mini lesson (follow-up is part of the system)
Many people forget. A gentle follow-up is normal.

### Tasks
1) Send 2 more asks  
2) Follow up 1 person from Week 2 (if no answer):
> Hi! Just checking in — no worries if it doesn’t fit 😊

3) Track responses and any shares

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now you’re increasing your chances without being pushy.”
- “One yes can create repeat reach.”

---

## Week 4 — Outreach wave #3 (1–2 asks) + review
### Mini lesson (find the channels that can repeat)
The goal is not many shares once. The goal is a few channels that can share you weekly.

### Tasks
1) Send 1–2 final asks  
2) Review:
- which promoter types responded best?
- which share kit text worked best?
- did any channel create orders?

3) Decide what you will keep next month:
- 2–3 promoters to nurture

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now you have a distribution system.”
- “You identified the channels that can scale you.”
