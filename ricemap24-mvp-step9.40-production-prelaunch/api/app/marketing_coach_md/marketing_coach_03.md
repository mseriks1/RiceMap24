# Marketing Coach – Month 3
## Focus: Video that builds trust and makes marketing easier

This month we focus on **video that builds trust and makes marketing easier**. You keep posting with good photos and the right copy, but now you start using video as proof of authenticity and as a way to make marketing easier going forward. The goal is to build a small video library — not to become “perfect at video” right away.

### Natural transition from Month 2
In Month 2 you got people to see you through photos and text. But when you’re new, many customers need an extra confirmation before they order: “Is this real? Does it look clean? Will I get what I think?” Video answers this faster than text.

### Why video builds trust (and makes marketing easier)
Video does three jobs at the same time:
1) **Stops the scroll** (movement catches attention)  
2) **Builds trust** (it looks real and fresh)  
3) **Creates clarity** (the customer understands portion and contents)

In practice, that means fewer people need to ask you in DM before they decide — and that makes marketing easier.

### What is a “video library” (in practice)?
A video library is just a folder of short clips you collect over time. When you have 10–20 clips, you can make new videos quickly by combining clips — without having to film all the time.

### Goals this month
1) **Video library:** at least 6 clips (bonus 12–20)  
2) **Routine:** keep photo+copy weekly  
3) **Safe start:** publishing video is optional (0–1)

### Minimum vs Bonus
**Minimum:**
- 1 photo post per week (4)
- Film 6 short clips (total for the month)
- Publish video: 0–1 (optional in Week 4)

**Bonus:**
- 2 photo posts per week
- Film 12–20 clips
- Publish 1–2 videos

### Progress in the dashboard
Two tracks:
- **Posting routine (40%)**
- **Video library (60%)**
