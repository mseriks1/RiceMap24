# Pandan Coconut Blondies (Asian Twist, Pickup-Friendly) — RiceMap24 Dessert Standard

Classic blondies with a Southeast Asian signature: **pandan + coconut**.  
Not a traditional Asian dessert—just a familiar “bar” format with an Asian aroma. Perfect for pickup and multipacks.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Blondie bars
- **Asian twist:** Pandan extract + coconut
- **Yield:** 16 bars (20×20 cm pan) or 12 larger bars
- **Active time:** ~15–20 min
- **Total time:** ~50–60 min (plus cool)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Pickup-friendly:** bars, no melting, easy to box.
- **Strong signature aroma:** pandan stands out.
- **High-margin packs:** 2/4/8-pack.
- **Batch-friendly:** bake trays.

---

## Equipment
- 20×20 cm (8×8) pan
- Parchment paper
- Mixing bowl + whisk
- Oven

---

## Ingredients

## A) Blondie base
- **170 g** unsalted butter, melted
- **200 g** brown sugar
- **2 large eggs**
- **1 tsp** vanilla
- **190 g** all-purpose flour
- **1/2 tsp** baking powder
- **1/2 tsp** salt

## B) Asian twist add-ins
- **1–2 tsp** pandan extract/paste (start with 1 tsp)
- **80 g** shredded coconut (unsweetened recommended)
- Optional: **60 g** white chocolate chips
- Optional: **1 Tbsp** coconut milk (for extra aroma)

## Finish
- Toasted coconut flakes (optional)
- Powdered sugar dust (optional)

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Prep
1. Preheat oven to **175°C**.
2. Line pan with parchment.

---

## 2) Mix batter (10 minutes)
1. Whisk melted butter + brown sugar until smooth.
2. Whisk in eggs and vanilla.
3. Whisk in pandan extract (and coconut milk if using).
4. Fold in flour, baking powder, salt.
5. Fold in shredded coconut (and white chocolate if using).

**Do not overmix**.

---

## 3) Bake (25–30 minutes)
1. Spread batter into pan.
2. Bake until edges are set and center is just set:
   - **25–30 minutes** (toothpick should have moist crumbs)

---

## 4) Cool + slice
1. Cool completely (45–60 minutes).
2. Slice into bars.
3. Optional: sprinkle toasted coconut or dust with powdered sugar.

---

## Quality Checks
- Blondies are chewy, not cakey.
- Pandan aroma is clear but not overpowering.
- Coconut evenly distributed.

## Storage

- Refrigerated: up to 5 days
- Freeze: 1 month
