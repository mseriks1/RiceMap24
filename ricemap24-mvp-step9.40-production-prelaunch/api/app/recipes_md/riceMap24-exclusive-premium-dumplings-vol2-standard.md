## How to sell “exclusive dumplings” on RiceMap24
**Recommended product types (highest success):**
1) **Cook-at-home premium frozen dumplings** (best quality control + margin)  
2) **Steamed premium tray** (travels best, looks like dim sum)

**Premium upsell system:**

# 1) Xiao Long Bao (小笼包) — Soup Dumplings (Luxury Icon)

### Why it’s “exclusive”
- Highest prestige dumpling category
- “Soup inside” is a wow factor and social media magnet
- Justifies premium pricing

### Best way to sell (recommended)
✅ **Cook-at-home frozen XLB kit** (raw dumplings frozen solid)  
Customers steam at home → best soup effect, minimal delivery risk.

### Core concept
XLB uses **aspic (gelatinized broth)** mixed into filling. When steamed, aspic melts into soup.

### Operational shortcuts (home kitchen realistic)
- Make aspic in a big batch (broth + gelatin/collagen), chill, dice.
- Use a consistent wrapper dough/wrappers (house-made is premium; store wrappers are faster).

### Packaging (critical)
- Freeze dumplings on tray (not touching) → bag with cooking card:
  - Steam from frozen 10–12 minutes (varies by size/steamer power)
- Include: **black vinegar + ginger** cup set (classic pairing)

**Upsells**
- “Extra soup” (more aspic in filling)
- “Truffle XLB” (tiny truffle oil drop in filling, or truffle soy dip)

# 2) Har Gow (虾饺) — Crystal Shrimp Dumplings (Dim Sum Luxury)

### Why it’s “exclusive”
- Translucent “crystal” wrapper looks premium instantly
- Shrimp-forward, clean, elegant
- Steaming travels well

### How to sell
✅ **Steamed dim sum tray** (10 or 20 pieces)  
✅ **Frozen cook-at-home** (steam at home; best wrapper texture)

### Premium twist ideas (still authentic-feeling)
- Add **small diced bamboo shoot** for crunch (classic)
- Add a tiny amount of **scallop** (optional) for luxury aroma

### Packaging
- Use a **rigid tray** so dumplings don’t squish.
- Add a small parchment sheet under dumplings to reduce sticking.

**Upsells**
- “Extra shrimp” (bigger pieces)
- “Chili oil + black vinegar” bundle

# 3) “Scallop Siu Mai” (烧卖) — Pork + Shrimp + Scallop Top (Luxury Dim Sum)

### Why it’s exclusive
- Shumai already feels like dim sum
- Add a **scallop top** (or tobiko) and it becomes “special”
- Steaming is delivery-stable

### How to sell
✅ Steamed tray 12 pcs  
✅ Mixed dim sum platter with har gow + shumai

### Premium look tricks
- Top each shumai with:
  - a small scallop disc, or
  - a tiny tobiko dot, or
  - a single pea + carrot dice (classic dim sum look)

**Upsells**
- “Dim sum sampler” (higher ticket)
- “Signature sauce” (ginger soy, truffle soy)

# 4) Wagyu-Style Beef Gyoza (Fusion Premium Potstickers)

### Why it’s exclusive
- Beef dumplings with a **luxury ingredient signal** (wagyu-style or premium beef blend)
- Potsticker crisp bottom is a “restaurant” cue
- Easy to brand as premium without being too complex

### Filling concept (safe + premium)
- Ground beef + scallion + ginger + soy + sesame  
- Add **shiitake** for deeper umami (premium feel)

### How to sell (best)
✅ Pan-fried (pickup with vent + re-crisp note)  
✅ Frozen cook-at-home (best crisp at home)

**Upsells**
- “Extra crisp” (pan-fry finish)
- “Truffle soy dip” add-on

---

# 5) Truffle Soy Potstickers (Signature Sauce Upgrade)

### Why it’s exclusive
- Truffle is a premium signal
- You don’t need truffle in the filling to charge premium—use a **signature dip**.

### How to implement cheaply
- Create a **Truffle Soy Dip**:
  - soy + black vinegar + tiny sugar + garlic + **micro-dose truffle oil**
- Sell as an upgrade sauce cup.

**Upsells**
- “Double dip” (classic + truffle)
- “Chili crisp + truffle” combo

### Why it’s exclusive

**Premium sauce upgrades**

- Miso dip (miso + vinegar + sesame)

## Packaging Rules (so your “exclusive” item stays exclusive)
- **Steamed dumplings:** rigid tray + keep flat  
- **Pan-fried:** vent 45–60 seconds before sealing + “re-crisp” note  
- **Frozen:** freeze on tray first, then bag; include cooking instructions card  
- **Sauce cups:** always sealed; offer “premium 60 ml” upgrade cup
