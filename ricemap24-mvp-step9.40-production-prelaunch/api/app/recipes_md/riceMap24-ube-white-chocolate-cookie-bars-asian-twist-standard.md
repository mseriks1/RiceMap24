# Ube White Chocolate Cookie Bars (Asian Twist, Pickup-Friendly) — RiceMap24 Dessert Standard

Classic cookie bars with a Filipino twist: **ube** (purple yam) + white chocolate.  
Not traditional Asian dessert—more “modern Asian bakery” fusion. Perfect for pickup (bars, no melting).

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Cookie bars
- **Asian twist:** Ube (ube jam or ube extract) + optional coconut
- **Yield:** 16 bars (20×20 cm) or 12 larger bars
- **Active time:** ~15–20 min
- **Total time:** ~45–55 min (plus cool)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Pickup-friendly:** bars pack cleanly.
- **Photogenic:** purple hue + white chocolate contrast.
- **High-margin packs:** 2/4/8-pack.
- **Batch-friendly:** bake trays.

---

## Equipment
- 20×20 cm (8×8) pan
- Parchment paper
- Mixing bowl + whisk/spatula
- Oven

---

## Ingredients

## A) Cookie bar base
- **170 g** unsalted butter, melted
- **180 g** brown sugar
- **1 large egg**
- **1 tsp** vanilla
- **190 g** all-purpose flour
- **1/2 tsp** baking powder
- **1/2 tsp** salt

## B) Ube flavor (choose ONE)
### Option 1 (best): Ube halaya (ube jam)
- **120–160 g** ube halaya

### Option 2 (easiest): Ube extract
- **1–2 tsp** ube extract + add **1 Tbsp** extra sugar (taste)

## C) Add-ins
- **120 g** white chocolate chips/chunks
- Optional: **60 g** shredded coconut (very “ube bakery” vibe)
- Optional: 1 Tbsp milk (if batter gets too thick with ube jam)

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Prep
1. Preheat oven to **175°C**.
2. Line pan with parchment.

---

## 2) Mix batter (10 minutes)
1. Whisk melted butter + brown sugar until smooth.
2. Whisk in egg and vanilla.
3. Mix in ube halaya (or ube extract).
4. Fold in flour, baking powder, salt.
5. Fold in white chocolate (and coconut if using).

**Do not overmix**—stop when no dry streaks remain.

---

## 3) Bake (22–28 minutes)
1. Spread batter into pan.
2. Bake until edges are set and center is just set:
   - **22–28 minutes**
Toothpick should have moist crumbs.

---

## 4) Cool + slice
1. Cool completely (45–60 minutes).
2. Slice into bars.

Optional finish:
- drizzle melted white chocolate
- sprinkle toasted coconut

---

## Quality Checks
- Soft-chewy center, not dry.
- Clear ube aroma and purple tint.
- White chocolate evenly distributed.

## Storage
- Room temp (airtight): 3 days
- Refrigerated: up to 5 days
- Freeze: 1 month
