# Teriyaki Chicken & Cucumber Crunch Baguette — RiceMap24 Standard (Breakfast/Lunch)

A premium, super-sellable baguette: glossy teriyaki chicken + yuzu/lime mayo + cucumber crunch + sesame.  
Fusion (not traditional), designed for pickup quality and repeat orders.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Baguette sandwich (Asian twist)
- **Cuisine vibe:** Japanese teriyaki profile
- **Yield:** 4 baguettes (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** ~20 min
- **Total time:** ~30 min
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Broad appeal:** teriyaki chicken is a universal favorite.
- **Looks premium:** glossy chicken + fresh green crunch.
- **Delivery friendly:** sauce clings; crunchy veg can be packed separately if needed.
- **Upsells:** extra chicken, extra teriyaki cup, add avocado, add nori crunch.

---

## Ingredients (4 baguettes)

## A) Chicken
- **700 g** boneless chicken thighs (juicier) or breast (lean), sliced
- Salt + pepper
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil

## B) Quick teriyaki sauce (glossy)
- **90 ml** soy sauce
- **60 ml** water
- **2 Tbsp** honey (or brown sugar)
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 tsp** grated ginger
- **1 clove** garlic, grated
- **1 tsp** cornstarch + **1 Tbsp** water (slurry)

---

## C) Cucumber crunch + greens
- **1 cucumber**, thin sticks/ribbons
- Lettuce leaves (optional but recommended as moisture barrier)

## D) Yuzu/lime mayo (premium)
- **120 ml** mayo
- **1–2 tsp** yuzu juice *(or 1 tsp yuzu marmalade)*  
  Substitute: lime juice
- **1 tsp** rice vinegar
- Pinch salt

---

## E) Baguettes + finish
- **4** baguettes (15–18 cm) or 2 long baguettes halved
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Sliced scallions (optional)
- Optional: nori strips (premium crunch)

---

## Equipment
- Skillet
- Small bowl (slurry)
- Knife + cutting board
- Mixing bowl for mayo

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Make yuzu/lime mayo (1 minute)
- Stir mayo + yuzu/lime + vinegar + pinch salt.

---

## 2) Cook chicken (8–10 minutes)
1. Heat skillet medium-high with oil.
2. Add chicken, season lightly, sear until browned and cooked through.
3. Reduce heat slightly.

---

## 3) Add teriyaki glaze (2 minutes)
1. Mix soy, water, honey, vinegar, ginger, garlic.
2. Pour into pan and simmer 1 minute.
3. Add slurry and stir until glossy and thick enough to cling.
4. Coat chicken and remove from heat.

**Quality target:** sticky glaze, not watery.

---

## 4) Build baguettes
Per baguette:
1. Split baguette.
2. Add lettuce (optional).
3. Spread 1–2 Tbsp yuzu mayo.
4. Add teriyaki chicken (160–190 g cooked).
5. Add cucumber.
6. Finish sesame + scallion + optional nori.

---

## Portioning (Per baguette)
- Chicken: **160–200 g cooked**
- Mayo: 25–30 g
- Cucumber: generous handful
- Sesame: small sprinkle

---

## Batch for 10 baguettes
- Chicken: **1.8 kg**
- Teriyaki: 2.5× sauce
- Mayo: 300 ml + citrus to taste
- Cucumbers: 3

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Wrap in parchment.
- If pickup is long: pack cucumber in a small cup to keep extra crisp.
- Offer extra teriyaki cup as upsell.

Label: “Add cucumber last (optional).”

---

## Storage
- Teriyaki chicken keeps 2 days refrigerated (reheat gently).
- Mayo 2–3 days refrigerated.

---

## Allergens
- Soy (soy sauce)
- Egg (mayo)
- Wheat (baguette)
- Gluten possible (soy sauce brand)
- Sesame if used
