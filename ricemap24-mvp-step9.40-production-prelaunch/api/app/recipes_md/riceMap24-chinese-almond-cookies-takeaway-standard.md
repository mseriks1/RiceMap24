Crisp, crumbly Chinese bakery cookies with almond aroma—super takeaway friendly and perfect for multipacks.  
**No melting, no refrigeration, long shelf life.**

## Ingredients (≈24 cookies)

### Dry
- All-purpose flour
- Almond flour (or ground almonds)
- Baking powder
- Salt

### Wet
- Unsalted butter (softened)
- Sugar
- Egg (or yolk, depending on recipe)
- Almond extract (optional, boosts aroma)

### Finish
- Whole blanched almonds (one per cookie)
- Egg wash (for shine)
- Optional: sesame seeds (some versions)

> Use the Woks of Life reference for the exact ingredient ratios.

---

## Step-by-step Method (Repeatable)

### 1) Mix dough (10 minutes)
1. Cream butter and sugar until smooth.
2. Add egg (and almond extract if using).
3. Whisk dry ingredients and add to bowl.
4. Mix until a soft cookie dough forms.

### 2) Shape (10 minutes)
1. Scoop dough into equal balls (about 25–30 g each).
2. Flatten slightly into thick discs.
3. Place on tray with space between.
4. Brush lightly with egg wash.
5. Press 1 almond on top of each cookie.

### 3) Bake (12–16 minutes)
1. Bake at **175°C** until edges are golden and centers are set.
2. Cool 10 minutes on tray, then move to rack.

- **Aroma:** almond-forward
- **Look:** almond centered, clean shape

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Cool fully before boxing (keeps crisp).
- Use parchment between layers.
- Add label: “Keep airtight for best crunch.”

---

## Storage
- Airtight at room temp: **5–7 days**
- Freeze: **1 month** (thaw at room temp)

---

## Allergens
- Wheat (flour)
- Egg
- Dairy (butter)
- Nuts (almond)
