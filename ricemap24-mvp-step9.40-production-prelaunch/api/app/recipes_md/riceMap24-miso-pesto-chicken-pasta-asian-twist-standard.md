# Miso-Pesto Chicken Pasta (Asian Twist Meal) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A familiar Western dinner (creamy pesto pasta) upgraded with a Japanese pantry twist: **white miso** folded into pesto for deeper umami.  
Not traditional Asian—this is fusion comfort food designed for home-kitchen sales and pickup/delivery.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Chicken pesto pasta (fusion)
- **Asian twist:** White miso + sesame/nori finish options
- **Yield:** 4 meal portions
- **Active time:** ~20 min
- **Total time:** ~25–30 min
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Banker-friendly:** pasta sells; familiar taste profile.
- **Fast production:** one pot pasta + one pan chicken.
- **Delivery-friendly:** sauce holds; reheats well with a splash of milk/water.
- **Upsells:** extra chicken, extra sauce cup, add broccoli, add chili crisp.

---

## Equipment
- Pot for pasta
- Skillet for chicken
- Mixing bowl (sauce)
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Ingredients (4 portions)

## A) Pasta + base
- **400 g** pasta (penne/fusilli)
- Salted pasta water

## B) Chicken
- **600 g** boneless chicken thigh (or breast), sliced
- Salt + pepper
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil

## C) Miso-pesto sauce (the identity)
- **120 g** basil pesto (store-bought OK)
- **1.5 Tbsp** white miso
- **120 ml** heavy cream *(or 150 ml milk for lighter)*
- **60 ml** pasta water (to loosen)
- **1 Tbsp** lemon juice
- **1 tsp** sesame oil (optional, tiny)
- **30–40 g** grated parmesan (optional)

## D) Optional add-ins (great for sales)
- 1 cup broccoli florets (steam with pasta last 2 minutes)
- Cherry tomatoes (fresh finish)
- Chili crisp (drizzle)
- Nori strips + sesame seeds (garnish)

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Cook pasta (10–12 min)
1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
2. Reserve **1 cup** pasta water, then drain.

Optional: add broccoli to the pasta pot in the last 2 minutes to blanch.

---

## 2) Cook chicken (8–10 min)
1. Heat skillet medium-high with oil.
2. Add chicken, season, and sear until browned and cooked through.
3. Remove from heat.

---

## 3) Make miso-pesto sauce (3 min)
1. In a bowl, whisk pesto + miso until smooth.
2. Whisk in cream (or milk) and lemon juice.
3. Add pasta water gradually until sauce is glossy and coats a spoon.

---

## 4) Combine (2–3 min)
1. Return pasta to pot on low heat.
2. Add sauce and toss.
3. Add chicken (and broccoli if using).
4. Taste and adjust:
   - Too thick → more pasta water
   - Needs brightness → more lemon
   - Needs salt → parmesan or a tiny pinch salt

---

## Quality Checks
- Sauce clings (not watery).
- Clear pesto aroma with deeper savory miso note.
- Pasta still has bite.

---

## Portioning (Per order)
- Pasta: **350–450 g**
- Chicken: **140–170 g**
- Optional sauce cup (upsell): 20–30 ml

---

## Scaling (10 orders)
Multiply by **2.5×**:
- Pasta 1.0 kg, chicken 1.5 kg, pesto 300 g, miso 3–4 Tbsp.
Cook pasta in two batches if needed.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Container A: pasta + chicken (sturdy)
- Optional cup: chili crisp drizzle
- Optional garnish cup: nori + sesame

Label: “Reheat gently. Add garnish last.”

---

## Storage & Reheat
- Refrigerate up to 3 days.
- Reheat with a splash of milk/water and stir well.

---

## Allergens
- Wheat (pasta)
- Soy (miso)
- Dairy (cream, parmesan if used)
- Nuts possible (pesto may contain pine nuts)
- Sesame (if used)
