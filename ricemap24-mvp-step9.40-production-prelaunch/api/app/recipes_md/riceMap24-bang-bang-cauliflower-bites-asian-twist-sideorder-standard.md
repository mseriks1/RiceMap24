# Asian-Style “Bang Bang” Cauliflower Bites (Fusion Side Order) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A familiar bar-style snack (crispy cauliflower bites) with a pan-Asian sauce twist: **sweet chili + sriracha + sesame** “bang bang” drizzle.  
Not a traditional Asian dish—this is fusion designed for pickup and upsell.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Crispy cauliflower bites (side order / share box)
- **Asian twist:** Sweet chili + sriracha + sesame + lime
- **Yield:** 6 side portions (or 3 share portions)
- **Active time:** ~20 min
- **Total time:** ~35–40 min
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (crisping)

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **High-margin side:** cauliflower + sauce = great margins.
- **Very photogenic:** sauced bites + sesame + scallions.
- **Pickup-friendly:** sauce can be packed separately for maximum crisp.
- **Upsells:** extra sauce cup, spicy level, add chicken version.

---

## Equipment
- Oven or air fryer (best for crisp)
- Mixing bowls
- Sheet pan + parchment
- Whisk
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Ingredients (6 side portions)

## A) Cauliflower
- **1 large head** cauliflower (about 900–1,000 g), bite-size florets

## B) Batter (for baked crisp)
- **120 g** all-purpose flour
- **1 tsp** baking powder
- **1/2 tsp** salt
- **1/2 tsp** garlic powder (optional)
- **240 ml** water (or milk for richer)
- Optional: 1 tsp paprika (color)

## C) Crunch coat (optional but recommended)
- **150 g** panko breadcrumbs (extra crunch)
- Optional: 1 Tbsp sesame seeds mixed into panko

## D) Bang Bang sauce (the identity)
- **120 ml** mayo
- **3 Tbsp** sweet chili sauce
- **1–2 Tbsp** sriracha (adjust)
- **1 tsp** rice vinegar or lime juice
- **1 tsp** sesame oil (tiny)
- Pinch salt

## E) Finish
- Sliced scallions
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Optional: lime wedges

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Prep
1. Preheat oven to **220°C** (or air fryer 200°C).
2. Line sheet pan with parchment.

---

## 2) Batter + coat (10–12 min)
1. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt (and optional spices).
2. Whisk in water until smooth (thick pancake batter).
3. Dip cauliflower florets in batter, shake off excess.
4. Optional: roll battered florets in panko for extra crunch.
5. Place on tray with space between.

---

## 3) Bake (18–22 min)
1. Bake **18–22 minutes**, flipping halfway, until golden and crisp.
2. For extra crisp: broil 1–2 minutes at the end (watch closely).

---

## 4) Sauce (2 min)
- Whisk mayo + sweet chili + sriracha + vinegar/lime + sesame oil.

---

## 5) Serve (two pickup options)
### Option A (best crisp for pickup)
- Pack cauliflower bites dry in container.
- Pack sauce cup separately.
- Customer dips or tosses.

### Option B (best photo immediately)
- Toss hot bites with sauce lightly.
- Garnish with scallions + sesame.

---

## Quality Checks
- Bites crisp outside, tender inside.
- Sauce creamy sweet-spicy balance.
- Not soggy (pack sauce separately for delivery).

---

## RiceMap24 Portioning
- **150–180 g** bites per side portion
- Sauce cup: **30–40 ml**

**Upsells**
- Extra sauce cup
- Spicy level: mild/medium/hot
- “Chicken bang bang” upgrade (add chicken bites instead of cauliflower)

---

## Scaling (10 side portions)
Multiply by **~1.7×** from 6-portion base.
Bake in two trays for best crisp.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Use vented container if possible (steam kills crisp).
- Always include sauce cup separately for best results.
- Label: “Dip or toss in sauce.”

---

## Storage & Reheat
- Best fresh.
- Reheat bites in oven/air fryer to re-crisp.
- Sauce keeps 3 days refrigerated.

---

## Allergens
- Wheat (flour, panko)
- Egg (mayo)
- Sesame (sesame oil/seeds)
- Dairy if using milk in batter
