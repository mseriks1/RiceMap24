# Hunan Stir-Fried Beef (小炒黄牛肉 / Xiǎo Chǎo Niúròu) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A proper Hunan “rice killer”: **thin-sliced beef** seared fast over high heat with **fresh chilies**, garlic, ginger, celery, scallions, and a big finish of cilantro.  
This is **not** the saucy sweet “Chinese restaurant beef”—it’s bold, dry, intensely aromatic, and perfect for RiceMap24 rice bowls.

**Source recipe:** Woks of Love — “Hunan Stir-fried Beef (小炒牛肉)” (Nov 5, 2025)  
https://woksoflove.com/hunan-stir-fried-beef/

---

## Summary
- **Cuisine:** Chinese (Hunan)
- **Style:** Ultra-fast high-heat stir-fry (under 2 minutes cook time)
- **Yield (source):** 2 servings (scale up easily; cook beef in batches)
- **Active time:** ~15 minutes
- **Total time:** ~20 minutes
- **Skill level:** Medium (mise en place + heat control)

---

## Equipment
- Wok or 12-inch / 30 cm skillet
- 3–4 small prep bowls (mise en place)
- Wok spatula/tongs
- Plate/bowl for par-cooked beef
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Ingredients (2 servings)

## A) Beef + Marinade (velvet-style)
- **8 oz (225 g)** beef tenderloin *(or flank steak, sirloin)*
- **1** large egg white
- **2 Tbsp** light soy sauce
- **2 Tbsp** cornstarch *(or potato starch)*
- **¼ tsp** freshly ground black pepper
- **1 Tbsp** cooking oil (vegetable/canola)

## B) Stir-fry
- **3–4 Tbsp** cooking oil, divided (vegetable/canola/peanut)
- **5 cloves** garlic, finely minced
- **1 inch** ginger *(about 5 slices)*, finely minced
- **5** millet peppers *(or Thai chilies)*  
  - For milder: 1–2 jalapeños, seeds/ribs removed
- **3 stalks** celery, cut into **2-inch** sections
- **3 stalks** scallions, white + green parts, cut into **2-inch** sections
- **1 Tbsp** oyster sauce
- **1 large handful** cilantro *(about 5 stalks)*, cut into **2-inch** sections (stems + leaves)

## To serve
- Steamed jasmine rice

---

## Prep (Critical — do this before turning on the heat)

### 1) Slice beef thin (best texture)
1. Put beef in freezer **20–30 minutes** until firm (easier slicing).
2. Slice **as thinly as possible** (about **3 mm / 1/8 inch**) **against the grain**.
3. Put sliced beef in a bowl of cold water **5 minutes**, then drain and pat very dry.

> Thin + against the grain = tender beef in a fast stir-fry.

### 2) Marinate beef (15 minutes)
1. In a bowl, combine dry beef slices with:
   - egg white
   - 2 Tbsp light soy
   - 2 Tbsp cornstarch
   - black pepper
2. Mix with your hands **in one direction** until absorbed and beef feels “tacky.”
3. Add 1 Tbsp oil and mix to seal.
4. Rest 15 minutes.

### 3) Mise en place (3 bowls)
- Bowl 1: minced garlic + ginger + chopped chilies
- Bowl 2: celery + scallions
- Bowl 3: cilantro
Keep oyster sauce open and ready.

---

## Cooking Method (Fast — total high heat time ≈ 2 minutes)

### Step 1 — Par-cook the beef (1 minute)
1. Heat wok/skillet over **medium-high**.
2. Add **2 Tbsp oil**.
3. Add beef in a single layer.
4. Let sear **30–45 seconds**, then stir-fry until **70% cooked** (still pink in spots), about **1 minute total**.
5. Immediately remove beef **and all juices** to a clean bowl.

> Don’t overcook here—the final toss finishes it.

---

### Step 2 — Final fry (45–60 seconds)
1. Wipe wok if needed. Put it back on **HIGH** heat until smoking.
2. Add **1–2 Tbsp oil**.

#### Aromatics (10–15 seconds)
3. Add garlic/ginger/chili mix. Stir-fry **10–15 seconds** until fragrant.

#### Veg + beef (30 seconds)
4. Add celery + scallions. Stir-fry **15 seconds**.
5. Add back beef + juices. Stir-fry **15 seconds**.

#### Sauce (15 seconds)
6. Drizzle **1 Tbsp oyster sauce** around the hot edges. Toss **15 seconds**.

#### Finish (15 seconds)
7. Turn **OFF** the heat.
8. Add cilantro. Toss in residual heat **15 seconds** (just wilted).

---

## Quality Checks
- **Beef:** tender, not gray, not overcooked.
- **Pan:** smoking hot for final fry (wok hei).
- **Timing:** 15 seconds + 15 seconds—this dish is a sprint.
- **Cilantro:** added off heat so it stays bright.

## Scaling for Production (10 bowls)
Multiply everything by **5×** (because base is 2 servings).

### Quick scale targets (10 bowls)
- Beef: **1.125 kg**
- Egg whites: **5**
- Light soy: **10 Tbsp**
- Cornstarch: **10 Tbsp**
- Black pepper: **1¼ tsp**
- Oil: plan **~300–350 ml** total depending on batches
- Garlic: **25 cloves**
- Ginger: **5 inches**
- Chilies: **25** (adjust)
- Celery: **15 stalks**
- Scallions: **15 stalks**
- Oyster sauce: **5 Tbsp**
- Cilantro: **5 large handfuls**

**Important:** Cook beef in batches (do not overcrowd), then finish each batch quickly with aromatics/veg/sauce.

## Packaging (To-Go) — RiceMap24 Recommended
- Container A: stir-fry (vent 1 minute before sealing to reduce steam)
- Container B: rice (separate)
- Optional cup: extra chili / extra oyster sauce drizzle

Label: “Spicy. Stir before eating.”

---

## Storage & Reheat
- Best fresh.
- Reheat in a very hot pan with a splash of oil for 30–60 seconds (microwave makes beef tough).

---

## Allergens
- Soy (soy sauce)
- Shellfish (oyster sauce)
- Gluten possible (sauces)
