# Iced Coconut Pandan Cooler (Tropical, Asian Twist) — RiceMap24 Drink Standard

A tropical, dessert-like cooler: coconut milk + pandan + lime, served iced.  
Great for RiceMap24 because it’s unique, photogenic, and bottle-friendly.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Coconut cooler (non-caffeinated)
- **Asian twist:** Pandan + coconut
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2–3 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## Classic size (≈450 ml)
- **220 ml** coconut milk (or 150 ml coconut milk + 70 ml water for lighter)
- **20–30 ml** pandan syrup
- **10 ml** lime juice (optional but recommended)
- Pinch of salt
- **180–220 g** ice

## Optional add-ons
- 1 Tbsp coconut cream (richer)
- Basil or mint garnish
- Sesame sprinkle (tiny)

---

## Sweetness Levels
- **Less sweet:** 15–20 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 25 ml
- **Extra sweet:** 35 ml

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Add pandan syrup + lime + pinch salt to cup and stir.
2. Add coconut milk and stir.
3. Fill with ice and stir once more.

---

## Batch Setup

## A) Pandan syrup (if you don’t already have it)
(Use the same syrup as Pandan Iced Latte.)
- 500 ml water + 500 g sugar + 2 tsp pandan extract

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic)
- Coconut milk: **2.2 L**
- Pandan syrup: **250 ml**
- Lime juice: **100 ml** (optional)
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**
- Pinch salt

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Bottle is excellent (shake before drinking).
- Cup with tight lid works too.

**Label suggestion:** “Shake well. Keep cold.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Best same day.
- Up to 24 hours refrigerated (shake before drinking).

---

## Allergens
- Coconut
