# Pineapple Ginger Fizz (Tropical Sparkler) — RiceMap24 Drink Standard

A bright tropical sparkler: pineapple + ginger + lime, topped with sparkling water.  
Fits Asian food menus very well and sells as a refreshing “mocktail” option.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Sparkling tropical drink
- **Profile:** pineapple + ginger + lime
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2 minutes (if base is ready)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Pineapple base
- **90 ml** pineapple juice
- **15 ml** lime juice
- **15 ml** ginger syrup

## B) Sparkling + ice
- **220–260 ml** sparkling water (cold)
- **180–220 g** ice

## Optional garnish
- Mint
- Pineapple wedge

---

## Sweetness Levels
Adjust via ginger syrup:
- **Less sweet:** 10 ml
- **Classic:** 15 ml
- **Extra sweet:** 25 ml

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Add pineapple juice + lime + ginger syrup to cup and stir.
2. Fill with ice.
3. Top with sparkling water.
4. Stir gently once.

---

## Batch Setup (Base for 10 drinks)
Mix and chill:
- **900 ml** pineapple juice
- **150 ml** lime juice
- **150 ml** ginger syrup

Portion: **120 ml base** per drink + top with sparkling.

Need sparkling water: **2.4 L** for 10.

---

## Quick Ginger Syrup (if you don’t already have it)
- 250 g ginger slices + 1 L water simmer 15 min, strain
- add 500 g sugar or honey, dissolve, chill

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
Sparkling loses fizz. Best:
- Bottle the base (no sparkling) + customer adds sparkling at home.
- Or serve ready-to-drink for quick pickup.

**Label suggestion:** “Best within 20–30 minutes for max fizz.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Base: 2–3 days refrigerated

---

## Allergens
None typical.
