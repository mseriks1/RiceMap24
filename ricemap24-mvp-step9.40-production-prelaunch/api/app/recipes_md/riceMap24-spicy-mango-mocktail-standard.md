# Spicy Mango “Chamoy” Mocktail (Asian-Inspired Twist) — RiceMap24 Drink Standard

A bold, viral-style tropical drink: mango + lime + a spicy-salty rim.  
Not strictly Asian traditional, but it fits Asian street-food menus and sells well as a “fun” non-alcoholic mocktail.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Mocktail / tropical cooler
- **Profile:** mango + lime + spicy-salty rim
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 3 minutes (if base is ready)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Mango base
- **80 ml** mango purée
- **20 ml** lime juice
- **15 ml** simple syrup (adjust)
- **120 ml** cold water (or sparkling for upsell)

## B) Ice
- **180–220 g** ice

## C) Spicy-salty rim (signature)
- Chili powder + salt (1:1)
- Optional: a little sugar mixed in

## Optional garnish
- Mango slice
- Mint

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Rim cup: dip edge in lime, then into chili-salt.
2. Add ice.
3. Shake/stir mango purée + lime + syrup + water.
4. Pour into cup and garnish.

---

## Batch base for 10 drinks
- Mango purée: **800 ml**
- Lime juice: **200 ml**
- Simple syrup: **150 ml**
Mix and chill.  
Portion: **100–120 ml base** per drink + top with water/sparkling.

---

## Sparkling Upsell
Top with sparkling water for a “mocktail” vibe.

---

## Packaging
- Best served ready-to-drink (rim effect is part of the fun).
- If bottled: keep rim mix in a small packet (customer rims at home).

---

## Allergens
None typical.
