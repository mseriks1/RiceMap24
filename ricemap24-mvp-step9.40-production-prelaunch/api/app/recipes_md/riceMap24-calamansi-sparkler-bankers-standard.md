# Calamansi Sparkler — RiceMap24 “Banker Drink” Standard

A bright, tropical, ultra-refreshing drink: **calamansi** citrus + sparkling water.  
Perfect for RiceMap24 because it’s **fast**, **high-margin**, and pairs especially well with rich/spicy food.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Sparkling citrus drink
- **Asian twist:** Calamansi (Filipino citrus)
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2 minutes
- **Total time:** 2 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this is a RiceMap24 “Banker”
- **Broad appeal:** tastes like premium lemonade.
- **Very fast:** syrup + sparkling + ice.
- **Great with food:** resets the palate with spicy/salty dishes.
- **Upsells:** mint, ginger, lychee, “spicy” version.

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Calamansi base (choose one)
### Option 1 (best): Calamansi juice
- **30–40 ml** calamansi juice

### Option 2: Calamansi concentrate
- Use per label, then standardize to “juice strength.”

## B) Sweetener
Choose one:
- **15–20 ml** simple syrup (1:1 sugar+water)  
OR
- **15 ml** honey syrup (1:1 honey+water)

## C) Sparkling + ice
- **220–260 ml** sparkling water (cold)
- **180–220 g** ice

## D) Optional garnish
- Lime wheel
- Mint
- Calamansi peel twist (if you have it)

---

## Sweetness Levels (menu toggles)
- **Less sweet:** 10–12 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 15–20 ml
- **Extra sweet:** 25 ml

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)

1) Add calamansi juice to cup.  
2) Add syrup and stir briefly.  
3) Fill with ice.  
4) Top with cold sparkling water.  
5) Stir gently once (don’t kill bubbles).  

**Taste check:**
- Too sharp → +5 ml syrup
- Too sweet → +30 ml sparkling

---

## Batch Setup (Recommended)

## A) Calamansi syrup (best for speed)
- **500 ml** calamansi juice
- **500 g** sugar
- **500 ml** hot water

Stir until dissolved. Chill.

**Portion per drink:** 30–35 ml syrup + 200–240 ml sparkling.

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic)
- Calamansi syrup: **320–350 ml**
- Sparkling water: **2.3 L**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**

---

## Optional Variations / Upsells
- **Mint calamansi:** add mint and slap leaves before adding sparkling.
- **Ginger calamansi:** add 10 ml ginger syrup (premium).
- **Lychee calamansi:** add 20 ml lychee syrup (sweet, tropical).
- **Spicy calamansi:** rim cup with chili-salt or add 1–2 slices fresh chili.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
Sparkling drinks lose bubbles. Best practices:
1) **Bottle the calamansi syrup base** (no sparkling) + customer adds sparkling at home  
2) If selling ready-to-drink: use a tight lid and recommend drinking soon.

**Label suggestion:** “Best within 20–30 minutes for maximum fizz.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Calamansi syrup: **7 days** refrigerated
- Sparkling water: keep cold and sealed

None typical.
