# Sesame-Ginger Chicken Caesar Salad Box (Asian Twist, Pickup-Friendly) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A familiar Western salad format (Caesar salad box) upgraded with an Asian pantry twist: **sesame-ginger chicken** + optional miso Caesar dressing.  
Not traditional Asian—this is fusion built for pickup/delivery with clean component packing.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Salad box (main or side)
- **Asian twist:** Sesame-ginger chicken + optional miso Caesar
- **Yield:** 4 meal salad boxes
- **Active time:** ~20 min
- **Total time:** ~25 min
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Pickup-friendly:** pack components separately; stays crisp.
- **Broad appeal:** Caesar salad is mainstream.
- **Upsells:** extra chicken, extra dressing cup, add avocado, add crunchy topping.
- **Repeat-order friendly:** lighter “banker” option.

---

## Equipment
- Skillet (or oven)
- Mixing bowl
- Knife + cutting board
- 4 salad containers + dressing cups

---

## Ingredients (4 salad boxes)

## A) Sesame-ginger chicken
- **600 g** chicken thigh or breast, sliced
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil
- Salt + pepper

### Quick sesame-ginger glaze
- **2 Tbsp** soy sauce
- **1 Tbsp** honey
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 tsp** sesame oil
- **1 tsp** grated ginger
- **1 clove** garlic, grated
- **60 ml** water
- **1 tsp** cornstarch + 1 Tbsp water (slurry)

---

## B) Caesar salad base
- **2 heads** romaine, chopped and dried well
- **60 g** parmesan, shaved or grated
- **1 cup** croutons (pack separately)

## C) Dressing options
### Option 1 (simple): Store Caesar dressing
- 120–150 ml

### Option 2 (Asian twist): Miso Caesar dressing (quick)
- **120 ml** Caesar dressing
- **1 Tbsp** white miso
- **1 tsp** lemon juice
Whisk until smooth.

---

## D) Optional add-ons (for sales)
- Nori strips (small handful)
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Cucumber ribbons
- Avocado (premium)

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Cook chicken (10–12 min)
1. Sear chicken in a hot skillet with oil until browned and cooked.
2. Add glaze ingredients (except slurry) and simmer 1 minute.
3. Add slurry and stir until glossy.
4. Cool chicken 5 minutes (prevents salad wilting).

---

## 2) Build salad boxes (5 min)
Per box:
1. Romaine base + parmesan.
2. Add chicken portion on top (or in a separate compartment if available).
3. Pack croutons in a small bag/cup.
4. Pack dressing in a sealed cup.
5. Optional: sesame/nori garnish cup.

---

## Quality Checks
- Salad crisp and dry.
- Chicken glazed, not watery.
- Dressing cup sealed; croutons dry.

---

## Portioning (Per box)
- Chicken: **150 g cooked**
- Romaine: generous handful
- Dressing: 30–40 ml
- Croutons: small handful

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Keep croutons separate.
- Add label: “Pour dressing and add croutons right before eating.”

---

## Storage
- Best same day.
- Chicken can be cooked ahead and chilled; assemble fresh.

---

## Allergens
- Soy (soy sauce, miso)
- Dairy (parmesan, dressing)
- Egg (Caesar dressing)
- Wheat (croutons)
