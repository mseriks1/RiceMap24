# Kimbap / Gimbap (Korean Rice Rolls) — Choose Protein + Pickup Box — RiceMap24 Standard

A premium handheld Asian genre: **kimbap** (Korean rice rolls).  
It’s a perfect **lunch box** item: clean slices, great photos, and batch-friendly prep.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Kimbap (Korean rice roll)
- **Cuisine vibe:** Korean
- **Yield:** 4 rolls (32–40 pieces)
- **Active time:** ~30 minutes
- **Total time:** ~45 minutes
- **Skill level:** Medium (rolling + tight slices)

---

## Why kimbap works for RiceMap24
- **Photogenic cross-section** sells.
- **Batch-friendly** once fillings are prepped.
- **Pickup-friendly** in bento-style boxes.
- **Upsells:** extra roll, extra sauce cup, add kimchi side.

---

## Ingredients (4 rolls)

## A) Rice
- **500 g** short-grain rice (uncooked)
- Cook per rice cooker instructions
- Seasoning:
  - **1 tsp** salt
  - **2 Tbsp** sesame oil
  - **1 Tbsp** toasted sesame seeds

## B) Wrap
- **4 sheets** roasted seaweed (gim/nori)
- Optional: extra sheets for double-wrap

## C) Standard fillings (premium look)
Choose 4–5 per roll set:

### 1) Egg strips (classic)
- 4 eggs, lightly salted, cooked into thin omelet, cut into strips

### 2) Spinach (quick)
- 300 g spinach, blanched 30 sec, squeezed dry
- Season with 1 tsp sesame oil + pinch salt

### 3) Carrot (color)
- 2 carrots, julienne, sauté 1–2 min with pinch salt

### 4) Pickled radish (danmuji)
- 1 pack danmuji, cut into strips (signature kimbap flavor)

### 5) Cucumber (fresh)
- Cut into long sticks, keep dry

## D) Choose Protein (pick ONE)
### Option 1: Bulgogi beef (premium)
- 450–500 g thin beef, quick bulgogi marinade, cooked and reduced dry

### Option 2: Ham + cheese (popular)
- Ham strips + cheese strips (easy, high repeat)

### Option 3: Tuna mayo (banker)
- 2 cans tuna + mayo + soy

### Option 4: Spam-style (if you want)
- Pan-seared luncheon meat strips

## Equipment
- Bamboo rolling mat
- Sharp knife
- Bowl of water (for rice handling)
- Cutting board

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Cook and season rice (35–40 minutes incl rest)
1. Cook rice and rest 10 minutes.
2. Mix in salt, sesame oil, sesame seeds.

---

## 2) Prep fillings (10 minutes)
- Make egg strips.
- Prep spinach, carrot, cucumber, danmuji.
- Cook your chosen protein and keep it fairly dry (important).

---

## 3) Roll kimbap (5–8 minutes)
1. Place gim on mat (shiny side down).
2. Spread rice thinly, leaving 2–3 cm at the top edge.
3. Lay fillings in a tight line across the bottom third.
4. Roll tightly using the mat.
5. Seal edge with a little water if needed.
6. Brush outside with a little sesame oil.

Repeat.

---

## 4) Slice (clean slices)
1. Let rolls rest 3–5 minutes.
2. Oil knife lightly.
3. Slice into 8–10 pieces per roll.

## Storage
- Kimbap best fresh (rice dries in fridge).
- If needed: hold a few hours at cool room temp.

---

## Allergens

- Soy
- Sesame
- Fish (tuna)
- Wheat possible (some sauces)
