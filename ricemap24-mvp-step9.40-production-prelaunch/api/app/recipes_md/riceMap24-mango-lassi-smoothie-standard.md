# Mango Lassi Smoothie (Bottle-Friendly) — RiceMap24 Drink Standard

A creamy, tropical “banker” smoothie: mango + yogurt with a light spice option.  
Great for RiceMap24 because it’s familiar, filling, easy to batch, and works well in bottles for pickup/delivery.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Smoothie / yogurt drink
- **Cuisine vibe:** South Asian-inspired
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2–3 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## Classic size (≈450–500 ml)
- **250 g** mango (frozen cubes preferred for thickness) OR 200 ml mango purée
- **200 ml** plain yogurt (or Greek yogurt for thicker)
- **120–160 ml** cold milk or water (adjust thickness)
- **10–20 g** sugar or honey (optional; depends on mango sweetness)
- Pinch of salt

## Optional “signature” spice (very small)
- **1/8 tsp** ground cardamom (recommended)
- Optional: tiny pinch cinnamon

## Ice
- If using fresh mango, add **80–120 g** ice.

---

## Sweetness Levels (menu toggles)
- **No added sugar:** rely on mango
- **Classic:** 10 g honey/sugar
- **Sweet:** 20 g honey/sugar

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Add liquid first (milk/water).
2. Add mango + yogurt + salt + optional honey + cardamom.
3. Blend **20–30 seconds** until smooth and thick.
4. Taste and adjust:
   - Too thick → add 30 ml liquid
   - Too thin → add more frozen mango or a little ice

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (bottle service)
- Mango (frozen): **2.5 kg**
- Yogurt: **2.0 L**
- Milk/water: **1.4 L** (adjust)
- Honey/sugar (classic): **120 g**
- Cardamom: ~**1 tsp** total (optional)

**Ops tip:** Pre-portion frozen mango bags (250 g each) for speed.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Best as a **sealed bottle** (shake before drinking).
- Use wide straw if serving in cup.
- Keep cold.

**Label suggestion:** “Keep refrigerated. Best within 24 hours.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Best same day.
- Up to **24 hours** refrigerated (shake before drinking).

---

## Allergens
- Dairy (yogurt, milk)
