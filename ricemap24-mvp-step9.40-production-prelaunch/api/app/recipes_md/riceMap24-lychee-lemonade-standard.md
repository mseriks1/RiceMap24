# Lychee Lemonade — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Tropical, Pickup-Friendly)

A bright, tropical lemonade upgrade: **lychee** + lemon/lime.  
Great for RiceMap24 because it’s refreshing, very “Instagrammable,” and easy to batch as a syrup base.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Lemonade
- **Profile:** lychee + citrus
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2 minutes (if base is ready)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Lychee
Choose one:
- **30 ml** lychee syrup (from canned lychees)  
OR
- **25 ml** lychee syrup (store-bought)

## B) Citrus
- **25 ml** lemon juice (or 15 ml lemon + 10 ml lime)

## C) Sweetness
- If using syrup: often enough
- Optional: **10 ml** simple syrup if you want it sweeter

## D) Water + ice
- **220–260 ml** cold water (or sparkling for upsell)
- **180–220 g** ice

## Optional garnish
- Mint
- Lemon wheel
- A couple lychees (in cup, looks premium)

---

## Sweetness Levels (menu toggles)
- **Less sweet:** 20 ml lychee syrup
- **Classic:** 30 ml lychee syrup
- **Extra sweet:** 40 ml lychee syrup

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Add lychee syrup + lemon juice to cup.
2. Fill with ice.
3. Add cold water (or sparkling).
4. Stir gently.
5. Garnish.

---

## Batch Setup (Recommended)

## A) Lychee-citrus base (for 10 drinks)
This is a “pour base” you top with water/sparkling.
- **300 ml** lychee syrup
- **250 ml** lemon juice
- **100 ml** water

Mix, chill.

Portion: **65 ml base** per drink + 220–260 ml water/sparkling.

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic)
- Base: **650 ml**
- Water/sparkling: **2.4 L**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**

---

## Sparkling Upsell
- Use sparkling water instead of still.
- Add a mint sprig for a “mocktail” look.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Bottle the base (no water) + customer adds water/sparkling at home for best freshness.
- Or serve ready-to-drink with tight lid.

**Label suggestion:** “Shake or stir. Add sparkling if included.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Base: **2–3 days** refrigerated

---

## Allergens
None typical.
