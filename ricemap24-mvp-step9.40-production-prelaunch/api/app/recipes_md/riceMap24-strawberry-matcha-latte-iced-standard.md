# Strawberry Matcha Latte (Iced) — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Asian Twist, Café-Style)

A highly popular modern café drink: strawberry base + matcha layer.  
Great for RiceMap24 because it’s photogenic, premium-feeling, and easy to batch (strawberry syrup + matcha concentrate).

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Iced latte (non-coffee)
- **Asian twist:** Matcha
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2–3 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (smooth matcha)

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Strawberry base
Choose one:
- **35–45 ml** strawberry syrup (batch)  
OR
- **60–80 ml** strawberry purée (strain for smooth)

## B) Milk + matcha
- **200–240 ml** cold milk (whole or oat)
- **2 tsp** matcha powder
- **60 ml** cold water (to dissolve matcha)

## C) Ice
- **180–220 g** ice

---

## Sweetness Levels
- **Less sweet:** 30 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 40 ml
- **Extra sweet:** 55 ml

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Fill cup with ice.
2. Add strawberry syrup.
3. Add milk (creates pink layer).
4. Make matcha paste (sift + whisk with a little water, then the rest).
5. Pour matcha gently on top for a green layer.
6. Stir before drinking.

---

## Batch Setup

## A) Strawberry syrup (house)
- **500 g** strawberries (fresh or frozen)
- **500 g** sugar
- **500 ml** water
- **1 Tbsp** lemon juice

Method:
1. Simmer strawberries + water 10 minutes.
2. Strain, then dissolve sugar in warm liquid.
3. Add lemon juice, chill.

Portion: **40 ml per drink**.

---

## B) Matcha concentrate (for 10 drinks)
- **40 g** matcha
- **600 ml** cold water
Whisk smooth, chill. Shake before use.
Portion: **60 ml per drink**.

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic)
- Strawberry syrup: **400 ml**
- Milk: **2.2 L**
- Matcha concentrate: **600 ml**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Cup with tight lid is best (shows layers).
- Bottle works too, but layers will mix—still tasty.
- Ice separate if pickup is long.

**Label suggestion:** “Shake or stir before drinking.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Strawberry syrup: **7 days** refrigerated
- Matcha concentrate: best within 24–48 hours

---

## Allergens
- Dairy if using milk; oat milk option.
