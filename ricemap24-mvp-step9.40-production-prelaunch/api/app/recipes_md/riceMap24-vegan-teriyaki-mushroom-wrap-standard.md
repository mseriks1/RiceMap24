# Vegan Teriyaki Mushroom Wrap (Miso Vegan Mayo + Pickles) — RiceMap24 Standard Wrap Recipe (Vegan)

A premium vegan wrap with strong Asian pantry flavor: glossy teriyaki mushrooms, egg-free miso mayo, cucumber crunch, and bright pickles.  
Designed for pickup/delivery and easy batch scaling.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Vegan wrap (Asian-inspired)
- **Asian twist:** teriyaki mushrooms + miso vegan mayo + sesame + pickles
- **Yield:** 4 large wraps
- **Active time:** ~15–20 minutes
- **Total time:** ~20–25 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Vegan but “meaty”** texture (king oyster + shiitake).
- **Photogenic** (glossy mushrooms + bright pickles).
- **Delivery friendly** (stable fillings, pickles can be separate).
- **Upsells:** extra mushrooms, extra sauce cup, add chili crisp, add avocado.

---

## Ingredients (4 wraps)

## A) Teriyaki mushrooms (the identity)
- **800 g** mushrooms (king oyster + shiitake mix is ideal), sliced
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil

### Quick teriyaki glaze (vegan)
- **90 ml** soy sauce
- **60 ml** water
- **2 Tbsp** brown sugar (or maple)
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 tsp** grated ginger
- **1 clove** garlic, grated
- **1 tsp** cornstarch + **1 Tbsp** water (slurry)
- Optional finish: 1 tsp sesame oil

---

## B) Vegan miso mayo (egg-free)
- **160 g** vegan mayonnaise
- **1.5 Tbsp** white miso
- **1 tsp** lime juice (or rice vinegar)
- Pinch salt (often not needed)

---

## C) Crunch + freshness
- Lettuce leaves (recommended moisture barrier)
- **1 cucumber**, thin sticks
- Pickled red onion or do chua (about **200 g**)
- Optional: shredded cabbage (adds volume)
- Optional: nori strips + sesame seeds
- Optional: cilantro (fresh finish)

## D) Wraps
- **4 large** tortillas/wraps

---

## Equipment
- Skillet/wok
- Small bowl (slurry)
- Mixing bowl (miso mayo)
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Mix vegan miso mayo (1 minute)
- Stir vegan mayo + miso + lime until smooth.

---

## 2) Cook mushrooms down (8–10 minutes)
1. Heat skillet medium-high with oil.
2. Add mushrooms and cook until they release water.
3. Continue cooking until most moisture is gone and mushrooms start to brown.

**Key:** cook fairly dry before glazing for best texture.

---

## 3) Add teriyaki glaze (2 minutes)
1. Mix soy, water, sugar, vinegar, ginger, garlic.
2. Pour into pan, simmer 1 minute.
3. Add slurry and stir until glossy and clingy.
4. Optional: finish with a tiny sesame oil drizzle.

---

## 4) Assemble wraps
Per wrap:
1. Warm tortilla briefly.
2. Add lettuce barrier.
3. Spread 1–2 Tbsp miso mayo.
4. Add teriyaki mushrooms (generous).
5. Add cucumber + pickles (+ optional cabbage).
6. Finish with sesame + nori + cilantro.
7. Roll tight.

---

## Portioning (Per wrap)
- Mushrooms: **180–240 g cooked**
- Miso mayo: **25–30 g**
- Pickles: **40–60 g**
- Cucumber/greens: as needed

---

## Batch for 10 wraps
- Mushrooms: **2.0 kg**
- Teriyaki glaze: 2.5×
- Vegan miso mayo: 400 g vegan mayo + miso/lime

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Delivery)
Best practice:
- Wrap A: tortilla + lettuce + mayo + mushrooms (rolled)
- Cup B: pickles (optional) for extra crispness
- Optional cup: extra miso mayo or extra teriyaki drizzle

**Label:** “Add pickles last (optional).”

---

## Storage
- Cooked mushrooms: 2 days refrigerated (reheat briefly in pan)
- Miso mayo: 3–4 days refrigerated
- Pickles: 5–7 days refrigerated

---

## Allergens
- Soy (soy sauce, miso)
- Wheat (tortilla)
- Gluten possible (soy sauce)
- Sesame if used
