# Black Sesame “Milk” (Iced) — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Asian Twist, Bottle-Friendly)

A modern Asian café-style drink: nutty **black sesame** blended into a lightly sweetened milk.  
Great for RiceMap24 because it’s unique, photogenic (gray/black), and bottle-friendly.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Iced milk drink
- **Asian twist:** Black sesame
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 3 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## Classic size (≈450 ml)
- **250 ml** milk (whole or oat)
- **25–30 g** black sesame paste *(or 20 g ground toasted black sesame)*
- **15–20 ml** simple syrup or honey syrup
- Pinch of salt (tiny)
- **180–220 g** ice

## Optional “premium” add-ons
- 1/4 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp tahini (adds depth, optional)
- Sesame sprinkle top

---

## Sweetness Levels
- **Less sweet:** 10 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 15–20 ml
- **Extra sweet:** 25 ml

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Add milk to blender.
2. Add black sesame paste + syrup + pinch salt.
3. Blend **15–20 seconds** until smooth.
4. Pour over ice.

**Texture check:** should be smooth, not gritty. If gritty, blend longer or strain.

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (bottle service)
- Milk: **2.5 L**
- Black sesame paste: **270 g** (range 250–320 g depending on intensity)
- Syrup: **180 ml**
- Pinch salt

Blend in two batches, bottle, keep chilled.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Bottle is ideal (shake before drinking).
- If serving in cup: wide straw is nice.

**Label suggestion:** “Shake well. Keep chilled.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Best same day.
- Up to **24–36 hours** refrigerated (shake before drinking).

---

## Allergens
- Sesame
- Dairy if using milk
