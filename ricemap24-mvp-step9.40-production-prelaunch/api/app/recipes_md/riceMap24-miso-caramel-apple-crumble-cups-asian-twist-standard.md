# Miso Caramel Apple Crumble Cups (Asian Twist, Pickup-Friendly) — RiceMap24 Dessert Standard

A familiar comfort dessert (apple crumble) with a Japanese twist: **miso caramel** (sweet-salty umami).  
Baked as individual cups/jars for pickup/delivery—no melting, strong aroma, high-margin upsells.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Apple crumble cups with miso caramel
- **Asian twist:** White miso in caramel
- **Yield:** 10 cups (200–250 ml)
- **Active time:** ~25 minutes
- **Total time:** ~55–65 minutes
- **Skill level:** Medium (caramel + crumble bake)

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Pickup-friendly:** individual cups; serve warm or room temp.
- **Big “wow” flavor:** miso caramel is memorable but still mainstream-friendly.
- **Batch-friendly:** bake a tray of cups.
- **Upsells:** extra caramel cup, “add vanilla cream” (optional), larger cup.

---

## Equipment
- Saucepan (caramel)
- Mixing bowls
- Knife + cutting board
- Baking tray
- 10 oven-safe cups/ramekins/jars (200–250 ml)

---

## Ingredients

## A) Apple filling
- **1.2 kg** apples (Granny Smith + sweet mix), peeled and diced 1.5 cm
- **60 g** brown sugar
- **1 Tbsp** lemon juice
- **1 tsp** cinnamon
- **1/4 tsp** salt
- **1 Tbsp** cornstarch
- Optional: 1 tsp vanilla

## B) Crumble topping
- **180 g** all-purpose flour
- **120 g** rolled oats
- **120 g** brown sugar
- **1/2 tsp** salt
- **150 g** cold butter, cubed
- Optional: **60 g** chopped nuts (almond/pecan) for premium crunch
- Optional: **1 Tbsp** toasted sesame seeds (tiny “Asian hint”)

## C) Miso caramel
- **150 g** sugar
- **60 ml** water
- **120 ml** heavy cream (warm)
- **30 g** butter
- **1.5 Tbsp** white miso paste (start with 1 Tbsp for mild)
- Pinch of salt (optional; miso is salty)

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Make crumble (5 minutes)
1. Mix flour, oats, brown sugar, salt (and nuts if using).
2. Rub in cold butter until crumbly (pea-size bits).
3. Chill crumble in fridge while you prep apples.

---

## 2) Prep apple filling (8 minutes)
1. Toss diced apples with brown sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, salt, cornstarch (and vanilla if using).
2. Let sit 5 minutes (juices start forming).

---

## 3) Make miso caramel (10 minutes)
1. Warm cream.
2. Cook sugar + water to amber (cola color).
3. Turn off heat, whisk in warm cream carefully.
4. Add butter and whisk smooth.
5. Whisk in white miso until fully dissolved.
6. Cool 10 minutes (thickens).

---

## 4) Assemble cups (5 minutes)
Per cup:
1. Spoon apple filling to 2/3 full.
2. Drizzle 1–2 tsp miso caramel into apples (optional swirl).
3. Top with crumble generously.

---

## 5) Bake (25–30 minutes)
1. Bake at **180°C** until crumble is golden and apples bubble:
   - **25–30 minutes**

---

## 6) Finish (service)
- Drizzle miso caramel on top.
- Serve warm or room temp.
- Optional: small “extra caramel” cup for premium upsell.

---

## Quality Checks
- Apples tender, not mush.
- Crumble golden and crisp.
- Caramel sweet-salty balanced (not overly miso).

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Cups with lids or foil.
- If warm pickup: vent 2 minutes before sealing to avoid soggy crumble.
- Label: “Best same day. Reheat 3–5 min at 170°C for crisp.”

---

## Storage
- Refrigerated: 2–3 days
- Reheat to re-crisp crumble.
- Caramel keeps 5–7 days refrigerated.

---

## Allergens

- Wheat (flour)
- Soy (miso)
- Nuts (if used)
