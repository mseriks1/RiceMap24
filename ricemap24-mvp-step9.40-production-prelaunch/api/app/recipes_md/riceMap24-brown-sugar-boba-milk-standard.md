# Brown Sugar Boba Milk (No Tea) — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Asian Café)

A strong “Asian café” seller: creamy milk + **brown sugar syrup** + boba pearls.  
Perfect for RiceMap24 because it’s dessert-like and highly popular, but note: boba quality depends on pickup time.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Boba milk (no tea)
- **Asian twist:** Brown sugar + tapioca pearls
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 4–6 minutes (if boba is cooked)
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (boba timing)

---

## Important quality note (boba)
Boba hardens when chilled. Best practice:
- Offer boba drinks for **quick pickup windows (30–45 min)**.
- Or offer “boba on demand” only.

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Boba
- **60–80 g** cooked boba pearls

## B) Brown sugar syrup
- **25–35 ml** brown sugar syrup (batch)

## C) Milk + ice
- **220–260 ml** cold milk (whole or oat)
- **180–220 g** ice (optional; some serve without ice for thicker feel)

---

## Sweetness Levels
- **Less sweet:** 20 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 30 ml
- **Extra sweet:** 40 ml

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Add cooked boba to cup.
2. Add brown sugar syrup and swirl inside cup for streaks.
3. Add ice (optional).
4. Add cold milk.
5. Stir before drinking.

---

## Batch Setup

## A) Brown sugar syrup (house)
- **500 g** brown sugar
- **500 ml** water
- Pinch salt
Optional: 1 tsp vanilla

Warm until dissolved, cool, bottle.  
Portion: 30 ml per drink.

---

## B) Cooking boba (quick guide)
Use package instructions, then standardize:
1. Boil water, add boba.
2. Cook 20–30 minutes (varies by brand).
3. Rest 20 minutes in hot water.
4. Drain and hold in a little brown sugar syrup.

**Hold time:** best within 2–3 hours.

---

## Batch for 10 drinks
- Milk: **2.4 L**
- Brown sugar syrup: **300 ml**
- Cooked boba: **700 g**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg** (if using)

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Use wide boba straw.
- Best served in cup, not bottle (boba blocks bottle neck).
- Label: “Best within 45 minutes for soft pearls.”

---

## Allergens
- Dairy (milk) unless oat milk
- None typical otherwise
