# Cucumber-Lime Cooler (Clean “Food-Friendly” Drink) — RiceMap24 Drink Standard

A light, refreshing drink that pairs with spicy/greasy food: cucumber + lime + a touch of sweetness.  
Not strongly “Asian,” but it fits Asian menus perfectly as a palate cleanser and sells well as a low-sugar option.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Cooler (still or sparkling)
- **Profile:** cucumber + lime + mint (optional)
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2 minutes (if base is ready)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Cucumber base
Choose one:
- **60 ml** cucumber juice (blended + strained)  
OR
- **40 ml** cucumber syrup (batch)

## B) Citrus
- **20 ml** lime juice (or lemon)

## C) Sweetness
- **10–15 ml** simple syrup (adjust)

## D) Water + ice
- **220–260 ml** cold water (or sparkling for upsell)
- **180–220 g** ice

## Optional garnish
- Mint
- Cucumber ribbon
- Lime wheel

---

## Sweetness Levels
- **Less sweet:** 5–10 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 10–15 ml
- **Extra sweet:** 20 ml

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Add cucumber base + lime juice + syrup to cup.
2. Fill with ice.
3. Add water (or sparkling).
4. Stir gently and garnish.

---

## Batch Setup (Recommended)

## A) Cucumber syrup (for 10 drinks)
- **500 g** cucumber (peeled optional)
- **500 ml** water
- **400 g** sugar
- **1 Tbsp** lemon juice
- Pinch salt

Method:
1. Blend cucumber + water, strain.
2. Warm strained cucumber water with sugar until dissolved (do not boil hard).
3. Add lemon juice + pinch salt.
4. Chill.

Portion: **40 ml syrup** per drink.

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic)
- Cucumber syrup: **400 ml**
- Lime juice: **200 ml**
- Water/sparkling: **2.4 L**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**

---

## Sparkling Upsell
- Replace water with sparkling.
- Add mint for a “mocktail” look.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Bottle without ice + separate ice cup for best quality.
- If ready-to-drink: tight lid and recommend drinking soon (sparkling loses fizz).

**Label suggestion:** “Shake or stir. Best chilled.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Cucumber syrup: **2–3 days** refrigerated
- Lime juice: best same day (or 24 hours)

---

## Allergens
None typical.
