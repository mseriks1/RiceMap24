# Salted Coconut Cold Brew (Asian Twist, Café-Style) — RiceMap24 Drink Standard

A modern Southeast Asia-inspired coffee: cold brew topped with a lightly **salted coconut cream** foam.  
Not a traditional Asian classic, but very common in “Asian café” style menus and sells as a premium drink.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Cold brew + coconut cream top
- **Asian twist:** Coconut + light salt (Vietnam/SE Asia café vibe)
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2–3 minutes (if cold brew is ready)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Coffee base
- **220 ml** cold brew (ready-to-drink)  
OR **150 ml** cold brew concentrate + 70 ml water

## B) Salted coconut cream top
- **60 ml** coconut cream (thick)
- **10–15 ml** simple syrup (or condensed milk for richer)
- Pinch of salt
- Optional: 20 ml milk/cream to soften texture

## C) Ice
- **180–220 g** ice

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Fill cup with ice and add cold brew.
2. In a small cup, whisk coconut cream + syrup + pinch salt until smooth.
   - If too thick, add a splash of milk.
3. Spoon/pour coconut cream on top.
4. Stir before drinking (or sip through layers).

---

## Batch Setup

## A) Cold brew (for 10 drinks)
Use the same concentrate method from Vietnamese iced coffee:
- 200 g coarse grounds + 1 L water = concentrate
For ready-to-drink:
- Dilute concentrate 1:1 with water.

Need: **2.2 L** ready-to-drink cold brew.

---

## B) Salted coconut cream mix (for 10 drinks)
- **600 ml** coconut cream
- **120 ml** simple syrup (adjust)
- **2–3 tsp** fine salt (start 2 tsp, taste)
- Optional: 200 ml milk for softer foam

Whisk and bottle. Shake before use.
Portion: **60 ml** per drink.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Best in cup with tight lid.
- If bottled: coconut layer will mix; still tasty.
- Ice separate if pickup is long.

**Label suggestion:** “Stir before drinking.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Cold brew: 3–5 days refrigerated
- Coconut cream mix: 2–3 days refrigerated (shake well)

---

## Allergens
- Coconut
- Dairy if using milk/cream
