# Asian Tuna Melt Baguette (Sesame-Wasabi Mayo) — RiceMap24 Standard (Breakfast/Lunch)

A familiar tuna baguette made premium with an Asian pantry twist: soy-sesame tuna mix + wasabi mayo + optional nori + (optional) melted cheese.  
Fusion (not traditional), designed for fast home-kitchen execution and high repeat orders.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Baguette sandwich
- **Asian twist:** sesame + soy + wasabi mayo + optional nori
- **Yield:** 4 baguettes
- **Active time:** ~10 minutes
- **Total time:** ~12–15 minutes (plus optional melt)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Very fast** (no cooking if you skip melt).
- **Premium feel** with wasabi + sesame + nori.
- **Pickup friendly** (stable, not messy).
- **Upsells:** extra tuna, spicy level, add cheese melt, add side pickles.

---

## Ingredients (4 baguettes)

## A) Tuna mix
- **4 cans** tuna (total drained weight ~450–500 g)
- **2 Tbsp** soy sauce
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 Tbsp** sesame oil
- **1 tsp** sugar (balance)
- **2 scallions**, thin sliced
- Optional: 1 tsp grated ginger

## B) Wasabi mayo (the identity)
- **120 ml** mayo
- **1–2 tsp** wasabi paste (adjust)
- **1 tsp** lime juice (optional)
- Pinch salt

## C) Crunch + finish
- **1 cucumber**, thin slices
- Optional: pickled red onion
- Optional: nori strips
- Optional: toasted sesame seeds

## D) Melt option (premium)
- Cheese slices or shredded cheese (mozzarella/cheddar)
- Optional: drizzle of teriyaki or chili crisp after melt

## E) Bread
- **4** baguettes (15–18 cm)

---

## Equipment
- Mixing bowl
- Knife + cutting board
- Optional: oven/broiler or toaster oven

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Mix tuna (3 minutes)
1. Drain tuna well (important).
2. Mix with soy, vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, scallions (and ginger if using).
3. Taste and adjust (more vinegar if too rich; more soy if too flat).

---

## 2) Mix wasabi mayo (1 minute)
- Stir mayo + wasabi + lime + pinch salt.

---

## 3) Build baguettes
Per baguette:
1. Split baguette.
2. Spread 1–2 Tbsp wasabi mayo.
3. Add tuna mix (about 110–130 g).
4. Add cucumber + optional pickles.
5. Finish with nori + sesame.

---

## 4) Optional “melt” version (3–5 minutes)
1. Add cheese on top of tuna.
2. Broil/toast until melted and bubbling.
3. Finish with nori + scallion.

**Note:** if you melt, serve quickly or bread softens.

---

## Portioning (Per baguette)
- Tuna: 110–130 g
- Mayo: 25–30 g
- Veg: as needed

---

## Batch for 10 baguettes
- Tuna drained: 1.2–1.3 kg (about 10–12 cans depending size)
- Wasabi mayo: 300 ml mayo
- Sauce mix: 2.5×

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Wrap in parchment.
- For melt version: vent 30 seconds before closing wrap (reduces steam sogginess).
- Offer pickles in a cup as premium option.

Label: “Best fresh. Spicy wasabi mayo.”

---

## Storage
- Tuna mix best same day, up to 24 hours refrigerated.
- Wasabi mayo 2–3 days refrigerated.

---

## Allergens
- Fish (tuna)
- Egg (mayo)
- Soy (soy sauce)
- Wheat (baguette)
- Sesame (sesame oil)
- Dairy (if cheese used)
- Gluten possible (soy sauce)
