# Chili Crisp Mac & Cheese (Asian Twist, Side or Main) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A familiar comfort food (mac & cheese) with a modern Asian pantry twist: **chili crisp** (laoganma-style).  
Not traditional Asian—this is fusion that sells well as a side order or a main bowl.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Mac & cheese bowl
- **Asian twist:** Chili crisp + optional soy/miso depth
- **Yield:** 6 portions (side) or 4 portions (main)
- **Active time:** ~20 min
- **Total time:** ~35 min
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Broad appeal:** mac & cheese is a “banker.”
- **Distinct signature:** chili crisp makes it memorable.
- **Pickup-friendly:** thick sauce travels well.
- **Upsells:** extra chili crisp cup, add chicken, add crispy topping.

---

## Equipment
- Pot for pasta
- Saucepan for cheese sauce
- Whisk
- Mixing spoon
- Optional: oven/broiler for toasted top (not required)

---

## Ingredients

## A) Pasta
- **400 g** macaroni (or shells)
- Salted water

## B) Cheese sauce (base)
- **60 g** butter
- **60 g** all-purpose flour
- **800 ml** milk (whole preferred)
- **1 tsp** Dijon mustard (optional but great)
- **1/2 tsp** salt (adjust)
- **1/2 tsp** black pepper
- **250 g** cheddar, grated
- **150 g** mozzarella, grated (or more cheddar)

## C) Asian twist
- **4–6 Tbsp** chili crisp (start at 4 Tbsp for mild)
- Optional depth:
  - **1 Tbsp** soy sauce OR
  - **1 Tbsp** white miso (whisk into sauce)
- Optional: **1 tsp** sesame oil (tiny)
- Optional: scallions and sesame seeds for garnish

## D) Optional crunchy top (premium)
- Panko breadcrumbs toasted in butter
- Or crispy fried onions

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Cook pasta (8–10 min)
1. Boil pasta in salted water until just al dente.
2. Drain and set aside.

---

## 2) Make roux + sauce (10–12 min)
1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium.
2. Whisk in flour and cook 1–2 minutes (no raw flour smell).
3. Slowly whisk in milk.
4. Simmer, whisking, until thickened (5–7 minutes).
5. Add mustard, salt, pepper.

---

## 3) Melt cheese (2–3 min)
1. Turn heat low.
2. Add cheeses in handfuls, whisking until smooth.

---

## 4) Add chili crisp (1–2 min)
1. Stir in chili crisp (start 4 Tbsp).
2. Optional: whisk in soy sauce or miso for depth.
3. Taste and adjust heat/salt.

---

## 5) Combine (2 min)
- Stir cooked pasta into sauce until fully coated.

---

## Quality Checks
- Sauce glossy and thick, not grainy.
- Chili crisp aroma present, not overpowering.
- Pasta still has bite.

---

## RiceMap24 Portioning
### Side order
- **250–300 g** mac & cheese per box

### Main bowl
- **400–450 g** mac & cheese per bowl

**Upsells**
- Extra chili crisp cup
- Add grilled chicken pieces
- Add panko crunch topping
- Add scallions/sesame garnish

---

## Scaling (10 side portions)
Multiply by **~1.7×** from the 6-portion base.
Cook pasta in a bigger pot; sauce in a wide pot for easy stirring.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Use sturdy containers (mac is heavy).
- Optional: pack crunchy topping separately to keep crisp.
- Label: “Stir well. Extra chili crisp optional.”

---

## Storage & Reheat
- Refrigerate up to 3 days.
- Reheat gently with a splash of milk.
- Stir well after reheating.

---

## Allergens
- Dairy (milk, cheese, butter)
- Wheat (pasta, flour)
- Soy (if chili crisp contains soy; check brand)
- Egg (some pasta)
- Sesame/peanut possible (brand dependent)
