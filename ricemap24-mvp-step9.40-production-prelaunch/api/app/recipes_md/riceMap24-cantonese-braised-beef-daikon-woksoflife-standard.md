# Cantonese Braised Beef Stew with Daikon (牛腩萝卜煲) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A homey Cantonese stew: **beef chunks** (rough flank / chuck) braised with warm spices and soy, then finished with tender daikon.  
Designed for home kitchens: **exact ingredients, clear timing, and delivery-friendly packaging**.

**Source recipe:** The Woks of Life — “Chinese Braised Beef Stew with Daikon” (Updated 11/3/2023)  
https://thewoksoflife.com/chinese-braised-beef-turnips/

---

## Summary
- **Cuisine:** Chinese (Cantonese)
- **Style:** Long braise + daikon finish
- **Yield:** **8 portions**
- **Active time:** ~20 min  
- **Total time:** ~3 hr 15 min
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (blanch + long simmer + optional thickening)

---

## Equipment
- Large pot (for blanching beef)
- Dutch oven / thick-bottomed pot with lid (6 L recommended)
- Colander/strainer
- Wooden spoon
- Measuring cups/spoons (scale optional)

---

## Ingredients (Serves 8) — from source
- **3 lb** beef rough flank, cut into **1½–2 inch (4–5 cm)** pieces *(or well-marbled chuck; may cook faster)*
- **2 Tbsp** vegetable oil
- **7 slices** ginger
- **5** cloves
- **3 pieces** star anise
- **3** bay leaves
- **1 cup** Shaoxing wine
- **3 Tbsp** light soy sauce
- **1 Tbsp** dark soy sauce
- **1 Tbsp** oyster sauce
- **5 cups** water
- **1 Tbsp** sugar
- **2 lb** daikon radish, peeled and cut into **1½–2 inch (4–5 cm)** pieces
- **Salt**, to taste
- **3** scallions, cut on a diagonal into **2-inch** pieces
- **1 Tbsp** cornstarch + **2 Tbsp** water (slurry, optional thickening)

---

## Prep (10–15 minutes)
1. **Cut beef:** 4–5 cm pieces.
2. **Cut daikon:** 4–5 cm chunks (bigger holds shape).
3. **Slice ginger:** 7 slices.
4. **Measure sauces/spices:** keep next to stove (this is a long braise—mise en place keeps it easy).

---

## Step-by-step Method (Precise, Easy-to-Follow)

## 1) Blanch beef (1 minute + rinse)
1. Add beef to a large pot and add enough water to cover.
2. Bring to a boil over high heat and boil **1 minute** (foam/scum rises).
3. Remove from heat, drain, and **rinse beef chunks clean**. Set aside.

> This step keeps the stew cleaner tasting.

---

## 2) Bloom aromatics (2–3 minutes)
1. Heat **2 Tbsp oil** in a Dutch oven over **medium** heat.
2. Add ginger, cloves, star anise, and bay leaves.
3. Cook **2–3 minutes** until fragrant.

---

## 3) Brown beef edges (8–10 minutes)
1. Increase heat to **high**.
2. Add blanched beef.
3. Cook **8–10 minutes**, stirring, until beef is lightly browned around the edges.

---

## 4) Season + braise (90 minutes to 2 hours)
1. Stir in:
   - **1 cup Shaoxing wine**
   - **light soy (3 Tbsp)**
   - **dark soy (1 Tbsp)**
   - **oyster sauce (1 Tbsp)**
2. Bring to a boil.
3. Once boiling, add **5 cups water** and **1 Tbsp sugar**.
4. Cover and bring back to a boil.
5. Reduce heat to **medium-low** and simmer **90 minutes to 2 hours** (slow bubble), stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

> Beef tenderness varies by cut. Check at 90 minutes; continue until beef is nearing tender.

---

## 5) Add daikon + finish (45 minutes)
1. Add daikon chunks and stir gently.
2. Continue simmering **45 minutes** until daikon is tender.

---

## 6) Final seasoning + scallions
1. Season with **salt to taste**.
2. Add scallions **just before serving**.

---

## Optional: Thicken sauce (glossy finish)
1. Mix **1 Tbsp cornstarch** with **2 Tbsp water** to make a slurry.
2. Pour into the bubbling sauce while stirring until slightly thick and glossy.
3. Add more slurry only if you want a thicker gravy.

---

## Quality Checks (Before Serving)
- **Beef:** Tender, not chewy (knife/fork meets little resistance).
- **Daikon:** Tender and slightly translucent, still holding shape.
- **Sauce:** Rich, aromatic; thickened only if desired.

---

## RiceMap24 Portioning (Per bowl)
- Beef + daikon + sauce: **350–450 g** per bowl (depending on price point)
- Rice: **250 g cooked** rice per bowl
- Optional side: stir-fried greens (bok choy/choy sum)

---

## Scaling for Production (10 portions)
Recipe yields 8. For ~10 bowls, multiply by **1.25×**.
- Beef: ~**1.9 kg**
- Daikon: ~**1.13 kg**
- Liquids/sauces/spices: 1.25×

---

## Packaging (To-Go) — RiceMap24 Recommended
- Stew in a leak-proof container.
- **Rice separate** (best texture + presentation).
- Note: “Reheat gently. Add a splash of water if sauce thickens too much.”

**Holding time:** Excellent; stew gets better after resting.

---

## Storage & Reheat
- Refrigerate up to **3 days**.
- Reheat gently to a simmer; stir and loosen with water if needed.

---

## Allergens & Dietary Notes
- **Contains:** Soy (soy sauces), oyster sauce (shellfish)
- **Gluten:** Likely (unless GF sauces used)
- **Alcohol:** Shaoxing wine (cooked down)
