# Bento Box (Asian Set Meal) — Choose Protein + 3 Sides — RiceMap24 Standard

A premium “set meal” Asian genre: **bento box** (organized lunch box with 1 main + rice + sides).  
Perfect for RiceMap24 because it looks professional, supports premium pricing, and scales with components.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Bento set meal (lunch box)
- **Cuisine vibe:** Japanese/Korean-inspired
- **Yield:** 4 bento boxes
- **Active time:** ~30 minutes
- **Total time:** ~40–50 minutes
- **Skill level:** Medium (component workflow)

---

## Why bento works for RiceMap24
- **Premium presentation** in compartments.
- **High perceived value** (multiple items).
- **Easy “banker” system:** rotate main + keep same sides.
- **Upsells:** extra protein, extra side cup, premium bento.

---

## Bento Structure (per box)
1) **Rice**  
2) **Main protein** (choose 1)  
3) **3 sides** (choose 3 from list)  
4) **Sauce cup** (optional)

---

## Ingredients (4 boxes)

## A) Rice
- Cooked rice: **250 g cooked per box** (1 kg total cooked)

---

## B) Choose Main Protein (pick ONE)

### Option 1: Teriyaki Chicken (banker)
- 700 g chicken thighs strips
- Teriyaki glaze: soy + honey + ginger + garlic + slurry

### Option 2: Bulgogi Beef (premium)
- 700 g thin beef strips + bulgogi marinade

### Option 3: Miso Salmon (premium)
- 700–800 g salmon + miso-butter glaze

---

## C) Choose 3 Sides (pick 3)
All are batch-friendly.

### Side 1: Spinach gomaae (sesame spinach)
- blanched spinach + sesame dressing

### Side 2: Quick cucumber pickles
- rice vinegar + sugar + salt

### Side 3: Do chua (carrot/daikon pickles)
- vinegar + sugar + salt

### Side 4: Edamame
- boiled + light salt

### Side 5: Mayak eggs (optional, not veg)
- soy-marinated eggs (premium)

### Side 6: Seaweed salad (if available)
- ready-made or quick wakame salad

---

## D) Sauce cups (1 per box)
Pick one:
- Yuzu mayo
- Gochujang mayo
- Soy-vinegar dip
- Chili oil cup

---

## Equipment
- Pots + skillet
- Bento containers (compartment)
- Sauce cups

---

## Step-by-Step Workflow (Fastest)

## 1) Start rice
Cook rice first (rice cooker is ideal).

## 2) Prep sides while rice cooks (15 minutes)
- Pickles (quick mix)
- Spinach (blanch + dress)
- Edamame (boil)
Keep everything dry-ish.

## 3) Cook main protein (10–12 minutes)
Use your chosen protein recipe (teriyaki/bulgogi/miso).

## 4) Assemble bento boxes (10 minutes)
Per box:
- Rice compartment
- Protein compartment
- 3 side compartments
- Sauce cup

---

## Portioning (Per box)
- Rice: 250 g cooked
- Protein: 160–200 g
- Each side: 50–80 g
- Sauce cup: 30–40 ml

---

## Batch for 10 bento boxes
- Rice: 2.5 kg cooked
- Protein: 1.8–2.0 kg
- Sides: multiply by 2.5×

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Delivery)
- Use tight-lid compartment containers.
- Keep sauce in cup.
- Label: “Mix sauce into protein as desired.”

---

## Storage
- Best same day.
- Pickles can be prepped 3–5 days ahead.

---

## Allergens
Varies by components:
- Soy, sesame, egg, fish, wheat possible
