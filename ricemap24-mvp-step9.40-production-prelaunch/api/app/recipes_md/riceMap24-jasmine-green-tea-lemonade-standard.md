# Jasmine Green Tea Lemonade — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Light, Food-Friendly)

A clean, refreshing drink: floral **jasmine green tea** + lemonade.  
Great for RiceMap24 because it’s easy to batch, pairs well with spicy/greasy foods, and feels premium.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Tea-lemonade
- **Profile:** jasmine + citrus
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2 minutes (if bases are ready)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Jasmine tea base (chilled)
- **180 ml** jasmine green tea (strong brew)

## B) Lemonade base
- **120 ml** lemonade (house base) OR lemon syrup + water

## C) Ice + finish
- **180–220 g** ice
- Optional garnish: mint, lemon wheel

---

## Sweetness Levels
- **Less sweet:** 90 ml lemonade + 210 ml tea
- **Classic:** 120 ml lemonade + 180 ml tea
- **Extra sweet:** 150 ml lemonade + 150 ml tea

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Fill cup with ice.
2. Pour jasmine tea + lemonade ratio.
3. Stir gently.
4. Garnish.

---

## Batch Setup (Recommended)

## A) Jasmine tea (for 10 drinks)
- **2 L** water (80°C)
- **20 g** jasmine green tea (or 10 bags)

Method:
1. Steep **3 minutes** (avoid bitterness).
2. Strain, cool fast, chill.

Portion: 180 ml per drink.

---

## B) Lemonade base (for 10 drinks)
- **1.2 L** lemonade (house)  
(Use your lemonade recipe or the one from Matcha Lemonade.)

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic)
- Jasmine tea: **1,800 ml**
- Lemonade: **1,200 ml**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Bottle without ice + separate ice cup = best quality.
- Or cup with ice for quick pickup.

**Label suggestion:** “Shake or stir. Keep chilled.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Jasmine tea: 2–3 days refrigerated
- Lemonade: 2–3 days refrigerated

---

## Allergens
None typical.
