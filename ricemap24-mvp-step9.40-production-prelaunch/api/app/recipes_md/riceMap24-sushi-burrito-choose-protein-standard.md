# Sushi Burrito (Nori Wrap) — Choose Protein + Crunch — RiceMap24 Standard

A premium handheld Asian genre: **sushi burrito** (a large nori-wrapped roll you eat like a burrito).  
High perceived value, strong photos, and easy “choose protein” menu logic.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Sushi burrito / nori wrap
- **Cuisine vibe:** Japanese-inspired (fusion)
- **Yield:** 4 sushi burritos
- **Active time:** ~25 minutes
- **Total time:** ~45–55 minutes (includes rice cooking/cooling)
- **Skill level:** Medium (rice + tight wrapping)

## Ingredients (4 burritos)

## A) Sushi rice
- **500 g** sushi rice (uncooked)
- Cook per rice cooker instructions

### Seasoning
- **80 ml** rice vinegar
- **35 g** sugar
- **1 tsp** salt

Mix and cool to room temp.

---

## B) Nori wrap
- **4 large** nori sheets (use full sheets)

---

## C) Choose Protein (pick ONE)
### Option 1: Spicy salmon (premium)
- 350–400 g raw salmon (sushi-grade) OR cooked salmon flakes if you prefer
- 2–3 Tbsp mayo + 1–2 tsp sriracha
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- Optional: sesame oil

### Option 2: Teriyaki chicken (cooked)
- 500 g teriyaki chicken strips (glossy, not watery)

### Option 3: Bulgogi beef (cooked)
- 500 g bulgogi beef, cooked fairly dry

---

## D) Veg + crunch (choose 3–4)
- Cucumber sticks
- Avocado slices
- Carrot sticks
- Pickled radish (danmuji) or pickled red onion
- Lettuce (optional)

### Crunch premium (choose 1)
- Tempura flakes (tenkasu)
- Crispy fried onions
- Crispy wonton strips
- Toasted sesame

---

## E) Sauces (control moisture)
### Spicy mayo (classic)
- 120 ml mayo + sriracha + pinch salt

### Soy dip cup (optional)
- soy + a touch of rice vinegar

---

## Equipment
- Rice cooker/pot
- Rolling mat (optional but helpful)
- Sharp knife
- Parchment + foil for wrapping

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Cook and season rice (35–40 minutes)
1. Rinse rice until water runs mostly clear.
2. Cook and rest 10 minutes.
3. Dissolve sugar + salt in vinegar.
4. Fold seasoning into rice gently; cool to room temp.

---

## 2) Prep fillings (10 minutes)
- Prepare chosen protein.
- Cut veg into long sticks.
- Keep ingredients dry (critical).

---

## 3) Assemble burritos (5–8 minutes)
Per burrito:
1. Place nori on a mat or board (shiny side down).
2. Spread rice in an even layer, leaving 3 cm at top edge.
3. Add a thin line of sauce (don’t overdo).
4. Add protein + veg + crunch in a tight line across the lower third.
5. Roll tightly, tucking as you roll.
6. Seal with a little water at edge.

---

## 4) Wrap for pickup
- Wrap tightly in parchment + foil to hold shape.
- Cut in half at an angle for premium presentation (optional).

---

## Portioning (Per burrito)
- Rice: 180–220 g cooked
- Protein: 120–160 g
- Veg: generous
- Sauce: 15–25 g max inside (offer extra cup)

---

## Batch for 10 burritos
- Rice: 1.25 kg uncooked
- Nori: 10 sheets
- Protein: 1.3–1.6 kg
- Veg/crunch: 2.5×

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Delivery)
- Burrito wrapped tight in foil/paper.
- Optional: extra sauce cup (30–40 ml).
- Keep chilled if using raw fish.

**Label:** “Keep chilled. Eat same day.”

---

## Food safety note (important)
If you use raw salmon:
- Use sushi-grade fish and strict cold chain.
- Consider offering **cooked salmon** as default for home kitchens.

---

## Allergens
- Fish (salmon)
- Egg (mayo)
- Soy (soy sauce)
- Sesame optional
- Wheat possible (some crunch items)
