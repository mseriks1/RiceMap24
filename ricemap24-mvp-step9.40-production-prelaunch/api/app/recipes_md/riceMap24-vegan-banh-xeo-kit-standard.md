# Vegan Bánh Xèo (Vietnamese Sizzling Turmeric Crepes) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card (Vegetarian, Non-Indian)

A premium vegetarian “wow” dish: crispy turmeric crepes filled with mushrooms + bean sprouts, eaten by wrapping in lettuce/herbs and dipping.  
For RiceMap24, the best-selling format is a **KIT** (crepes + greens + sauce cups) so it stays crisp and looks premium.

**Reference (for grounding):** The Viet Vegan — Vegan Bánh Xèo  
https://thevietvegan.com/vegan-banh-xeo/

---

## Summary
- **Cuisine:** Vietnamese
- **Type:** Vegetarian main (vegan)
- **Yield:** 6–8 crepes (2–4 portions depending on size)
- **Active time:** ~25 minutes
- **Total time:** ~45–60 minutes
- **Skill level:** Medium (pan technique for crisp crepe)

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Premium “experience” dish:** crispy crepe + wrap + dip.
- **Photogenic:** bright yellow crepe + greens + sauce.
- **Pickup-friendly:** sell as a kit; keeps crisp.
- **Upsells:** extra crepe, extra sauce, add tofu, add pickles.

---

## What Bánh Xèo needs to feel authentic
- Turmeric-yellow batter
- Coconut milk aroma
- Crispy edges
- Fresh lettuce + herbs
- Dipping sauce (vegan nuoc cham style)

---

## Ingredients

## A) Batter (for 6–8 crepes)
- **250 g** rice flour
- **60 g** cornstarch (helps crispness)
- **1 tsp** turmeric powder
- **1/2 tsp** salt
- **400 ml** coconut milk
- **450–500 ml** water (adjust to thin batter)
- **1 scallion**, thinly sliced
- Optional: 1/2 tsp sugar (balances)

**Batter texture:** thin like crepe batter, not thick.

---

## B) Filling (vegan premium)
- **350–450 g** mushrooms (shiitake + oyster mix), sliced
- **200 g** firm tofu, sliced or cubes (optional but great)
- **250–300 g** bean sprouts
- **1 small onion**, thin slices
- 1–2 Tbsp neutral oil
- Pinch salt + black pepper

## C) Greens + herbs (kit essentials)
- Lettuce leaves (large, soft)
- Mint + Thai basil + cilantro (mix)
- Cucumber sticks (optional but premium)
- Pickles (do chua) optional but very good

## D) Vegan dipping sauce (“nuoc cham” style)
- **90 ml** soy sauce *(or vegan fish sauce)*
- **120 ml** water
- **35–45 g** sugar
- **2 Tbsp** lime juice
- **1 clove** garlic, minced
- Chili slices (optional)

**Taste target:** sweet-salty-tangy.

---

## Equipment
- Nonstick or seasoned skillet (28–30 cm ideal)
- Ladle (for batter)
- Spatula
- Mixing bowl (batter)
- Sauce cups + kit containers

---

## Prep (10–15 minutes)
1. Mix batter ingredients until smooth; rest **10 minutes** (helps hydration).
2. Slice mushrooms/onion; prep tofu if using.
3. Wash and dry lettuce/herbs well (dry greens = premium).
4. Mix dipping sauce; portion into cups.

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## Step 1 — Cook filling (5–7 minutes)
1. Heat 1 Tbsp oil in a pan.
2. Cook onion + mushrooms until softened and browned slightly.
3. Add tofu (optional) and season with salt/pepper.
4. Remove to a bowl.
5. Keep bean sprouts raw (they wilt inside crepe) OR briefly stir 30 seconds.

---

## Step 2 — Cook crepes (core technique) (3–5 minutes each)
For each crepe:
1. Heat skillet medium-high, add **1–2 tsp oil**.
2. Stir batter (it separates), then ladle in a thin layer.
3. Swirl pan quickly to spread.
4. Sprinkle scallions (if not already in batter).
5. Add a handful of filling + bean sprouts to one side.
6. Cover **60–90 seconds** to steam slightly.
7. Remove lid and let cook until edges are crisp and lifted.
8. Fold in half.

**Crisp rule:** finish uncovered so moisture evaporates.

---

## Quality Checks
- Crepe is crisp at edges, not rubbery.
- Batter is thin and cooks quickly.
- Greens are dry and fresh.
- Sauce is balanced.

---

## RiceMap24 KIT Packaging (best practice)
Per 2-person kit:
- **Container A:** 2–3 crepes (keep flat, vent briefly before closing)
- **Container B:** lettuce + herb mix (dry, chilled)
- **Cup C:** dipping sauce (60 ml)
- **Cup D (optional):** pickles (do chua)
- Optional: “how to eat” card

**Label:** “Wrap pieces of crepe in lettuce/herbs and dip.”

---

## Portioning
- 1 portion = 2 crepes + greens + sauce
- Premium upsell = 3 crepes + extra sauce cup

---

## Scaling (10 portions as kits)
- Batter: multiply by 2.5×
- Mushrooms: 1.0–1.2 kg
- Sprouts: 700–800 g
Cook crepes to order for best crisp, or cook shortly before pickup and vent containers.

---

## Storage & Reheat
- Best fresh.
- Re-crisp crepes in a dry skillet 1–2 minutes per side.
- Keep greens chilled and dry.

---

## Allergens
- Soy (soy sauce, tofu)
- Coconut (coconut milk)
- Gluten-free base (rice flour/cornstarch) but check soy sauce gluten.
