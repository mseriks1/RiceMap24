BriteSight Video Editor step31.7 — Safari preview + gapless handoff fix

Changes:
- More robust Safari preview initialization for imported videos.
- Adds poster/thumbnail fallback for video preview before Safari has painted the first decoded frame.
- Forces a preview re-render after window load to avoid an empty initial preview frame.
- Adds CSS aspect-ratio fallback for Safari/WebKit edge cases.
- Starts preloading/prestarting the next video earlier before clip end.
- Keeps the outgoing video frame alive briefly during handoff if the next clip is not already prestarted, reducing black/frozen gaps between clips in Safari.
- Keeps the previous dual-video handoff logic, but avoids pausing the old video too early on Safari.
