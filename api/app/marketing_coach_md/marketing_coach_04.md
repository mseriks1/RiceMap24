# Marketing Coach – Month 4
## Focus: Efficient social media marketing using video

This month we focus on **efficient social media marketing using video**. You use video as the main tool in social media, but you do it smart: you reuse what you have already filmed, cut it into different lengths, and post the same video on multiple platforms. The result is more visibility and more effect — without you having to create new content all the time.

### Natural transition from Month 3
In Month 3 you learned why video builds trust, and you built a small video library. Now it’s about using video more strategically in social media: you get more out of each video by reusing it correctly.

### Why video is especially important in social media marketing (for food)
Video is effective because it:
- makes people stop (scroll-stopper)
- gives a feeling of authenticity (trust)
- shows the result and portion quickly (clarity)

In food, this is especially powerful: movement, texture, and “freshness” makes people hungry and curious.

### What “efficient” means in practice (for a home kitchen)
You have limited time and capacity. Efficient marketing therefore means:
- high effect per minute you spend
- a rhythm you can maintain every week
- a system that doesn’t collapse when you have a lot of orders

### How reuse makes you more efficient (without seeming “spam”)
Reuse means you use the same footage in multiple ways:
- the same video on TikTok + Reels + Shorts
- short (6–9 sec) and medium (12–18 sec) versions of the same material
- you can post the same video again later with new text/new angle

This is smart because people often need to see you several times before they order. Reuse creates more “touchpoints” without you working more.

### Goals this month
1) **Build a base video** that can be cut into multiple variations  
2) **Publish 2 videos** total (Week 3 and Week 4)  
3) **Keep photo posting stable** (at least 1 per week)  
4) **Save time** by using the same content across platforms

### Minimum vs Bonus
**Minimum:**
- 1 photo post per week (FB/IG)
- 2 published videos total (Week 3 + 4)
- Film 4–6 new clips for the library

**Bonus:**
- 2 photo posts per week
- 3 published videos (add Week 2)
- Film 10–15 new clips

## Week 2 — Make a base video you can reuse
### Mini lesson (a base makes everything easier)
When you have one base video, you can cut it shorter or longer without starting over.

### Tasks

2) Film 1–2 proof clips (packing/ready for pickup)

## Week 4 — Publish Video #2 (medium/long-ish) + reuse smarter
