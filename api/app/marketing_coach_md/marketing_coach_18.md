# Marketing Coach – Month 18  
## Focus: Creative new content (without becoming “the same marketing all the time”)

This month we focus on creating **novelty and variation** in your marketing, so the content feels fresh — even if, in practice, you do many of the same things (post, deliver, repeat). As a home kitchen business grows, a common challenge is that marketing becomes a bit “the same”: the same type of photo, the same type of copy, the same type of video. That can work for a while, but over time you need more creativity and more variation to:
- capture attention again
- give new reasons to order
- make you more memorable locally

The goal is still not to become an “influencer.” The goal is to become better at telling the story of your food in new ways.

---

## Why this focus now
You already have:
- routine (2 photos + 2 videos per week)
- library
- menu logic (bundles, cold-ready, finish-at-home)
- offline experience and network

Now the timing is right to develop your “marketing language”: new angles and new formats give you more effect from what you already do. You don’t need to post more — you need smarter variation.

---

## This month’s strategy (3 steps)
1) Build variation with **new angles** (same dish, new story)  
2) Use **format variation** (same content, different presentation)  
3) Create a “creative bank” you can reuse later

---

## Goals this month
1) Create at least **12 new angles** (creative bank) you can use later  
2) Test at least **4 new post formats** (1 per week)  
3) Keep the routine: 2 photo posts + 2 videos per week  
4) Do 2 offline actions (Week 2 and Week 4)

---

## Fixed posting routine (baseline this month)
Every week:
- 2 photo posts (FB + IG, same content)  
- 2 videos (TikTok + Reels + Shorts where you can)  
- Offline actions: Week 2 and Week 4 (2 total)

---

## Progress in the dashboard (two colors)
- **Creative bank (60%)**: 12 angles created + saved  
- **Execution (40%)**: weekly routine completed + new formats tested  

---

# Part A: “Creative bank” — 12 angles you can reuse (template)
You create 12 angles this month. These are not full posts. They are short ideas you can reuse later.

Use this template per angle:
- Dish: [name]
- Angle: [the story/purpose]
- Hook sentence: [one line]
- CTA: “Menu + ordering: [link]”

### Examples of angles (you can adapt)
- “For 2” angle (sharing)
- “Lunch” angle (easy weekday)
- “For later” angle (cold-ready)
- “Restaurant at home” angle (finish-at-home)
- “Best seller is back”
- “Limited batch”
- “Customer favorite”
- “Behind the scenes”
- “Ingredients story”
- “Culture/pride”
- “Before/after”
- “Pack with me”

---

# Part B: 4 new post formats (one per week)
You test one new format each week. Keep it simple.

Examples of formats:
1) **Carousel** (3–5 photos) — story in steps  
2) **Before/after** — ingredients → finished dish  
3) **Voice-over or text overlay** — “this is how ordering works”  
4) **Mini-series** — “Dish of the week” repeated weekly
