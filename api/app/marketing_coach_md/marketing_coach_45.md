# Marketing Coach – Month 45  
## Focus: Measurability + planning (make marketing smarter over time)

This month you build something that makes you stronger every month: **measurability**. Many local businesses market blindly. The ones who grow faster build a simple measurement habit so they can:
- stop wasting effort on channels that don’t work
- double down on channels that do work
- plan with more confidence

The point is not “big analytics.” The point is a small habit:
- ask
- note
- learn
- repeat what works

Early important term:  
CTA = Call To Action (what you want the customer to do now). Use one CTA per post/message, always the same:  
**“Menu and ordering: [link]”**

⸻

## What measurability means (simple)
Measurability means you make it possible to answer:
- Where do customers actually come from?
- Which actions create orders?
- Which actions create repeat?

You do this with one simple question:
**“How did you hear about us?”**

And you record the answer in a simple place.

⸻

## Why this matters now
At this stage you already do a lot:
- online posting
- offline placements
- campaigns
- referrals
- VIP
- customer list follow-up

Without measurement, you can’t know what to focus on next. With measurement, your marketing becomes calmer and more effective.

Measurability is how you turn effort into strategy.

⸻

## This month’s strategy (4 steps)
1) Create a simple tracking system (buckets/categories)  
2) Add the “how did you hear” question into your workflow  
3) Review the data weekly (light)  
4) Build a 3-month plan based on what worked

⸻

## Goals this month
1) You track at least **20 customer discovery data points** (or as many as you can)  
2) You categorize sources into simple buckets  
3) You identify your top 3 channels  
4) You make a 3-month plan based on the winners  
5) You keep routine: 2 photo posts + 2 videos per week

⸻

## Fixed routine (baseline this month)
- 2 photo posts per week (FB + IG)  
- 2 videos per week (TikTok + Reels + Shorts)  
- Tracking habit: ask and note for every new customer  
- Weekly review (10 minutes)

Offline:
- optional this month (only if it fits)

⸻

## Progress in the dashboard (two colors)
- **Tracking system (60%)**: question + buckets + notes habit active  
- **Planning (40%)**: insights + 3-month plan created  

⸻

# Part A: Your tracking buckets (simple categories)
Create categories that cover most sources:

- Poster location (name)  
- Local Facebook group (name)  
- Friend referral (name)  
- Instagram/Facebook  
- TikTok/Reels  
- Coworking/office  
- Event/meeting  
- Other  

**Rule:** keep it simple. You can add detail later.

⸻

# Part B: Where to record (simple)
Choose one place:
- phone notes
- spreadsheet
- customer list notes

Record:
- date
- source bucket
- optional: specific location/group name
- optional: order size (small/medium/large)

⸻

# Part C: The standard question (script)
Use one line:
> Quick question 😊 How did you hear about us?

If you need it in written form:
> Thanks for your order! 😊 Quick question: How did you hear about us?

⸻

# Weekly plan (4 weeks)

## Week 1 — Set up the system (question + buckets + note place)
### Mini lesson (one question creates clarity)
You don’t need big tools. You need one habit.

### Tasks
1) Define your buckets  
2) Choose where you record (notes/spreadsheet)  
3) Start asking the question for every new customer

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now you can measure marketing without complexity.”
- “This turns effort into learning.”

---

## Week 2 — Collect data (ask consistently)
### Mini lesson (asking is a habit, not a special event)
Even 10–20 answers reveal patterns.

### Tasks
1) Ask every new customer  
2) Record 5–10 data points this week  
3) Keep routine: 2 photos + 2 videos

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now you are gathering real growth insights.”
- “This makes future marketing smarter.”

---

## Week 3 — Review the data and identify top channels
### Mini lesson (patterns appear quickly)
Even small datasets reveal your winners.

### Tasks
1) Count occurrences per bucket  
2) Identify top 3 channels  
3) Note any surprise winners

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now you know what actually works.”
- “This reduces guesswork.”

---

## Week 4 — Build a 3-month plan based on the winners
### Mini lesson (planning is easier when data guides you)
A simple plan is enough.

### Tasks
1) Write your 3-month plan:
- channel #1: frequency
- channel #2: frequency
- channel #3: frequency
- one small test channel

2) Decide what you stop/reduce:
- low-performing channels

✅ Done confirmations:
- “Now you have a data-driven growth plan.”
- “This makes marketing calmer and more effective.”

---

## Month 45 done: you’re “ready”
You’re ready when:
- you tracked customer discovery sources ✅  
- you identified top 3 channels ✅  
- you made a 3-month plan based on real results ✅  
- you maintained the 2 photo posts + 2 videos routine ✅
