# Miso-Butter Salmon Wrap (Yuzu Mayo + Cucumber Pickles) — RiceMap24 Standard Wrap Recipe

A premium salmon wrap with a clear Asian pantry twist: **miso-butter glazed salmon**, **yuzu/lime mayo**, and crisp cucumber pickles.  
Designed for lunch sales and pickup quality (sauce + pickles can be packed smartly).

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Wrap (Asian-inspired, premium)
- **Cuisine vibe:** Japanese pantry (miso + yuzu)
- **Yield:** 4 large wraps
- **Active time:** ~20 minutes
- **Total time:** ~25–30 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (don’t overcook salmon)

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Premium signal:** salmon + glossy miso glaze
- **Photogenic:** orange salmon + green crunch + sesame
- **Pickup friendly:** sauce cup + pickle cup option
- **Upsells:** extra salmon, extra yuzu mayo cup, extra glaze cup, add avocado

---

## Ingredients (4 wraps)

## A) Salmon
- **700–800 g** salmon fillet, cut into 4 portions (or bite-size pieces for easier wrapping)
- Salt + black pepper
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil

## B) Miso-butter glaze (the identity)
- **60 g** unsalted butter, softened
- **2 Tbsp** white miso
- **1 Tbsp** honey (or brown sugar)
- **1 Tbsp** soy sauce
- **1 tsp** rice vinegar (or lemon)
- Optional: **1 tsp** grated ginger
- Optional: **1 tsp** sesame oil (tiny finish)

## C) Yuzu/Lime mayo
- **120 ml** mayo
- **1–2 tsp** yuzu juice *(or 1 tsp yuzu marmalade)*  
  Substitute: lime juice
- Pinch salt

## D) Cucumber quick pickles (crunch)
- **1 cucumber**, thin slices or sticks
- **2 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 tsp** sugar
- **1/2 tsp** salt
- Optional: chili flakes

## E) Wrap build
- **4 large** tortillas/wraps
- Lettuce leaves (recommended moisture barrier)
- Optional: pickled red onion (adds color)
- Finish: toasted sesame seeds + scallions
- Optional: nori strips (premium crunch)

---

## Equipment
- Skillet (or oven)
- Mixing bowls
- Knife + cutting board
- Sauce cups (recommended)

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Make cucumber pickles (5 minutes)
1. Mix vinegar, sugar, salt until dissolved.
2. Toss cucumber and set aside while cooking salmon.

Drain lightly before using (avoid soggy wrap).

---

## 2) Mix miso-butter glaze + yuzu mayo (2 minutes)
- Mix glaze ingredients into a smooth paste.
- Mix yuzu mayo.

---

## 3) Cook salmon (8–10 minutes)
### Skillet (fast)
1. Pat salmon dry; season.
2. Heat skillet medium-high with oil.
3. Sear salmon 3–4 minutes; flip 2–3 minutes until almost done.

**Doneness target:** 52–55°C for juicy salmon (cook slightly longer if preferred).

---

## 4) Glaze salmon (1 minute)
1. Lower heat to low.
2. Add miso-butter to pan.
3. Spoon glaze over salmon 30–60 seconds until glossy.

Optional: break salmon into chunks for easier wrap bites.

---

## 5) Assemble wraps (RiceMap24 build)
Per wrap:
1. Warm tortilla briefly.
2. Add lettuce barrier.
3. Spread **1–2 Tbsp** yuzu mayo.
4. Add salmon (about **170–200 g cooked**).
5. Add drained cucumber pickles (+ optional pickled onion).
6. Finish with sesame + scallions (+ optional nori).
7. Roll tight.

---

## Portioning (Per wrap)
- Salmon: **170–200 g cooked**
- Mayo: **25–30 g**
- Pickles: **40–60 g**
- Optional garnish: sesame/nori

---

## Batch for 10 wraps
- Salmon: **1.8–2.0 kg**
- Glaze: 2.5×
- Yuzu mayo: 300 ml mayo + citrus to taste
- Cucumbers: 3–4

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Delivery)
Best practice:
- Wrap A: tortilla + lettuce + mayo + salmon (rolled tight)
- Cup B: cucumber pickles (customer adds) for max crunch
- Optional cups: extra yuzu mayo / extra glaze

**Label:** “Add pickles last. Stir sauce if included.”

---

## Storage
- Salmon best same day (reheat gently if needed).
- Glaze can be made ahead (3 days refrigerated).
- Pickles keep 2 days chilled.

---

## Allergens
- Fish (salmon)
- Soy (miso, soy sauce)
- Dairy (butter)
- Egg (mayo)
- Wheat (tortilla)
- Sesame if used
- Gluten possible (soy sauce brand)
