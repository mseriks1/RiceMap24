# Pad Kra Pao (Thai Holy Basil Stir-Fry) — Choose Your Protein — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A Thai street-food staple: garlic + chilies + savory sauce + holy basil, served over rice (often with a fried egg).  
This is perfect for RiceMap24/home kitchens because it uses **one master sauce** and works with **any protein**.

**Primary source recipe:** Hot Thai Kitchen — “The Only PAD KRA PAO Recipe You Need (Pad Kra Pao Anything)”  
https://hot-thai-kitchen.com/pad-kra-pao-anything/

---

## Summary
- **Cuisine:** Thai
- **Style:** Fast wok stir-fry
- **Yield:** 4 portions
- **Active time:** ~15 min
- **Total time:** ~20 min
- **Skill level:** Easy (timing + heat control)

---

## Choose Your Protein (Pick ONE)
Use **~500 g total protein** for 4 portions (≈ 125 g per bowl).

### Option A — Ground pork (classic)
- 500 g ground pork

### Option B — Ground chicken
- 500 g ground chicken (thigh preferred)

### Option C — Ground beef
- 500 g ground beef (15–20% fat is best)

### Option D — Sliced chicken or beef (quick “wok slices”)
- 500 g thin-sliced chicken thigh or beef sirloin/flank  
  *(Slice 3–4 mm thin across the grain.)*

### Option E — Shrimp
- 500 g raw shrimp, peeled and chopped (or keep whole small shrimp)

### Option F — Tofu (vegetarian)
- 500 g firm tofu, pressed and crumbled (or small cubes)

> Keep protein weight consistent so your menu pricing is predictable.

---

## Key Aromatics + Basil (non-negotiable)
- Garlic (minced)
- Thai bird’s eye chilies (or other hot chili), minced/sliced
- **Holy basil** (ideal). If unavailable: Thai basil or sweet basil works (flavor changes slightly).

---

## Master Sauce (Mix once, use every time)
This is the **RiceMap24 service hack**: pre-mix sauce and portion it.

**Master Sauce (for 4 portions)**
- Fish sauce
- Light soy sauce
- Dark soy sauce (small amount for color)
- Oyster sauce
- Sugar

> Use Hot Thai Kitchen’s exact measurements from the source link above.  
> Then portion into **4 cups** (1 cup per bowl) or **2 cups** (2 bowls per wok batch).

---

## Add-on (Recommended)
- Fried eggs (one per bowl) — very popular pad kra pao style

## Equipment
- Wok or large skillet
- Spatula
- Small bowl (sauce)
- Knife + cutting board

- Optional: scale for consistent portions

---

## Prep (10 minutes)
1. **Mix Master Sauce** (exact amounts from source). Portion into cups.
2. **Mince garlic + chilies** (wear gloves if needed).
3. **Basil:** pick leaves (keep whole).
4. **Protein prep:**
   - Ground meat: ready as-is.
   - Sliced meat: slice thin and keep dry (pat with towel).
   - Shrimp: chop or keep small whole.
   - Tofu: press, crumble/cube.

---

## Cooking Method (Repeatable Workflow)

> Best practice: cook **2 bowls at a time** in one wok batch for heat control.

### 1) Aromatics first (15–30 seconds)
1. Heat wok over **high heat** until very hot.
2. Add a small amount of oil.
3. Add garlic + chilies. Stir quickly until fragrant—do not burn.

### 2) Cook protein (timing depends)
**Ground meat**
1. Add ground meat.
2. Stir-fry until no pink remains and it starts to brown.

**Sliced meat**
1. Add slices in a single layer.
2. Sear quickly, then toss until mostly cooked (remove early if needed).

**Shrimp**
1. Add shrimp and toss just until opaque/pink (fast).

**Tofu**
1. Stir-fry until hot and slightly dried, or sear cubes until edges lightly golden.

### 3) Add Master Sauce (30–60 seconds)
1. Pour in sauce (1–2 portion amount depending on your batch).
2. Toss until sauce bubbles and coats protein.

### 4) Basil last (10–15 seconds)
1. Add holy basil leaves.
2. Stir just until wilted and aromatic.
3. Immediately remove from heat.

## RiceMap24 Bowl Assembly
- Rice base: **250 g cooked** per bowl
- Pad kra pao topping: **250–300 g** per bowl (depends on your pricing)
- Optional: fried egg on top

**Customer note:** “Spicy. Add egg for the classic style.”

---

## Quality Checks
- **Aroma:** Garlic + chili + basil burst (basil must be added at the end).
- **Sauce:** Coats protein; not watery.
- **Heat level:** controllable by chili amount.

---

## Scaling for Production (10 bowls)
- Multiply protein to **1.25 kg**
- Make 2.5× master sauce (from the Hot Thai Kitchen amounts)
- Pre-portion sauce into 10 cups for fast service

---

## Packaging (To-Go) — RiceMap24 Recommended
- Pad kra pao topping in one container.
- Rice **separate** (better texture).
- Egg: best fresh; if included, pack on top and vent container briefly before sealing.

**Holding time:** Best within 1–2 hours.

---

## Allergen Notes
- Fish sauce (fish), oyster sauce (shellfish), soy sauces (soy/wheat).  
- Vegetarian version: use vegan “oyster” sauce and soy-based alternatives.
