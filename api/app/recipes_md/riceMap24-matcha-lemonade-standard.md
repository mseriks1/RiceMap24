# Matcha Lemonade — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Asian Twist, Pickup-Friendly)

A modern café hit: bright lemonade with a clean **matcha** layer.  
Great for RiceMap24 because it’s visually striking, refreshing, and easy to batch (lemonade base + matcha concentrate).

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Lemonade with matcha
- **Asian twist:** Matcha (Japanese green tea)
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2–3 minutes (if bases are ready)
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (avoid matcha clumps)

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Lemonade base
Choose one:
- **220 ml** lemonade (house base, chilled)  
OR
- **200 ml** cold water + **30 ml** lemon syrup

## B) Matcha layer
- **2 tsp** matcha powder (culinary)
- **60 ml** cold water (or cold milk for creamier version)
- Optional: 5 ml simple syrup if matcha is bitter

## C) Ice + finish
- **180–220 g** ice
- Optional garnish: lemon wheel, mint

---

## Sweetness Levels (menu toggles)
- **Less sweet:** 15 ml syrup per drink
- **Classic:** 25 ml syrup per drink
- **Extra sweet:** 35 ml syrup per drink

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)

### 1) Make matcha (smooth, no clumps)
1. Sift matcha into a small cup.
2. Add 2 Tbsp water and whisk into a paste.
3. Add remaining water and whisk smooth.

### 2) Build drink
1. Fill serving cup with ice.
2. Pour chilled lemonade.
3. Slowly pour matcha over the back of a spoon for a layered look (optional).
4. Stir before drinking.

---

## Batch Setup (Recommended)

## A) Lemonade base (for 10 drinks)
Target: bright but not harsh.
- **1.8 L** cold water
- **250 ml** lemon juice
- **250 g** sugar
- Optional: pinch salt

Method:
1. Dissolve sugar in a little warm water, then mix with cold water + lemon.
2. Chill.

Portion: **220 ml per drink**.

---

## B) Matcha concentrate (for speed)
- **40 g** matcha
- **600 ml** cold water

Method:
1. Sift matcha.
2. Whisk into paste with a little water, then whisk in the rest.
3. Chill. Shake before use.

Portion: **60 ml per drink**.

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic)
- Lemonade base: **2,200 ml**
- Matcha concentrate: **600 ml**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Bottle or cup with tight lid.
- If bottled, customer shakes to combine.
- Ice separate if pickup time is long.

**Label suggestion:** “Shake before drinking.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Lemonade base: 2–3 days refrigerated
- Matcha concentrate: best within 24–48 hours (color is brightest early)

---

## Allergens
None typical (unless you use milk).
