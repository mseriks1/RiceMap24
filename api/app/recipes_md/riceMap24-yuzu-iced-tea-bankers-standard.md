# Yuzu Iced Tea — RiceMap24 “Banker Drink” Standard

A high-performing, premium-feeling iced tea: black tea + **yuzu citrus** + honey/sugar.  
Perfect for RiceMap24 because it’s **easy to batch**, **looks fresh**, and pairs with almost any food.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Iced tea (citrus)
- **Asian twist:** Yuzu (Japanese citrus)
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 3 minutes (if tea is pre-brewed)
- **Total time:** 5 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this is a RiceMap24 “Banker”
- **Broad appeal:** like lemonade + tea, but more premium.
- **Fast to serve:** pour + ice + garnish.
- **Batch-friendly:** brew tea and yuzu syrup ahead.
- **Upsells:** sparkling version, extra yuzu, less sweet, mint.

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Tea base
- **200 ml** strong black tea (chilled)  
  (English Breakfast, Assam, Ceylon)

## B) Yuzu citrus
Choose one:
- **20–25 ml** yuzu juice  
  OR
- **1.5 Tbsp** yuzu marmalade (easier to source)

## C) Sweetener (choose)
- **10–15 ml** honey syrup (1:1 honey + warm water)  
  OR
- **15–20 ml** simple syrup (1:1 sugar + water)

## D) Ice + finish
- **180–220 g** ice
- Optional garnish: lemon wheel, mint, yuzu peel, or a thin orange slice

---

## Sweetness Levels (menu toggles)
- **Less sweet:** 10 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 15–20 ml syrup
- **Extra sweet:** 25 ml syrup

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)

### 1) Build in cup
1. Add yuzu juice (or marmalade) to the cup.
2. Add syrup and stir briefly.

### 2) Add tea + ice
1. Add chilled tea.
2. Fill with ice.
3. Stir 10 seconds.

### 3) Taste check
- If too sharp → add 5 ml syrup.
- If too sweet → add a splash more tea or water.

---

## Batch Setup (Recommended for Home Kitchens)

## A) Brew tea concentrate (for 10 drinks)
You want it strong so it doesn’t taste watery over ice.
- **2 L** water
- **20 g** black tea (or ~10 tea bags)

Method:
1. Steep **5 minutes** (don’t oversteep; bitterness).
2. Remove tea.
3. Cool quickly and refrigerate.

Portion per drink: **200 ml**.

---

## B) Yuzu syrup (best for speed + consistency)
### If you have yuzu juice
- **250 ml** yuzu juice
- **250 g** sugar
- **250 ml** hot water

Stir until dissolved. Chill.

### If you have yuzu marmalade (easiest)
- **300 g** yuzu marmalade
- **300 ml** hot water
Stir until smooth. Strain if you want it ultra-smooth.

Portion per drink: **30–35 ml** syrup (depending on sweetness).

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic sweetness)
- Chilled tea: **2,000 ml**
- Yuzu syrup: **300–350 ml**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**

---

## Sparkling Upsell (very recommended)
Replace half the tea with sparkling water:
- 120 ml tea + 120 ml sparkling water  
Add ice and serve immediately.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
### Best options
1) **Bottle (no ice) + separate ice cup** (best quality if pickup time is long)
2) **Cup with ice** (best for immediate pickup)

**Label suggestion:** “Shake or stir. Add ice if included.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Brewed tea: **2–3 days** refrigerated
- Yuzu syrup: **7 days** refrigerated

---

## Allergens
None typical (check marmalade ingredients).
