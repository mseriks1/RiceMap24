# Teriyaki Chicken Wrap (Sesame Crunch + Cucumber) — RiceMap24 Standard Wrap Recipe

A premium, broad-appeal wrap: glossy teriyaki chicken, yuzu/lime mayo, cucumber crunch, and sesame finish.  
Fusion (not traditional), designed for pickup/delivery with smart moisture control.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Wrap (Asian-inspired)
- **Cuisine vibe:** Japanese teriyaki profile
- **Yield:** 4 large wraps
- **Active time:** ~15–20 minutes
- **Total time:** ~25–30 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Universal favorite:** teriyaki chicken sells everywhere.
- **Premium look:** glossy chicken + fresh green crunch.
- **Delivery friendly:** sauce clings; veg can be packed separate if needed.
- **Upsells:** extra chicken, extra teriyaki cup, add avocado, add nori crunch.

---

## Ingredients (4 wraps)

## A) Chicken
- **700 g** boneless chicken thighs (juicier) or breast (lean), sliced strips
- Salt + pepper
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil

## B) Quick teriyaki glaze (glossy)
- **90 ml** soy sauce
- **60 ml** water
- **2 Tbsp** honey (or brown sugar)
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 tsp** grated ginger
- **1 clove** garlic, grated
- **1 tsp** cornstarch + **1 Tbsp** water (slurry)

## C) Yuzu/Lime mayo (premium)
- **120 ml** mayo
- **1–2 tsp** yuzu juice *(or 1 tsp yuzu marmalade)*  
  Substitute: lime juice
- **1 tsp** rice vinegar (optional)
- Pinch salt

## D) Crunch + greens
- Lettuce leaves (recommended moisture barrier)
- **1 cucumber**, thin sticks/ribbons
- Optional: shredded cabbage (adds volume)
- Optional: pickled red onion (nice contrast)

## E) Wrap + finish
- **4 large** tortillas/wraps
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Sliced scallions (optional)
- Optional: nori strips (premium)

---

## Equipment
- Skillet
- Small bowl (slurry)
- Knife + cutting board
- Mixing bowl (mayo)

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Mix yuzu/lime mayo (1 minute)
- Stir mayo + yuzu/lime + (vinegar) + pinch salt.

---

## 2) Cook chicken (8–10 minutes)
1. Heat skillet medium-high with oil.
2. Add chicken, season lightly, sear until browned and cooked through.
3. Reduce heat slightly.

---

## 3) Add teriyaki glaze (2 minutes)
1. Mix soy, water, honey, vinegar, ginger, garlic.
2. Pour into pan and simmer 1 minute.
3. Add slurry and stir until glossy and clingy.
4. Remove from heat.

**Quality target:** sticky glaze, not watery.

---

## 4) Assemble wraps (RiceMap24 build)
Per wrap:
1. Warm tortilla briefly (prevents tearing).
2. Add lettuce barrier.
3. Spread **1–2 Tbsp** yuzu mayo.
4. Add teriyaki chicken (about **160–190 g cooked**).
5. Add cucumber crunch (and optional cabbage/pickles).
6. Finish with sesame + scallions + optional nori.
7. Roll tight.

---

## Portioning (Per wrap)
- Chicken: **160–200 g cooked**
- Mayo: **25–30 g**
- Cucumber/veg: generous handful
- Sesame: small sprinkle

---

## Batch for 10 wraps
- Chicken: **1.8 kg**
- Teriyaki glaze: 2.5×
- Yuzu mayo: 300 ml mayo + citrus to taste
- Cucumbers: 3

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Delivery)
- Wrap in parchment + sticker.
- If pickup time is long: pack cucumber/veg in a cup (keeps crunch).
- Offer extra teriyaki cup as upsell.

**Label:** “Add crunch veg last (optional).”

---

## Storage
- Teriyaki chicken: 2 days refrigerated (reheat gently)
- Mayo: 3 days refrigerated
- Veg best fresh

---

## Allergens
- Soy (soy sauce)
- Egg (mayo)
- Wheat (tortilla)
- Gluten possible (soy sauce)
- Sesame if used
