# Miso-Butter Salmon Baguette (Yuzu Mayo + Pickles) — RiceMap24 Standard (Breakfast/Lunch Premium)

A premium lunch baguette: miso-butter glazed salmon + yuzu mayo + crisp pickles.  
Fusion (not traditional), designed for **high perceived value** and **pickup quality**.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Baguette sandwich (Asian twist, premium)
- **Cuisine vibe:** Japanese pantry (miso + yuzu)
- **Yield:** 4 baguettes
- **Active time:** ~20 min
- **Total time:** ~25–30 min
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (don’t overcook salmon)

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Premium signal:** salmon + glossy miso glaze
- **Photogenic:** orange salmon + green herbs + pickles
- **Delivery friendly:** pack sauce/pickles smartly to avoid soggy bread
- **Upsells:** extra salmon, extra yuzu mayo cup, add avocado

---

## Ingredients (4 baguettes)

## A) Salmon
- **700–800 g** salmon fillet, skin removed or on (your choice)
- Salt + black pepper
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil

## B) Miso-butter glaze
- **60 g** butter, softened
- **2 Tbsp** white miso
- **1 Tbsp** honey (or brown sugar)
- **1 Tbsp** soy sauce
- **1 tsp** rice vinegar (or lemon)
- **1 tsp** sesame oil (optional)
- Optional: 1 tsp grated ginger

## C) Yuzu mayo
- **120 ml** mayo
- **1–2 tsp** yuzu juice *(or 1 tsp yuzu marmalade)*  
  Substitute: lime juice
- Pinch salt

## D) Crunch + finish
- Lettuce leaves (recommended moisture barrier)
- **1 cucumber**, thin sticks
- Quick pickles (optional but great): pickled red onion or do chua
- Optional: nori strips + sesame
- Fresh herbs: dill or cilantro (pick one style)

## E) Bread
- **4** baguettes (15–18 cm) or 2 long baguettes halved

---

## Equipment
- Skillet (or oven)
- Mixing bowls
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Mix glaze + mayo (3 minutes)
- Mix miso-butter glaze ingredients to a smooth paste.
- Mix yuzu mayo.

---

## 2) Cook salmon (8–10 minutes)
### Skillet method (fast)
1. Pat salmon dry; season.
2. Heat skillet medium-high with oil.
3. Sear salmon 3–4 minutes, flip 2–3 minutes until almost done.

**Doneness target:** juicy (52–55°C) or cook a bit longer if customers prefer.

---

## 3) Glaze salmon (1 minute)
1. Reduce heat to low.
2. Add miso-butter to pan.
3. Spoon glaze over salmon 30–60 seconds until glossy.

---

## 4) Build baguettes
Per baguette:
1. Split baguette.
2. Add lettuce barrier.
3. Spread 1–2 Tbsp yuzu mayo.
4. Add salmon portion (about **170–200 g cooked**).
5. Add cucumber + pickles.
6. Finish with herbs + optional nori/sesame.

---

## Portioning (Per baguette)
- Salmon: 170–200 g cooked
- Mayo: 25–30 g
- Pickles: 30–50 g
- Cucumber: handful

---

## Batch for 10 baguettes
- Salmon: **1.8–2.0 kg**
- Glaze: 2.5×
- Mayo: 300 ml + citrus to taste

**Ops tip:** Cook salmon in batches and glaze quickly; build sandwiches fast at pickup.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
For best quality:
- Wrap in parchment.
- Offer pickles in a cup for long pickup times (premium option).
- Sauce cup upsell is easy.

Label: “Best fresh. Add pickles last (optional).”

---

## Storage
- Cooked salmon best same day.
- Glaze can be mixed ahead and kept chilled.

---

## Allergens

- Soy (miso/soy sauce)
- Dairy (butter)
- Egg (mayo)
- Wheat (baguette)
- Sesame if used
- Gluten possible (soy sauce)
