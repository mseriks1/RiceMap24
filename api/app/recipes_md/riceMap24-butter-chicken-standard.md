# Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A globally loved Indian curry: tender chicken in a rich tomato-butter-cream sauce with warm spices. Excellent as a premium curry bowl with rice or naan.

**Source recipe:** Swasthi’s Recipes — “Butter Chicken Recipe (Indian Chicken Makhani)”  
https://www.indianhealthyrecipes.com/butter-chicken/

## Ingredients (≈ 4 portions)

> **Important:** Butter chicken depends on **marinade**, **tomato-to-cream balance**, and **spice ratios**.  
> Use the linked Swasthi recipe for **exact measurements** (especially tomatoes, cashews, cream, spices, and cook times).

## A) Chicken marinade (per source)
- Chicken (boneless thigh/breast), amount per source
- Yogurt
- Lemon juice
- Ginger-garlic paste
- Red chili powder
- Garam masala
- Salt
- Optional: turmeric, kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) per source

## B) Sauce base (per source)
- Tomatoes (or tomato puree)
- Butter
- Cream
- Cashews (for body) *(common in the source method)*
- Aromatics: ginger, garlic
- Spices: garam masala + chili powder + optional whole spices per source
- Sugar (small amount, optional, per source)
- Salt

## C) To serve
- Steamed basmati rice (portion per bowl)
- Optional: naan
- Garnish: cream swirl + cilantro + kasuri methi (optional)

## Prep Plan (Recommended order)
1) Marinate chicken (30 min minimum)  
2) Cook chicken (pan sear or bake per source)  
3) Make tomato-cashew base + blend  
4) Finish sauce with butter + cream  
5) Combine chicken + sauce  
6) Portion with rice

---

## Method (Process Flow — follow source for exact times/amounts)

## 1) Marinate chicken
1. Mix marinade ingredients per source.
2. Coat chicken thoroughly.
3. Marinate at least **30 minutes** (longer is better).

## 2) Cook chicken
1. Cook marinated chicken per source method (pan sear, grill, or oven).
2. Aim for browned edges (adds “tandoori-style” depth).
3. Set cooked chicken aside.

## 3) Make the sauce (tomato + cashew base)
1. Cook tomatoes with aromatics and spices per source until broken down.
2. Add cashews (if using) and simmer briefly.
3. Blend until smooth (strain if you want extra silky).

## 4) Finish the makhani sauce
1. Return blended sauce to the pan.
2. Add butter and simmer gently.
3. Add cream and adjust seasoning per source.
4. Add kasuri methi (if using) near the end.

## 5) Combine
1. Add cooked chicken to the sauce.
2. Simmer gently until chicken is heated through and flavors meld.

## Quality Checks
- **Sauce:** Smooth, rich, orange-red; not gritty.
- **Chicken:** Tender, lightly charred edges.
- **Balance:** Creamy tomato with mild heat; not overly acidic.

---

## Scaling for Production (10 portions)
Base is ~4. Multiply by **2.5×**.
- Multiply all **exact source measurements** by 2.5× for marinade + sauce.

### Batch notes
- Make sauce in a big pot; cook chicken in batches.
- Portion control: weigh curry per box for consistent cost.
- Sauce thickens on holding; loosen with a splash of water/cream.

## Storage & Reheat
- **Refrigerate:** Up to **3 days**.
- **Reheat:** Gentle simmer or microwave; stir well (butter/cream can separate slightly).
