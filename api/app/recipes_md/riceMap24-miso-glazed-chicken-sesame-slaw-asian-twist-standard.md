# Miso-Glazed Chicken Thighs + Sesame Slaw (Asian Twist Meal) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A familiar Western-style chicken dinner upgraded with Japanese pantry flavors: **miso glaze** + a crunchy sesame slaw.  
Not traditional Asian—this is fusion designed for home-kitchen sales and pickup/delivery.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Glazed chicken meal + crunchy slaw (main + side)
- **Asian twist:** White miso + soy + honey + sesame
- **Yield:** 4 meal portions
- **Active time:** ~20 minutes
- **Total time:** ~35 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (glaze timing)

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Banker-friendly:** chicken thighs are forgiving and popular.
- **Great delivery:** glaze holds; slaw packed separately stays crisp.
- **Photogenic:** glossy chicken + crunchy slaw + sesame.
- **Upsells:** extra chicken, extra glaze cup, add rice, add pickles.

---

## Equipment
- Oven or skillet (oven is easiest for batches)
- Mixing bowls
- Sheet pan + parchment
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Ingredients (4 portions)

## A) Chicken
- **800 g** boneless chicken thighs (4 pieces)
- Salt + pepper
- Optional: 1 Tbsp neutral oil (if baking without skin)

## B) Miso glaze (the identity)
- **2 Tbsp** white miso
- **2 Tbsp** honey (or maple)
- **1 Tbsp** soy sauce
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar (or lemon)
- **1 tsp** sesame oil
- **1 tsp** grated ginger (optional)
- **1 clove** garlic, grated (optional)
- **2 Tbsp** water (to loosen)

## C) Sesame slaw (crisp side)
- **300 g** shredded cabbage (or coleslaw mix)
- **1 carrot**, shredded
- **2 scallions**, thinly sliced

### Slaw dressing
- **2 Tbsp** mayo (optional; for creamy version) OR use all vinegar version below
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 Tbsp** soy sauce
- **1 Tbsp** honey
- **1 tsp** sesame oil
- **1 Tbsp** toasted sesame seeds
- Optional: chili flakes

> For a lighter version: skip mayo and add +1 Tbsp vinegar + 1 Tbsp neutral oil.

## D) To serve (RiceMap24 options)
- Jasmine rice (optional add-on): 250 g cooked per order
- Lime wedge (optional)

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Mix glaze (2 min)
- Whisk miso, honey, soy, vinegar, sesame oil, water (+ ginger/garlic if using).

---

## 2) Bake chicken (20–25 min)
1. Preheat oven to **220°C**.
2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
3. Place on parchment-lined tray.
4. Bake **15 minutes**.
5. Brush or spoon glaze over chicken.
6. Bake **8–10 minutes** more until glazed and cooked through.

**Doneness target:** 74°C internal.

Alternative: pan-sear chicken, then add glaze at the end.

---

## 3) Make sesame slaw (5 min)
1. Mix cabbage, carrot, scallions.
2. Whisk slaw dressing.
3. Toss lightly (don’t overdress).

---

## 4) Assemble meal boxes
Per order:
- Chicken thigh with extra glaze drizzle
- Slaw packed separately
- Optional rice separate

---

## Quality Checks
- Chicken glossy, not burnt.
- Glaze balanced sweet-salty.
- Slaw crisp, not watery.

---

## Portioning (Per order)
- Chicken: 1 thigh (180–220 g cooked)
- Slaw: 120–150 g
- Optional rice: 250 g cooked
- Extra glaze cup: 20–30 ml (upsell)

---

## Scaling (10 orders)
Multiply by **2.5×**:
- Chicken: 2.0 kg
- Glaze: 2.5×
- Slaw: 2.5×
Bake in 2–3 trays.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Container A: chicken + glaze
- Cup B: slaw (separate)
- Container C: rice (optional)

Label: “Add slaw after reheating. Extra glaze optional.”

---

## Storage & Reheat
- Chicken keeps 2–3 days refrigerated; reheat gently.
- Slaw best same day (or keep undressed and toss before pickup).

---

## Allergens
- Soy (miso/soy sauce)
- Sesame
- Egg (if mayo used)
