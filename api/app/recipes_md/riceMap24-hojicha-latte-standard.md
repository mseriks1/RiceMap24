# Hojicha Latte (Hot or Iced) — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Asian Twist)

A café-friendly “coffee alternative”: **hojicha** (roasted green tea) tastes nutty, toasty, and mild.  
Great for RiceMap24 because it’s comforting, pairs with desserts, and feels premium without being “too bitter.”

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Latte (hot or iced)
- **Asian twist:** Hojicha (Japanese roasted green tea)
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 3–4 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (whisk powder smoothly)

---

## Why it’s a strong menu item
- **Broad appeal:** “latte vibe” without coffee.
- **Great upsells:** vanilla, brown sugar, oat milk.
- **Iced version sells well** with pickup/delivery.

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## Option A: Hojicha powder (best for consistency)
### Hot (≈300 ml)
- **2 tsp** hojicha powder
- **60 ml** hot water (80–90°C)
- **220 ml** milk (whole or oat)
- **10–15 ml** simple syrup or honey syrup (optional)

### Iced (≈450 ml)
- **2 tsp** hojicha powder
- **60 ml** hot water (to dissolve powder)
- **200 ml** cold milk
- **180–220 g** ice
- Optional: 10–15 ml syrup

---

## Option B: Hojicha tea bags/loose leaf (if no powder)
### Hot
- **240 ml** strong hojicha tea (double strength)
- **60 ml** milk
- Optional syrup

### Iced
- **240 ml** strong hojicha tea (double strength, chilled)
- **120 ml** milk
- Ice

---

## Sweetness Levels (menu toggles)
- **Unsweetened:** 0 syrup
- **Classic:** 10–15 ml syrup
- **Sweet:** 20 ml syrup

---

## Step-by-Step (Powder Method)

### Hot
1. Sift hojicha powder into a cup.
2. Add hot water and whisk to a smooth paste, then smooth liquid.
3. Steam or heat milk (do not boil).
4. Pour milk into hojicha and stir.
5. Sweeten if desired.

### Iced
1. Make hojicha paste with hot water (same as hot version).
2. Add syrup if using.
3. Fill cup with ice.
4. Add cold milk, then pour hojicha mixture.
5. Stir.

---

## Batch Setup (Recommended)

## A) Hojicha “concentrate” (powder slurry) for 10 iced lattes
- **40 g** hojicha powder
- **600 ml** hot water (80–90°C)

Whisk smooth, chill.  
Portion: **60 ml** per drink.

## B) Milk (10 iced lattes)
- **2.0 L** cold milk (whole or oat)
Portion: **200 ml** per drink.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- **Iced:** cup with tight lid or bottle.  
- **Hot:** insulated cup with lid.

**Label suggestion:** “Shake or stir before drinking.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Hojicha slurry/concentrate: best within **48 hours** refrigerated.
- Brewed hojicha tea: **2–3 days** refrigerated.

---

## Allergens
- Dairy if using milk; oat milk version available.
