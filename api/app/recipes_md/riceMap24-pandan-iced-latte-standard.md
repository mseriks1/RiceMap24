# Pandan Iced Latte — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Asian Twist, Pickup-Friendly)

A modern café drink: iced latte with a Southeast Asian signature aroma—**pandan**.  
Great for RiceMap24 because it’s premium-feeling, photogenic (light green tint), and easy to standardize with pandan syrup.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Iced latte
- **Asian twist:** Pandan (SE Asia)
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2–3 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Espresso
- **60 ml** double espresso (or 90 ml strong coffee)

## B) Pandan syrup
- **20–30 ml** pandan syrup *(house or store-bought)*

## C) Milk + ice
- **200–240 ml** cold milk (whole or oat)
- **180–220 g** ice

## Optional premium finishes
- Coconut cream float (1 Tbsp)
- Toasted sesame sprinkle (tiny)
- Vanilla (1/4 tsp)

---

## Sweetness Levels (menu toggles)
- **Less sweet:** 15–20 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 25 ml syrup
- **Extra sweet:** 35 ml syrup

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Fill cup with ice.
2. Add pandan syrup.
3. Add cold milk.
4. Pour espresso over (nice layered look).
5. Stir before drinking.

**Taste check:** coffee should still be present; pandan should feel like aroma, not candy.

---

## Batch Setup (Recommended)

## A) Pandan syrup (simple house version)
- **500 ml** water
- **500 g** sugar
- **2 tsp** pandan extract (start with 1.5 tsp, adjust)
- Optional: 1 tsp vanilla + pinch salt

Method:
1. Warm water + sugar until dissolved.
2. Cool, then stir in pandan extract (and vanilla).
3. Chill.

Portion: **25 ml per drink**.

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic)
- Espresso: **600 ml** (or brew per order)
- Pandan syrup: **250 ml**
- Milk: **2.2 L**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**

**Ops tip:** Pre-portion syrup cups (25 ml) for speed.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Cup with tight lid is fine.
- Bottle works too (shake before drinking).
- If pickup is long, keep espresso separate (mini shot cup) and customer combines for best flavor.

**Label suggestion:** “Stir well. Best within 60 minutes.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Pandan syrup: **7 days** refrigerated

---

## Allergens
- Dairy if using milk; oat milk option.
