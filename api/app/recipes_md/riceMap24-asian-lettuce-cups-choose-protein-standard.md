# Lettuce Cups (Asian-Style) — Choose Protein + Sauce Cups — RiceMap24 Standard

A premium handheld/light-lunch Asian genre: **lettuce cups** (wrap-in-lettuce).  
Great for customers who want **fresh, low-carb**, and it sells well with **sauce cup upsells**.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Lettuce cups (handheld)
- **Cuisine vibe:** pan-Asian (Chinese/Korean-style sauces)
- **Yield:** 4 portions (8–12 cups depending size)
- **Active time:** ~20 minutes
- **Total time:** ~25 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Fresh + premium** (looks clean and colorful).
- **Delivery-friendly:** pack cups + filling + sauce separately.
- **Choose-protein logic:** easy menu customization.
- **Upsells:** extra protein, extra sauce cups, add rice side.

---

## Ingredients (4 portions)

## A) Lettuce
- **1–2 heads** butter lettuce or romaine hearts  
Aim for 8–12 sturdy “cup” leaves.

---

## B) Choose Protein (pick ONE)
### Option 1: Hoisin-Ginger Chicken (easy)
- **650 g** ground chicken (or finely chopped chicken)
- 2 Tbsp hoisin
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tsp ginger, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 Tbsp neutral oil

### Option 2: Korean beef (classic)
- **650 g** ground beef
- 3 Tbsp soy sauce
- 2 Tbsp brown sugar
- 1 Tbsp sesame oil
- 2 cloves garlic + 1 tsp ginger
- Optional: 1 tsp gochujang

### Option 3: Shrimp (premium)
- **600 g** shrimp, chopped
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp garlic
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- Optional: chili flakes

---

## C) Veg + crunch (choose 2–3)
- Carrot, julienne
- Cucumber, diced
- Scallions
- Cilantro
- Sesame seeds
- Optional: crushed peanuts
- Optional: crispy wonton strips

---

## D) Sauce cups (choose 1–2)
### 1) Classic hoisin-lime
- hoisin + lime + a little water

### 2) Gochujang mayo (spicy)
- mayo + gochujang + vinegar + honey

### 3) Peanut-lime (rich)
- peanut butter + soy + lime + water

---

## Equipment
- Skillet
- Knife + cutting board
- Sauce cups

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Cook chosen protein (10–12 minutes)
1. Heat oil, cook meat until browned.
2. Add garlic/ginger.
3. Add sauces/seasonings.
4. Reduce until glossy (not watery).

---

## 2) Prep lettuce + toppings (5 minutes)
- Wash and dry lettuce leaves very well.
- Prep veg and crunch toppings.

---

## 3) Pack as a kit (best for pickup)
- Container A: cooked filling (warm)
- Container B: lettuce cups (dry)
- Cup C: toppings
- Cup D/E: sauces

Customer builds at home.

---

## Portioning
- 8–12 lettuce cups per order
- Filling: 350–450 g per order
- Sauce: 1–2 cups (30–60 ml)

---

## Batch for 10 orders
- Protein: 2.0–2.3 kg
- Sauces: 2.5×
- Lettuce: 5–6 heads (depends size)

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Delivery)
- Keep lettuce **dry and separate** from hot filling.
- Include “build instructions” card:
  1) scoop filling
  2) add toppings
  3) drizzle sauce

---

## Allergens
Varies:
- Soy (soy/hoisin)
- Egg (mayo sauces)
- Peanuts (if peanut sauce)
- Sesame optional
- Shellfish (shrimp option)
