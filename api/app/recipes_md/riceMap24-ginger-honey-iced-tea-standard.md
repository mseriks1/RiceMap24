# Ginger Honey Iced Tea — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Batch-Friendly)

A simple, high-margin drink that pairs with Asian food: **ginger + honey** tea, served iced.  
Great for RiceMap24 as a “clean” option and easy to batch.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Iced tea
- **Profile:** ginger + honey + lemon
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2 minutes (if base is ready)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Ginger-honey base
- **40 ml** ginger-honey syrup (batch)

## B) Tea base
Choose one:
- **200 ml** chilled black tea  
OR
- **200 ml** chilled green tea

## C) Citrus + ice
- **10 ml** lemon juice (optional but recommended)
- **180–220 g** ice

---

## Sweetness Levels
- **Less sweet:** 25–30 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 40 ml
- **Extra sweet:** 55 ml

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Add ginger-honey syrup to cup.
2. Add tea and lemon juice.
3. Fill with ice and stir.

---

## Batch Setup (Recommended)

## A) Ginger-honey syrup (for 10–12 drinks)
- **250 g** fresh ginger, sliced thin
- **1 L** water
- **500 g** honey
- **1 Tbsp** lemon juice
- Pinch of salt

Method:
1. Simmer ginger in water **15 minutes**.
2. Strain (press ginger lightly).
3. While warm (not boiling), stir in honey until dissolved.
4. Add lemon juice + pinch salt.
5. Chill.

Portion: **40 ml per drink**.

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic)
- Syrup: **400 ml**
- Tea: **2.0 L**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**
- Lemon juice: **100 ml** (optional)

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Bottle without ice + separate ice cup for best quality.
- Or serve in cup with ice for quick pickup.

**Label suggestion:** “Shake or stir. Keep chilled.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Ginger-honey syrup: **7 days** refrigerated
- Brewed tea: **2–3 days** refrigerated

---

## Allergens
None typical (honey not for infants).
