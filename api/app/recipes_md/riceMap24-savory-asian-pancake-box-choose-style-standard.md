# Savory Asian Pancake Box (Choose Style) — RiceMap24 Standard (Pajeon / Okonomiyaki / Scallion Pancake)

A premium “share box” Asian genre: savory pancakes/fritters cut into wedges with dipping sauce.  
Great as a **side order**, **snack**, or **lunch share** item for pickup.

- **Yield:** 2 large pancakes (8–12 wedges) or 4 smaller pancakes

## Choose 1 Style (pick ONE)

## Style A — Korean Pajeon (Scallion Pancake)
### Ingredients
- **200 g** all-purpose flour
- **40 g** cornstarch (extra crisp)
- **1 tsp** salt
- **260–300 ml** ice-cold water
- **1 egg** (optional but improves structure)
- **1 bunch** scallions (cut to pan length)
- Optional premium: **200 g shrimp** or squid

### Method (short)
Mix batter, arrange scallions, pour batter, pan-fry until crisp both sides.

---

## Style B — Japanese Okonomiyaki (Cabbage Pancake)
### Ingredients
- **250 g** cabbage, shredded
- **120 g** flour
- **2 eggs**
- **120 ml** water or dashi
- **1 tsp** baking powder (optional)
- Optional premium: shrimp, corn, mushrooms

### Finishes (premium identity)
- Okonomiyaki sauce + kewpie mayo + bonito flakes (if you use) + aonori (optional).

---

## Style C — Chinese Scallion Pancake (Cong You Bing)
### Ingredients (dough)
- **300 g** flour
- **180 ml** hot water
- **1 tsp** salt
- Sesame oil + chopped scallions for layering

### Method (short)
Knead, rest, roll with scallions/oil, coil, re-roll, pan-fry until flaky.

---

## Dipping Sauce (1–2 cups)
### Classic soy-vinegar dip
- 60 ml soy sauce
- 30 ml rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- Optional: chili flakes + scallion

---

## Step-by-Step Method (Pajeon “Banker” Version)

## 1) Make batter (2 minutes)
1. Mix flour, cornstarch, salt.
2. Whisk in ice-cold water (and egg if using).

## 2) Cook pancake (10 minutes)
1. Heat pan with oil, medium-high.
2. Arrange scallions in pan.
3. Pour batter over to bind.
4. Cook 4–5 min until golden; flip; cook 3–4 min.

## 3) Cut + box (2 minutes)
- Cut into wedges and pack with sauce cup.

## Packaging (Pickup/Delivery)
- Vent briefly to protect crisp edges.
- Sauce cups separate.
- Add a small “re-crisp” note: 2–3 min in pan/air fryer.

## Allergens
Varies by style:

- Egg (if used)
- Soy (sauce)
- Shellfish (if shrimp added)
- Gluten possible (soy sauce)
