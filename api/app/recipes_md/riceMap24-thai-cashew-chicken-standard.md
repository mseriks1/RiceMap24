# Gai Pad Med Mamuang (Thai Cashew Chicken) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A Thai restaurant classic: tender chicken stir-fried with roasted cashews, bell peppers, onions, and a glossy sweet-savory sauce. Great as a mild-to-medium “crowd pleaser” rice box.

**Source recipe:** Hot Thai Kitchen — “Cashew Chicken (Gai Pad Med Mamuang)”  
https://hot-thai-kitchen.com/cashew-chicken/

---

## Summary
- **Category:** Thai • Stir-Fry • Nuts
- **Yield:** 2–3 portions (depending on portion size)
- **Active time:** ~15 min  
- **Total time:** ~20–25 min
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (high-heat timing)

---

## Equipment
- Wok or large skillet
- Small bowl (sauce)
- Cutting board + knife
- Tongs/spatula
- Measuring spoons

---

## Ingredients (2–3 portions)

> **Important:** The sauce balance and prep order matter for this stir-fry.  
> Follow the linked Hot Thai Kitchen recipe for **exact measurements** and the recommended order.

### Main
- Chicken (thigh or breast), cut into bite-size pieces (amount per source)
- Roasted cashews (amount per source)
- Onion, sliced
- Bell peppers, cut into chunks
- Garlic, minced
- Dried chilies (optional, for heat) per source
- Oil (for stir-frying)

### Sauce (per source)
- Oyster sauce
- Soy sauce
- Fish sauce
- Sugar
- Water/stock (as needed)
- Optional: chili paste or other seasonings as specified

### Optional add-ons
- Scallions
- Jasmine rice to serve

---

## Prep (Do this before heating the wok)
1. **Sauce:** Mix sauce ingredients in a small bowl (per source).
2. **Chicken:** Cut evenly; pat dry.
3. **Vegetables:** Prep onion + peppers.
4. **Cashews:** Have roasted cashews ready (toast briefly if needed).
5. **Aromatics:** Mince garlic; prepare dried chilies (optional).

---

## Method (Precise, Easy-to-Follow)

## 1) Start the wok
1. Heat wok/skillet over **high heat** until hot.
2. Add oil.

## 2) Chicken
3. Add chicken and stir-fry until mostly cooked through and lightly browned.

## 3) Aromatics + vegetables
4. Add garlic (and dried chilies if using); stir briefly until fragrant.
5. Add onions and bell peppers; stir-fry until crisp-tender.

## 4) Sauce + cashews
6. Pour in the prepared sauce. Toss until sauce thickens and coats everything.
7. Add cashews and toss briefly (keep them crunchy).

## 5) Serve
8. Serve immediately with jasmine rice.

---

## Quality Checks (Before Serving)
- **Sauce:** Glossy, coating chicken and veg; not watery.
- **Veg:** Crisp-tender (not limp).
- **Cashews:** Still crunchy (add at the end).

---

## Scaling for Production (10 portions)
Because the base yield is 2–3 portions, scale using the source recipe amounts:
- If your base batch makes 2 portions → multiply by **5×**  
- If it makes 3 portions → multiply by **3.33×**

### Batch notes
- Cook in **batches** to keep heat high (avoid steaming).
- Keep cashews separate until the final toss.

---

## Packaging (To-Go) — RiceMap24 Recommended
- Rice in base; stir-fry on top.
- Keep extra cashews in a small cup for maximum crunch (premium touch).

**Holding time:** Best within **1–2 hours**.

---

## Storage & Reheat
- **Refrigerate:** Up to **2 days**.
- **Reheat:** Quick hot-pan toss is best. Microwave is OK, but cashews soften.
