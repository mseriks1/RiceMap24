# Vegan “Umami Crunch” Baguette (Teriyaki Mushrooms + Miso Mayo + Pickles) — RiceMap24 Standard

A truly **vegan** baguette with a strong Asian pantry twist: glossy teriyaki mushrooms, tangy pickles, cucumber crunch, and a creamy **miso mayo** (egg-free).  
Designed for lunch sales and pickup quality (moisture control built in).

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Vegan baguette sandwich
- **Asian twist:** teriyaki mushrooms + miso “mayo” + sesame + pickles
- **Yield:** 4 baguettes
- **Active time:** ~20 min
- **Total time:** ~25–30 min
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Vegan but “meaty”** (mushroom umami + glossy glaze).
- **Photogenic** (dark glossy mushrooms + bright pickles/greens).
- **Pickup-friendly** with components that don’t get soggy fast.
- **Upsells:** extra mushrooms, extra sauce cup, add chili crisp, add avocado.

---

## Ingredients (4 baguettes)

## A) Teriyaki mushrooms (the identity)
- **700–800 g** mushrooms (king oyster + shiitake mix is ideal), sliced
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil

### Quick teriyaki glaze (vegan)
- **90 ml** soy sauce
- **60 ml** water
- **2 Tbsp** brown sugar (or maple)
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 tsp** grated ginger
- **1 clove** garlic, grated
- **1 tsp** cornstarch + **1 Tbsp** water (slurry)

Optional: 1 tsp toasted sesame oil (finish)

---

## B) Vegan miso “mayo” (egg-free)
- **160 g** vegan mayonnaise *(best for speed)*  
  OR make quick mayo with aquafaba if you prefer
- **1.5 Tbsp** white miso
- **1 tsp** lime juice (or rice vinegar)
- Pinch salt (often not needed; miso is salty)

---

## C) Crunch + freshness
- Lettuce leaves (recommended moisture barrier)
- **1 cucumber**, thin sticks
- Pickled red onion or do chua (about **200 g**)
- Fresh cilantro (optional)
- Optional: nori strips (premium)
- Optional: sesame seeds

---

## D) Bread
- **4** baguettes (15–18 cm)

---

## Equipment
- Skillet/wok
- Small bowl for slurry
- Mixing bowl (miso mayo)
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Mix vegan miso mayo (1 minute)
- Stir vegan mayo + miso + lime until smooth.

---

## 2) Cook mushrooms (8–10 minutes)
1. Heat skillet medium-high with oil.
2. Add mushrooms and cook until they release water and start to brown.
3. Keep cooking until most moisture is gone (important for “meaty” texture).

---

## 3) Add teriyaki glaze (2 minutes)
1. Mix soy, water, sugar, vinegar, ginger, garlic.
2. Pour into pan and simmer 1 minute.
3. Add slurry and stir until glossy and coating mushrooms.
4. Optional: finish with a tiny sesame oil drizzle.

**Quality target:** sticky glaze, not watery.

---

## 4) Build baguettes
Per baguette:
1. Split baguette.
2. Add lettuce barrier.
3. Spread 1–2 Tbsp miso mayo.
4. Add teriyaki mushrooms (generous).
5. Add cucumber + pickles.
6. Finish with cilantro + sesame + optional nori.

---

## Portioning (Per baguette)
- Mushrooms: **170–220 g cooked**
- Miso mayo: 25–30 g
- Pickles: 40–60 g

---

## Batch for 10 baguettes
- Mushrooms: **2.0 kg**
- Teriyaki glaze: 2.5×
- Vegan miso mayo: 400 g vegan mayo + miso/lime

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Wrap in parchment.
- If pickup is long: pack pickles in a cup so bread stays extra crisp.
- Offer extra glaze cup as upsell.

Label: “Add pickles last (optional).”

---

## Storage
- Cooked mushrooms: 2 days refrigerated (reheat gently in pan).
- Miso mayo: 3–4 days refrigerated.
- Pickles: 5–7 days refrigerated.

---

## Allergens
- Soy (soy sauce, miso)
- Wheat (baguette)
- Gluten possible (soy sauce brand)
- Sesame if used
