# Peach Oolong Iced Tea — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Batch-Friendly)

A clean, premium iced tea: fragrant **oolong** with a bright **peach** note.  
Great for RiceMap24 because it’s easy to batch, pairs with food, and feels more “special” than standard iced tea.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Iced tea
- **Profile:** floral oolong + peach
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2–3 minutes (if tea is pre-brewed)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Tea base
- **220 ml** chilled oolong tea (strong brew)

## B) Peach
Choose one:
- **25–35 ml** peach syrup *(best for speed/consistency)*  
OR
- **60–80 ml** peach purée (then strain for smoothness)

## C) Sweetness (if needed)
- If using peach syrup: usually enough
- If using purée: add **10–15 ml** simple syrup as needed

## D) Ice + finish
- **180–220 g** ice
- Optional garnish: peach slice, mint, lemon wheel

---

## Sweetness Levels (menu toggles)
- **Less sweet:** 20 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 30 ml syrup
- **Extra sweet:** 40 ml syrup

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Add peach syrup (or purée) to the cup.
2. Add chilled oolong tea and stir briefly.
3. Fill with ice and stir 5–8 seconds.
4. Garnish (optional).

**Taste check:** floral tea should still be present, not just “peach soda.”

---

## Batch Setup (Recommended for Home Kitchens)

## A) Brew oolong (for 10 drinks)
You want it strong so it survives ice dilution.
- **2.5 L** hot water
- **20–25 g** oolong tea (or follow tea brand)

Method:
1. Steep **4–5 minutes** (avoid bitterness).
2. Strain and cool fast.
3. Refrigerate.

Portion: **220 ml per drink**.

---

## B) Peach syrup (simple house version)
- **500 g** peach purée (blend peaches or use canned)
- **500 g** sugar
- **500 ml** water
- **1 Tbsp** lemon juice

Method:
1. Warm water + sugar until dissolved.
2. Add peach purée + lemon, stir, cool.
3. Strain if you want it super smooth.

Portion: **30 ml per drink**.

---

## Batch for 10 drinks (classic)
- Oolong tea: **2,200 ml**
- Peach syrup: **300 ml**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- **Bottle without ice + separate ice cup** = best quality
- Cup with ice is fine for quick pickup

**Label suggestion:** “Shake or stir. Add ice if included.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Brewed oolong: **2–3 days** refrigerated
- Peach syrup: **7 days** refrigerated

---

## Allergens
None typical.
