# Veggie Premium Baguette — Miso Eggplant + Pickles (Nasu Dengaku Sandwich) — RiceMap24 Standard

A premium vegetarian baguette: miso-glazed eggplant (nasu dengaku vibe) + sesame mayo + bright pickles + crunch.  
Fusion (not traditional), designed for lunch sales and pickup quality.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Vegetarian baguette sandwich
- **Asian twist:** miso glaze + sesame + pickles
- **Yield:** 4 baguettes
- **Active time:** ~20 min
- **Total time:** ~30–35 min
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (eggplant tenderness + glaze)

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Premium vegetarian option** that feels “restaurant.”
- **Photogenic** (glossy miso + pickles + greens).
- **Pickup friendly** with smart moisture control.
- **Upsells:** extra eggplant, extra sesame mayo cup, add tofu.

---

## Ingredients (4 baguettes)

## A) Eggplant (miso-glazed)
- **2 large eggplants** (or 4 Japanese eggplants), sliced into 1.5–2 cm planks
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil
- Pinch salt

### Miso glaze
- **3 Tbsp** white miso
- **2 Tbsp** sugar
- **2 Tbsp** mirin
- **1 Tbsp** sake *(or 1 Tbsp water + 1 tsp rice vinegar)*
- Optional: 1/2 tsp grated ginger

---

## B) Sesame mayo
- **120 ml** mayo
- **1 tsp** sesame oil
- **1 tsp** rice vinegar (or lemon)
- **1 Tbsp** toasted sesame seeds
- Pinch salt

---

## C) Pickles + crunch
- Pickled red onion (or do chua), about **200 g**
- **1 cucumber**, thin sticks
- Lettuce leaves (recommended moisture barrier)
- Optional: nori strips
- Optional: fresh herbs (cilantro or dill)

---

## D) Bread
- **4** baguettes (15–18 cm)

---

## Equipment
- Skillet or oven + broiler
- Small saucepan (glaze)
- Mixing bowl
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Make miso glaze (3 minutes)
1. Combine miso, sugar, mirin, sake in a small saucepan on low heat.
2. Stir until smooth and glossy. Remove from heat.

---

## 2) Cook eggplant (12–15 minutes)
### Skillet method (fast)
1. Heat skillet medium with oil.
2. Cook eggplant planks 4–5 min per side until tender.
3. Transfer to tray.

### Oven method (easy batch)
1. Roast at 200°C for 12–15 minutes until soft.

---

## 3) Glaze + caramelize (2–4 minutes)
1. Spoon glaze on eggplant.
2. Broil 2–4 minutes until bubbling and caramelized.
3. Watch closely (miso can burn fast).

---

## 4) Mix sesame mayo (1 minute)
- Stir mayo + sesame oil + vinegar + sesame + salt.

---

## 5) Build baguettes
Per baguette:
1. Split baguette.
2. Add lettuce barrier.
3. Spread 1–2 Tbsp sesame mayo.
4. Add 2–3 eggplant planks (hot or warm).
5. Add pickles + cucumber.
6. Finish nori/herbs.

---

## Portioning (Per baguette)
- Eggplant: 2–3 planks (about 160–220 g cooked depending size)
- Mayo: 25–30 g
- Pickles: 40–60 g

---

## Batch for 10 baguettes
- Eggplant: 5 large
- Glaze: 2.5×
- Sesame mayo: 300 ml mayo + seasonings

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Wrap in parchment.
- If pickup is long: pack pickles separate (cup) to keep bread crisp.
- Offer extra sesame mayo cup as upsell.

Label: “Add pickles last for best crunch.”

---

## Storage
- Glaze: 3–5 days refrigerated
- Cooked eggplant best same day (reheat in oven/air fryer)

---

## Allergens
- Soy (miso)
- Egg (mayo)
- Wheat (baguette)
- Sesame
- Gluten possible (miso/mirin brands)
