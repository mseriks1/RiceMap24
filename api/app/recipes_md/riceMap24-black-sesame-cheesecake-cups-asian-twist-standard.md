# Black Sesame Cheesecake Cups (Asian Twist, Pickup-Friendly) — RiceMap24 Dessert Standard

A familiar dessert (cheesecake) with a bold Asian signature: **black sesame**.  
Served as single-serve cups with a cookie crust—perfect for pickup/delivery.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** No-bake cheesecake cups
- **Asian twist:** Black sesame paste + toasted sesame aroma
- **Yield:** 8 cups (200–250 ml each)
- **Active time:** ~25 minutes
- **Chill time:** 4–6 hours (best overnight)
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Pickup-friendly:** cups with lids, no slicing.
- **Premium look:** gray-black cream with white/black contrast + sesame sprinkle.
- **High-margin upsells:** extra sesame, yuzu dollop, “mochi bite” add-on.
- **Batch-friendly:** assemble trays.

---

## Equipment
- Mixing bowls
- Hand mixer/whisk
- Spatula
- 8 dessert cups with lids
- Fine sieve (optional; for ultra-smooth paste)

---

## Ingredients (8 cups)

## A) Crust
- **180 g** digestive biscuits or graham crackers
- **80 g** unsalted butter, melted
- Optional: **1 Tbsp** toasted sesame seeds (adds aroma)

## B) Black sesame cheesecake
- **400 g** cream cheese (softened)
- **300 ml** heavy cream (cold)
- **80–100 g** sugar (adjust)
- **120 g** black sesame paste (or ground toasted black sesame + honey)
- **1 Tbsp** honey (optional, rounds flavor)
- **1 tsp** vanilla (optional)
- **Pinch of salt**
- **10 g** gelatin powder + **50 ml** water *(recommended for clean set)*  
  - OR use 6 gelatin sheets

### Gelatin note
You can skip gelatin if your cream is whipped firm, but gelatin makes it safer for delivery and warm rooms.

---

## C) Topping (simple, premium)
- Toasted black sesame seeds
- Optional: yuzu curd or citrus jam (small dollop)
- Optional: white chocolate shavings

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Make crust (5 minutes)
1. Crush biscuits to fine crumbs.
2. Mix with melted butter (and sesame seeds if using).
3. Press into the bottoms of 8 cups (about 2 Tbsp each).
4. Chill cups while you make filling.

---

## 2) Bloom gelatin (2 minutes)
1. Sprinkle gelatin over 50 ml water.
2. Let sit 5 minutes, then warm gently until fully dissolved (microwave 10–15 sec).

---

## 3) Make black sesame cheesecake (12 minutes)
1. Beat cream cheese until smooth.
2. Add sugar, black sesame paste, honey, salt, and vanilla; mix smooth.
3. In a separate bowl, whip cold heavy cream to **soft peaks**.
4. Fold whipped cream into sesame mixture.
5. Stream in dissolved gelatin while mixing gently (avoid lumps).

**Texture target:** thick, smooth, pipeable.

---

## 4) Fill cups (5 minutes)
1. Spoon or pipe filling onto crust.
2. Smooth tops.
3. Chill 4–6 hours (best overnight).

---

## 5) Finish (service time)
- Sprinkle toasted black sesame on top.
- Optional: a small yuzu/citrus dollop for contrast.

---

## Quality Checks
- Clean set, not runny.
- Strong sesame aroma but balanced sweetness.
- Visual contrast (black + white + garnish).

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Use clear cups with tight lids.
- Keep cold.
- Label: “Keep chilled. Best within 48 hours.”

---

## Storage
- Refrigerated: 2–3 days best texture.
- Not freezer-friendly (texture changes).

---

## Allergens
- Dairy (cream cheese, cream, butter)
- Wheat (cookies)
- Sesame
- Gelatin (if used)
