# Miso Caramel Iced Latte — RiceMap24 Drink Standard (Asian Twist, Café-Style)

A sweet-salty “signature” coffee: **miso caramel** + iced latte.  
Not traditional Asian—this is modern fusion that feels premium and sells well as a specialty drink.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Iced latte
- **Asian twist:** White miso caramel
- **Yield:** 1 drink (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 2–3 minutes (if syrup is ready)
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (make syrup safely)

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

## A) Espresso / coffee
- **60 ml** double espresso (or 90 ml strong coffee)

## B) Miso caramel syrup
- **20–30 ml** miso caramel syrup (batch)

## C) Milk + ice
- **200–240 ml** cold milk (whole or oat)
- **180–220 g** ice

## Optional finish
- Pinch flaky salt (tiny)
- Caramel drizzle inside cup
- Cinnamon dust (optional)

---

## Sweetness Levels
- **Less sweet:** 15–20 ml syrup
- **Classic:** 25 ml syrup
- **Extra sweet:** 35 ml syrup

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Drink)
1. Fill cup with ice.
2. Add miso caramel syrup.
3. Add milk.
4. Pour espresso over for a layered look.
5. Stir before drinking.

---

## Batch Setup

## A) Miso caramel syrup (house version)
- **300 g** sugar
- **120 ml** water
- **250 ml** heavy cream (warm)
- **60 g** butter
- **2 Tbsp** white miso (start 1.5 Tbsp if strong)
- Pinch salt (optional)

Method:
1. Cook sugar + water to amber (cola color).
2. Turn off heat, whisk in warm cream carefully.
3. Add butter and whisk smooth.
4. Whisk in miso until fully dissolved.
5. Cool and bottle.

Portion: **25 ml per drink**.

---

## Batch for 10 iced lattes (classic)
- Espresso: **600 ml**
- Miso caramel syrup: **250 ml**
- Milk: **2.2 L**
- Ice: **1.8–2.2 kg**

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Cup with tight lid works well.
- Bottle is great too (shake before drinking).
- If pickup is long: espresso shot can be kept separate and combined at pickup.

**Label suggestion:** “Stir well. Keep cold.”

---

## Shelf Life
- Syrup: **7 days** refrigerated
- Drink best same day

---

## Allergens
- Dairy (cream, butter) unless you use dairy-free alternatives
- Soy (miso)
