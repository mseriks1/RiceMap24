# Korean Pajeon (Crispy Scallion Pancake) — RiceMap24 Side Order Standard

A top-tier side order that sells everywhere: crispy outside, chewy inside, packed with scallions.  
Serve with a tangy soy-vinegar dipping sauce. Great as a **snack add-on** and it photographs well.

**Reference recipe:** My Korean Kitchen — Pajeon (Korean Scallion Pancake)  
https://mykoreankitchen.com/korean-scallion-pancake/

---

## Summary
- **Category:** Korean • Side order / snack
- **Yield:** 1 large pancake (2–3 side portions) or 2 medium pancakes
- **Active time:** ~10–15 min
- **Total time:** ~20 min
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (crisping technique)

---

## Best Ways to Sell It (RiceMap24)
- **1 pancake (cut into 8 pieces)** + dip cup
- **2 mini pancakes** (better for delivery crisp)
- Optional upgrade: **seafood pajeon** (+ shrimp pieces)

---

## Equipment
- Nonstick or cast-iron skillet
- Mixing bowl + whisk
- Spatula
- Knife + scissors (to cut scallions)
- Small bowl (dipping sauce)

---

## Ingredients

## A) Pancake batter (simple)
Use the My Korean Kitchen method as reference. A practical base:
- **120 g** all-purpose flour
- **30 g** cornstarch (helps crisp)
- **1 tsp** salt
- **200 ml** cold water (or sparkling water for extra lightness)
- **1 egg** (optional but nice)
- **1 bunch** scallions (cut to pan length)

### Optional add-ins (premium versions)
- 150 g shrimp (chopped) or squid
- 1 small red chili, thin sliced (photo + heat)

---

## B) Dipping sauce (classic)
- **2 Tbsp** soy sauce
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 tsp** sugar (optional)
- **1 tsp** sesame oil
- **1 tsp** sesame seeds
- Optional: chopped scallion + chili flakes

---

## Method (Repeatable)

### 1) Mix dipping sauce
Mix and set aside.

### 2) Mix batter
1. Whisk flour, cornstarch, salt.
2. Add cold water and whisk smooth.
3. Add egg if using.

### 3) Cook pajeon (crisp method)
1. Heat skillet over **medium-high** and add a generous layer of oil.
2. Add scallions first, spread flat.
3. Pour batter evenly over scallions.
4. Cook 3–4 minutes until bottom is deep golden and crisp.
5. Flip and cook 2–3 minutes more.

**Crisp secret:** don’t be shy with oil, and keep the heat medium-high.

### 4) Serve
Cut into wedges, serve with dipping sauce.

---

## Quality Checks
- **Bottom:** deep golden, crisp.
- **Center:** cooked, not raw batter.
- **Scallions:** tender with some bite.

---

## Portioning
- Side portion: 3–4 wedges + dip
- Snack: full pancake cut into 8 + dip
- Add-on cup of extra dip (easy upsell)

---

## Scaling (10 side orders)
Make **5 large pancakes** (cut each into 8) or **10 mini pancakes** for best delivery.

---

## Packaging (To-Go) — RiceMap24 Recommended
- Use a vented container (steam softens crisp).
- Pack dip in a separate sealed cup.
- Best: sell as **mini pancakes** (stay crisp longer).

---

## Storage & Reheat
- Best fresh.
- Reheat in hot dry pan or air fryer 190°C for 2–4 minutes.

---

## Allergens
- Wheat (flour)
- Egg (if used)
- Soy (dipping sauce)
- Shellfish (if seafood version)
