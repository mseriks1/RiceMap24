# Chili Crisp Roasted Brussels Sprouts (Asian Twist Side Order) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A familiar Western side (roasted Brussels sprouts) upgraded with a modern Asian pantry staple: **chili crisp** + a touch of soy and vinegar.  
Not traditional Asian—just a bold fusion side that sells well and travels well.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Roasted vegetable side
- **Asian twist:** Chili crisp + soy + rice vinegar + sesame
- **Yield:** 6 side portions
- **Active time:** ~10 min
- **Total time:** ~30–35 min
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Easy add-on side:** pairs with bowls, meat mains, wraps.
- **Photogenic:** crispy char + red chili oil sheen.
- **Batch-friendly:** roast trays.
- **Delivery friendly:** holds texture better than fries; reheat works.

---

## Equipment
- Sheet pan
- Parchment
- Mixing bowl
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Ingredients (6 side portions)

## A) Brussels sprouts
- **1.2 kg** Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
- **1.5 Tbsp** neutral oil
- Salt + black pepper

## B) Chili crisp glaze
- **4 Tbsp** chili crisp (start 3 Tbsp for mild)
- **1 Tbsp** soy sauce
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar (or lemon)
- **1 Tbsp** honey (optional, balances heat)
- **1 tsp** sesame oil
- Optional: 1 tsp grated garlic or ginger

## C) Finish
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Sliced scallions
- Optional: crushed peanuts (premium crunch)

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Roast sprouts (20–25 min)
1. Preheat oven to **220°C**.
2. Toss sprouts with oil, salt, pepper.
3. Spread in a single layer cut-side down.
4. Roast **20–25 minutes** until deeply browned and crisp on edges.

---

## 2) Toss with chili crisp (2 min)
1. Whisk chili crisp, soy, vinegar, honey (optional), sesame oil.
2. Toss hot sprouts in the glaze until glossy.

---

## 3) Optional “set” (3 minutes)
- Return to oven 3 minutes to caramelize the glaze slightly.

---

## 4) Finish
- Sprinkle sesame and scallions (and peanuts if using).

---

## Quality Checks
- Sprouts have crispy browned edges, not steamed.
- Glaze glossy and balanced (not too salty).
- Heat level appropriate for your audience.

---

## RiceMap24 Portioning
- **150–180 g** per side portion

**Upsells**
- Extra chili crisp cup
- Peanut crunch upgrade
- Mild vs spicy

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Vent 1 minute before sealing to reduce steam.
- Optional garnish pack separate for best look.

Label: “Best warm. Reheat 3–5 min in oven/air fryer.”

---

## Storage & Reheat
- Refrigerate up to 3 days.
- Reheat in oven/air fryer to restore crispness.

---

## Allergens
- Soy (soy sauce; chili crisp brand may include soy)
- Sesame (sesame oil)
- Nuts (if using peanuts)
