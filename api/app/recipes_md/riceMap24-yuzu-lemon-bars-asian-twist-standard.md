# Yuzu Lemon Bars (Asian Twist, Pickup-Friendly) — RiceMap24 Dessert Standard

A classic lemon bar with a Japanese citrus upgrade: **yuzu**.  
This is not a traditional Asian dessert—just a familiar Western favorite with a premium Asian signature. Travels great for pickup/takeaway.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Citrus bars (shortbread base + custard top)
- **Asian twist:** Yuzu juice/zest (or yuzu marmalade)
- **Yield:** 16 bars (20×20 cm pan) or 12 larger bars
- **Active time:** ~25 min
- **Total time:** ~1 hr 10 min (plus chill)
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Pickup friendly:** bars pack cleanly; no melting.
- **Premium look:** powdered sugar finish + bright yellow interior.
- **High-margin upsells:** “extra yuzu,” “matcha dust,” larger bar.
- **Batch-friendly:** bake trays, sell slices/bars.

---

## Equipment
- 20×20 cm (8×8) pan (or 23×23 cm / 9×9)
- Parchment paper
- Mixing bowls
- Whisk
- Fine sieve
- Oven

---

## Ingredients

## A) Shortbread base
- **230 g** all-purpose flour
- **50 g** powdered sugar
- **1/4 tsp** salt
- **170 g** unsalted butter, cold cubes

## B) Yuzu custard layer
- **4 large eggs**
- **250 g** sugar
- **30 g** all-purpose flour
- **120 ml** lemon juice
- **60 ml** yuzu juice *(or use yuzu marmalade option below)*
- **1 Tbsp** yuzu zest (optional) OR lemon zest
- **1/4 tsp** salt

### Yuzu marmalade option (easier to source)
- Replace yuzu juice with **3 Tbsp yuzu marmalade** + add 30 ml extra lemon juice

## C) Finish
- Powdered sugar for dusting
- Optional: white chocolate drizzle
- Optional: tiny pinch flaky salt on top

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Bake shortbread base (18–22 min)
1. Preheat oven to **175°C**.
2. Line pan with parchment.
3. Mix flour, powdered sugar, salt.
4. Cut in cold butter until sandy crumbs form.
5. Press firmly into pan.
6. Bake until lightly golden, **18–22 min**.

---

## 2) Make yuzu custard (5 min)
1. Whisk eggs, sugar, flour, salt.
2. Whisk in lemon juice + yuzu juice (and zest if using).
3. Pour onto hot shortbread.

---

## 3) Bake custard (18–22 min)
- Bake until set but still has a slight jiggle in the center.

---

## 4) Cool + chill (minimum 2 hours)
1. Cool completely at room temp.
2. Chill 2–4 hours for clean cuts.

---

## 5) Slice + finish
1. Lift out using parchment.
2. Cut into bars.
3. Dust with powdered sugar.

---

## Quality Checks
- Custard set and sliceable, not runny.
- Shortbread crisp at the base.
- Flavor bright with a clear yuzu aroma.

---

## RiceMap24 Menu Formats
- Single bar
- 2-bar box
- 4-bar box (best seller)
- Party tray (16 pieces)

### Upsells
- Extra yuzu glaze drizzle
- Matcha dust (tiny amount) for “fusion”
- White chocolate drizzle

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Chill bars before boxing.
- Use parchment between layers.
- Label: “Keep chilled. Best within 3 days.”

---

## Storage
- Refrigerated: 3–4 days
- Freeze: 1 month (dust sugar after thaw)

---

## Allergens
- Wheat (flour)
- Dairy (butter)
- Egg
