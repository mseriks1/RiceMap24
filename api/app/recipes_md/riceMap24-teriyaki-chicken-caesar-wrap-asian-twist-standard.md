# Teriyaki Chicken Caesar Wrap (Asian Twist, Pickup-Friendly) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A familiar Western wrap (Caesar) with a Japanese-style twist: **teriyaki chicken** + sesame + optional nori crunch.  
Not a traditional Asian dish—this is fusion built for pickup sales and fast home-kitchen execution.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Wrap / lunch dinner
- **Asian twist:** Teriyaki chicken + sesame + optional nori
- **Yield:** 4 large wraps
- **Active time:** ~20 minutes
- **Total time:** ~25 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Pickup-friendly:** handheld wrap, easy to box.
- **Broad appeal:** Caesar is mainstream; teriyaki adds a signature twist.
- **Fast production:** one pan chicken + assemble.
- **Upsells:** add extra chicken, add fries/side salad, add extra sauce cup.

---

## Equipment
- Skillet
- Mixing bowl
- Knife + cutting board
- Tongs

---

## Ingredients (4 wraps)

## A) Teriyaki chicken
- **600 g** chicken thigh (boneless), sliced
- Salt + pepper
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil

### Quick teriyaki sauce (or store-bought)
- **90 ml** soy sauce
- **60 ml** water
- **2 Tbsp** honey (or sugar)
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 tsp** grated ginger
- **1 clove** garlic, grated
- **1 tsp** cornstarch + 1 Tbsp water (slurry) for thickness

---

## B) Caesar base
- **4 large** tortillas/wraps
- **1 head** romaine lettuce, chopped
- **60 g** grated parmesan
- **120 ml** Caesar dressing (store-bought is fine)

## C) Asian “finish” add-ons (optional but recommended)
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Nori strips (tiny handful)
- Pickled red onion (nice contrast)

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Cook teriyaki chicken (10–12 minutes)
1. Heat skillet over medium-high with oil.
2. Add chicken, season lightly, and sear until browned.
3. Mix teriyaki sauce ingredients (except slurry) and pour into pan.
4. Simmer 1–2 minutes.
5. Add slurry and stir until glossy and thick.
6. Coat chicken and turn off heat.

---

## 2) Mix Caesar salad (3 minutes)
1. Toss romaine with Caesar dressing and parmesan.
2. Keep it lightly dressed (not soggy).

---

## 3) Assemble wraps (5 minutes)
Per wrap:
1. Add Caesar salad base.
2. Add teriyaki chicken on top.
3. Sprinkle sesame (and optional nori).
4. Roll tight.

---

## Quality Checks
- Wrap is tight and not leaking.
- Chicken glossy, not watery.
- Salad crisp, not overdressed.

---

## Portioning (Per wrap)
- Chicken: **150 g cooked**
- Lettuce: generous handful
- Dressing: 25–30 ml max

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Wrap paper or foil + sticker label.
- If holding longer than 20 minutes: pack salad and chicken separately to prevent sogginess.

Label: “Best eaten fresh.”

---

## Storage
- Best same day.
- Teriyaki chicken can be prepped and reheated; assemble fresh.

---

## Allergens
- Soy (soy sauce)
- Dairy (parmesan, dressing)
- Egg (Caesar dressing)
- Wheat (wrap)
