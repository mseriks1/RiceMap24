# Crispy Chicken “Bang Bang” Baguette (Sweet Chili + Sriracha Mayo) — RiceMap24 Standard (Breakfast/Lunch)

A premium street-food style baguette: crispy chicken strips + creamy sweet-chili sauce + pickled crunch.  
Fusion (not traditional), designed for high sales and strong pickup quality (sauce packed smartly).

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Baguette sandwich
- **Asian twist:** sweet chili + sriracha + sesame + quick pickles
- **Yield:** 4 baguettes
- **Active time:** ~20 minutes
- **Total time:** ~30–35 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (crispy chicken workflow)

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Crispy chicken sells** (high repeat).
- **Sauce is addictive** and high-margin.
- **Photogenic** (crispy + glossy sauce + green garnish).
- **Upsells:** extra sauce cup, extra chicken, spicy level, side fries.

---

## Ingredients (4 baguettes)

## A) Chicken
- **700 g** chicken thigh or breast, cut into strips
- Salt + pepper

### Quick crispy coating
- **120 g** all-purpose flour
- **60 g** cornstarch (extra crisp)
- **1 tsp** salt
- **1/2 tsp** black pepper
- Optional: 1 tsp garlic powder

### Wet dip
- **2 eggs** beaten  
  OR 250 ml buttermilk (if you prefer)

### Frying
- Oil for frying  
  (or air-fryer version: spray oil)

---

## B) “Bang Bang” sauce (the identity)
- **120 ml** mayo
- **3 Tbsp** sweet chili sauce
- **1–2 Tbsp** sriracha (adjust)
- **1 tsp** lime juice
- Pinch salt
- Optional: 1 tsp sesame oil (tiny)

---

## C) Crunch + veg
- **250 g** shredded cabbage
- **1 carrot**, julienne
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar
- **1 tsp** sugar
- Pinch salt
- **1 cucumber**, thin sticks (optional)
- Optional: pickled jalapeño (heat lovers)

## D) Bread + finish
- **4** baguettes (15–18 cm)
- Sesame seeds
- Sliced scallions
- Optional: nori strips

---

## Equipment
- Pot for frying (or air fryer)
- Mixing bowls
- Tongs
- Thermometer (optional but good)
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Quick slaw (5 minutes)
- Toss cabbage + carrot + vinegar + sugar + salt. Keep light and crisp.

---

## 2) Mix sauce (1 minute)
- Stir mayo + sweet chili + sriracha + lime + pinch salt.

---

## 3) Crisp chicken (10–12 minutes)
### Fry method (best crisp)
1. Heat oil to **175–180°C**.
2. Coat chicken: dip in egg, then in flour/cornstarch mix.
3. Fry in batches **3–5 minutes** until golden and cooked.
4. Drain well.

**Doneness:** 74°C internal.

### Air-fryer method (easier)
1. Coat strips, spray lightly with oil.
2. Air-fry **200°C** until crisp, flipping once.

---

## 4) Sauce application (pickup-friendly options)
### Option A (best crunch for pickup)
- Keep chicken dry in sandwich.
- Pack sauce cup separately for dipping or drizzling.

### Option B (best immediate taste)
- Lightly toss chicken in sauce (not fully soaked).

---

## 5) Build baguettes
Per baguette:
1. Split baguette.
2. Add a thin smear of sauce (optional).
3. Add crispy chicken (160–200 g cooked).
4. Add slaw + cucumber.
5. Finish with sesame + scallions.

---

## Portioning (Per baguette)
- Chicken: 160–200 g cooked
- Slaw: 60–90 g
- Sauce: 30–40 g (or separate cup)

---

## Batch for 10 baguettes
- Chicken: 1.8 kg
- Coating: 2.5×
- Sauce: 300 ml mayo + 7–8 Tbsp sweet chili + sriracha

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Wrap in parchment.
- If fried: vent 30–60 seconds before fully sealing.
- Sauce cup separate for best crunch.

Label: “Sauce on the side (recommended).”

---

## Allergens
- Egg (mayo, egg dip)
- Wheat (flour, baguette)
- Soy possible (sweet chili brands)
- Gluten possible (brands)
- Sesame if used
