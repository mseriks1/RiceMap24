# Miso-Maple Roasted Carrots (Asian Twist Side Order) — RiceMap24 Standard Recipe Card

A simple Western-style roasted veggie side with a Japanese pantry twist: **miso + maple** glaze.  
Not traditional Asian—this is an easy, photogenic side that adds “premium” feel to home-kitchen menus.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Roasted vegetable side
- **Asian twist:** White miso + sesame + optional yuzu/lime
- **Yield:** 6 side portions
- **Active time:** ~10 min
- **Total time:** ~30–35 min
- **Skill level:** Easy

---

## Why this works for RiceMap24
- **Easy upsell side:** pairs with bowls, curries, grilled meats.
- **Photogenic:** glossy orange carrots + sesame + scallion.
- **Batch-friendly:** roast trays.
- **Delivery friendly:** holds well; reheat works.

---

## Equipment
- Sheet pan
- Parchment
- Mixing bowl
- Knife + cutting board

---

## Ingredients (6 side portions)

## A) Carrots
- **1.2 kg** carrots, peeled and cut into sticks (or whole small carrots)
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil
- Salt + pepper

## B) Miso-maple glaze
- **2 Tbsp** white miso
- **2 Tbsp** maple syrup (or honey)
- **1 Tbsp** rice vinegar (or lemon juice)
- **1 tsp** sesame oil
- **2 Tbsp** warm water (to loosen)
- Optional: 1 tsp grated ginger

## C) Finish (premium look)
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Sliced scallions
- Optional: chili flakes
- Optional: yuzu/lime zest

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Roast carrots (20–25 min)
1. Preheat oven to **220°C**.
2. Toss carrots with oil, salt, pepper.
3. Spread on a parchment-lined tray in one layer.
4. Roast **20–25 min**, turning once, until tender and caramelized.

---

## 2) Make glaze (2 min)
- Whisk miso, maple, vinegar, sesame oil, water (and ginger).

---

## 3) Glaze and finish (3–5 min)
1. Toss hot carrots with glaze until glossy.
2. Return to oven **3–5 min** to set the glaze (optional but nice).
3. Finish with sesame seeds and scallions.

---

## Quality Checks
- Carrots tender with caramelized edges.
- Glaze glossy, not watery.
- Balance sweet-salty with miso depth.

---

## RiceMap24 Portioning
- **150–180 g** per side portion

**Upsells**
- Extra glaze cup
- Spicy version (chili flakes)
- Sesame upgrade

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Pack warm but let vent 1 minute before sealing.
- Optional: garnish pack (sesame/scallion) separate for freshest look.

Label: “Best warm. Reheat 3–5 min in oven/air fryer.”

---

## Storage & Reheat
- Refrigerate up to 3 days.
- Reheat in oven/air fryer to restore caramelization.

---

## Allergens
- Soy (miso)
- Sesame (sesame oil/seeds)
