A premium handheld Asian genre: **skewers** (sticks) with a signature sauce.  
Perfect as side orders, party boxes, or “protein add-ons” to rice/noodle bowls.

## Summary
- **Style:** Skewers (handheld / party food)

## Ingredients (12 skewers)

## A) Choose Protein (pick ONE)
### Option 1: Chicken thighs (satay classic)
- **800 g** chicken thighs, cut into skewer-size strips

### Option 2: Beef strips (premium)
- **800 g** beef (sirloin/strip), cut into strips

### Option 3: Shrimp (fast premium)
- **700 g** shrimp, peeled/deveined

---

## B) Marinade (satay-ish, versatile)
- **2 Tbsp** soy sauce
- **2 Tbsp** brown sugar (or palm sugar)
- **1 Tbsp** neutral oil
- **1 Tbsp** grated ginger
- **2 cloves** garlic, grated
- **1 tsp** curry powder
- Optional: 1/2 tsp turmeric
- Optional: chili flakes

Marinate **20–30 minutes**.

---

## C) Sauce Cups (choose 1–2 to sell)
### 1) Peanut-Lime Satay Sauce (best seller)
- 120 g peanut butter
- 2 Tbsp hoisin (or soy+sugar)
- 1 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp lime juice
- warm water to loosen (thick)

### 2) Teriyaki glaze (yakitori vibe)
- soy + sugar/honey + ginger + garlic + slurry (sticky)

### 3) Chili crisp mayo (modern)
- mayo + chili crisp + lime

---

## D) Skewers + finish
- Bamboo skewers (soak 20 minutes)
- Sesame seeds (optional)
- Scallions (optional)
- Lime wedges (nice)

---

## Equipment
- Grill pan / skillet / oven broiler
- Tongs
- Mixing bowls
- Sauce cups

---

## Step-by-Step Method

## 1) Soak skewers (20 minutes)
- Prevents burning.

---

## 2) Marinate protein (20 minutes)
- Toss protein with marinade. Rest.

---

## 3) Skewer and cook (10–12 minutes)
### Grill pan / skillet
1. Heat pan medium-high.
2. Cook skewers, turning, until browned and cooked through.

### Broiler (easy batch)
1. Place skewers on tray.
2. Broil 8–12 minutes, turning once.

**Doneness targets:**
- Chicken: 74°C
- Beef: to preference (but safe handling)
- Shrimp: pink and firm (2–3 min per side)

---

## 4) Glaze (optional premium)
- Brush teriyaki glaze on skewers in final 1–2 minutes.
