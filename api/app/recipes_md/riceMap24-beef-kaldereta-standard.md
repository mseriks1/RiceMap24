## Quality Checks (Before Serving)
- **Beef:** Fork-tender (if chewy, simmer longer).
- **Sauce:** Thick, clingy (liver spread helps body).
- **Veg:** Tender but intact (not mushy).
- **Balance:** Savory tomato with mild spice; olives add salty pop.
