# Matcha White Chocolate Shake — RiceMap24 “Banker Drink” Standard

A premium, trendy milkshake: **matcha** (green tea) + **white chocolate** + vanilla.  
Perfect for RiceMap24 because it’s photogenic, has strong “specialty café” vibes, and sells well as an upsell drink.

---

## Summary
- **Style:** Milkshake
- **Asian twist:** Matcha (Japanese green tea)
- **Yield:** 1 shake (with batch scaling included)
- **Active time:** 4 minutes
- **Total time:** 4 minutes
- **Skill level:** Easy–Medium (avoid matcha clumps)

---

## Key to a smooth shake
Make a quick **matcha paste** first. This prevents clumps.

---

## Ingredients (1 shake)

## Classic size (≈450–500 ml)
- **250 g** vanilla ice cream
- **180–220 ml** cold milk (whole milk preferred)
- **2 tsp** matcha powder (culinary grade)  
- **25–35 g** white chocolate sauce *(or melted white chocolate)*  
- Pinch of salt (tiny)

### Optional “premium” add-ons
- 1 tsp vanilla (extra aroma)
- Whipped cream top
- White chocolate drizzle inside cup

---

## Sweetness Control
White chocolate is sweet. Start with **25 g** sauce.
- Too sweet → reduce sauce or add more matcha/milk.
- Not sweet enough → add +10 g sauce.

---

## Step-by-Step (Single Shake)

### 1) Make matcha paste (30 seconds)
1. Sift matcha into a small bowl.
2. Add **1–2 Tbsp** warm water (not hot) and whisk to a smooth paste.

### 2) Blend (30 seconds)
1. Add milk to blender first.
2. Add vanilla ice cream + matcha paste + white chocolate sauce + pinch salt.
3. Blend **20–30 seconds** until smooth.

### 3) Adjust thickness
- Too thick → add 30 ml milk
- Too thin → add 1 scoop ice cream

---

## Batch for 10 shakes
### Core ingredients
- Vanilla ice cream: **2.5 kg**
- Milk: **2.0 L**
- Matcha powder: **20 tsp** (~40 g)
- White chocolate sauce: **300 g** (range 250–350 g)

### Ops tip: Pre-make matcha syrup/paste
Mix:
- **40 g matcha**
- **200 ml warm water**
Whisk smooth, chill.  
Use **20 ml** matcha syrup per shake.

---

## Packaging (Pickup/Takeaway)
- Use thick shake cup + tight lid.
- Use wide straw.
- Keep cold.

**Label suggestion:** “Keep cold. Best within 30–60 minutes.”

---

## Allergens
- Dairy (ice cream, milk)
- Possible soy lecithin (white chocolate brands)
